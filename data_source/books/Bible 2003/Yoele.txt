Yoele 1
1 Dî dia Yehowa diakalua kudi Yoele, muana wa Petuele, ne,
2 Unvuai dî edi, nuenu bantu bakulumpe;
3 Ambilai bana benu muanda eu,
4 Tshintu tshiakashiyabo kudi mukumbi udi ukosa
5 Tabalai, nuenu bakuatshiki ba maluvu, dilai bienu;
6 Bualu bua tshisamba tshia bantu tshiakulua kubunda ba mu buloba buanyi,
7 Tshiakunyanga mionshi yanyi ya tumuma tua nvinyo,
8 Asai muadi bu mukashi utshidi kamama muluate bilamba bikayabala
9 Mulambu wa bia kudia ne mulambu wa bia kunua yakushika,
10 Madimi akubutudibua;
# bualu bua manvua
11 Ba bidime bakufua bundu,
12 Muonshi wa tumuma udi munyanguke,
13 Luatai bilulu bikayabala, asai muadi, nuenu bakuidi,
14 Jidilai tshikondo tshia kujila bia kudia,
15 Aka! Bua difuku adi!
16 Bia kudia kabiena biumushibue
17 Maminu adi abola muinshi mua mabuwa a buloba,
# bualu bua manvua
18 Monai mudi bimuna bitua mikemu!
19 Yehowa, ndi ndila kûdi;
20 E, nyama ya mu mpata mene idi idila kûdi,

Yoele 2
1 Elai mpungi mu Siona,
2 Ndituku dia mîdima ne dia mufitu,
3 Ku mpala kua mikumbi ei kahia kadi kosha bintu, kabimana,
4 Tshifuanyikishi tshiai tshidi bu tshifuanyikishi tshia tubalu;
5 Bu mudi tshiona tshia makalu a nvita ha mikuna
6 Bantu badi bazakala ku mpala kuai ne buowa bukole,
7 Idi inyema lukasa bu bena bukitu;
8 Kayena isakilangana,
9 Idi yenda idiela mu musoko,
10 Buloba budi buzakala ku mpala kuai;
11 Yehowa udi wela dî dikole
12 Yehowa udi wamba ne, Mpindiu mene,
13 Handai mitshima yenu, kanuhandi hivualu bienu,
14 Nganyi udi mumanye ne kena ukudimuna mutshima wandi,
15 Elai mpungi mu Siona,
16 sangishai bantu.
17 Bakuidi, bakuatshi ba mudimu wa Yehowa,
18 Hashishe, Yehowa wakakuatshika mukau bua buloba buandi,
19 Yehowa wakakudimuna bantu bandi dî,
20 Nennûmushile bena luseke lua nôde, nembatume kule;
21 Wewe buloba, kutshinyi nansha!
22 Nyama ya mu mpata, kanu tshinyi;
23 Nuenu bana ba balume ba mu Siona, sankai bienu,
24 Bishihuidi nebiule tente ne blé,
25 Nennuhingashile bintu ha mutu ha bienu biakadiabo kudi mikumbi,
26 Nenudie bintu bikumbane, nenukute;
27 Nenumanye ne meme ndi munkatshi mua Bena Isalele,
28 Ku nyima kua malu â
29 Nengitshikishile Nyuma wanyi kabidi
30 Nendeshe bantu malu a kukema mu ditu ne ha buloba: mashi, kahia ne mulanjilanji wa muishi.
31 Diba nedikudimuke midima ne ngondo neakudimuke mashi, diambedi dituku adi dinene dia Yehowa, didi dikuatshisha bantu buowa, kadiyi dianze kulua.
32 Halua bualu ebu, muntu yonso watela dina dia Yehowa neasungidibue; bualu bua mu mukuna wa Siona ne mu Yelushalema nemuikale bakuabo bahanduke bu muakamba Yehowa; ne munkatshi mua bantu aba badi bashala nemuikale bakuabo badi Yehowa Ubikila.

Yoele 3
1 Monai, mu matuku au ne mu tshikondo atshi, hangumusha Bena Yuda ne Bena Yelushalema mu buhika bua kubaha diakalengele,
2 nensangishe ba mu bisamba bionso, nempueke nʼabo mu tshibanda tshia Yehoshafata; nembalumbuluishilema bua bantu banyi, buhianyi buanyi, Bena Isalele, bakamuangalashabo munkatshi mua bisamba bia bantu. Bakabanyangana buloba buanyi,
3 bakelela bantu banyi nshobo, bakahana muana wa balume ku mukashi wa masandi, bakahana muana wa bakashi ku maluvu a nyinyo, bakânua.
4 E, nuenu kabidi, Tulo ne Sidona, ne nseke yonso ya Peleshete, nudi bu banganyi kundi? Nudi basue kunfuta bua bualu bunakuenza, anyi kungenzela bunga bualu, anyi? Nennuhingashile difutu lukasa bua malu mabi enu!
5 Bualu bua nuakangata biamu bianyi bia argent ne bia or, ne nuakaya ne bintu bianyi bia mushinga mukole mu ntempelo yenu.
6 Nuakahana Bena Yuda ne Bena Yelushalema kudi Bena Gelika bua kubumusha mu buloba buabo ne bua kubatuma kule.
7 Monai, nembanuishe ku muaba unuakabahana; nennuhingashile difutu bua bualu bunuakenza.
8 Nempane bana benu ba balume ne bana benu ba bakashi kudi Bena Yuda, ne bobo nebabahane kudi Bena Seba, bantu aba ba kule. Bualu bua Yehowa wakuamba dî edi.
9 Elai dî edi munkatshi mua bisamba bia bantu ne,
10 Tulai miele ya nvita ku nkasu yenu,
11 Ambulusbai ne luai bienu,
12 Ba mu bisamba bia bantu babike,
13 Tualai muele wa kunowa nʼau,
14 Misumba ne misumba ya bantu
15 Diba ne ngondo biakuandamuka mîdima,
16 Yehowa udi wela dî diandi dikole difume ku Siona,
17 Nunku nuenu nenumanye ne, Meme, Yehowa Nzambi wenu,
18 Hashishe, mu dituku adi
19 Ejipitu neabutudibue,
20 Kadi Bena Yuda nebajalame tshiendelele,
21 Nensombuele mashi abo andi tshiyi muanji kusombuela;
