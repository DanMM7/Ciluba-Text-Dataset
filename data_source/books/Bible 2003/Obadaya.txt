Obadaya 1
1 Tshikena kumona tshia Obadaya.
2 Mona, nakukuvuisha bu tshisamba tshikese munkatshi mua binga bisamba,
3 Dihetu dia mu mutshima webe diakukudinga,
4 Yehowa udi wamba ne,
5 Bialua bibi, nansha banyengi, kûdi bufuku,
6 Monai mudibo bahaule Bena Esau,
7 Bonso bakadi bahunga nʼebe tshihungidi
8 Yehowa udi wamba ne,
9 Temena, bena bukitu bebe nebakuatshike buowa,
10 Bua tshimbambila tshiwakenzela muanʼenu Yakoba,
11 Mu dituku adi diwakimana mutangilangane nʼabo,
12 Kadi kuvuishi dituku dia makenga a muanʼenu
13 Kubuedi mu tshibuelelu tshia bantu banyi
14 Kuimanyi mu masanga a nshila
15 Bualu bua dituku dia Yehowa didi ha buihi dilongoluela
16 Bu munuakunua makenga ha mukuna wanyi wa tshijila,
17 Kadi mu mukuna wa Siona nemuikale tshisumbu tshia bantu bahanduke,
18 Bena Yakoba nebikale kahia,
19 Badi ku luseke lua Sude nebadiangatshile mukuna wa Esau,
20 Bahika bakadi mu busalayi ebu bua Bena Isalele
21 Bahikudi nebabande ha mukuna wa Siona
