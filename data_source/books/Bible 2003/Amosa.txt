Amosa 1
1 Au mmei a Amosa, wakadi munkatshi mua balami ba mikoko ba ku Tekoa, akunvueye bualu bua Bena Isalele, mu matuku a Uziya, mukelenge wa Yuda, ne mu matuku a Yalobama, muana wa Yoasha, mukelenge wa Isalele, bidimu bibidi ku mpala kua kutaka kua buloba.
2 Yeye wakamba ne,
3 Yehowa udi wamba ne,
4 Nunku nentumine ba mu nsubu wa Hazaele kahia;
5 Nentshibule lukanu lua ku tshibi tshia ku Damaseke,
6 Yehowa udi wamba dî edi ne,
7 Kadi nentemeshe kahia ha lumbu lua Gaza,
8 Nengumushe bena musoko ba mu Ashedodo,
9 Yehowa udi wamba dî edi ne,
10 Kadi nentemeshe kahia ha lumbu lua Tulo,
11 Yehowa udi wamba dî edi ne,
12 Kadi nentemeshe kahia mu Temana,
13 Yehowa udi wamba dî edi ne,
14 Nunku nentemeshe kahia ha lumbu lua Laba,
15 Mukelenge wabo neabuele mu buhika,

Amosa 2
1 Yehowa udi wamba dî edi ne,
2 Nunku nentume kahia bua kuosha Moaba,
3 Nenshihe nfumu bua kikadi munkatshi muabo,
4 Yehowa udi wamba dî edi ne,
5 Nunku nentumine Bena Yuda kahia,
6 Yehowa udi wamba ne,
7 Badi bajinga bua kumona luhuishi mene ha mitu ya bahele,
8 Badi badiolola hanshi
9 Kadi meme hanyi nakashiha Muena Amole ku mpala kuabo,
10 Meme nakanuluhula mu buloba bua Ejipitu,
11 Nakabisha bakuabo ba munkatshi mua bana benu ba balume bua kuikalabo baprofete,
12 Kadi nuakenzesha Banazi bua kunuabo maluvu a nvinyo,
13 Monai, nennushindike hanshi mu muaba wenu,
14 Bukole bua kunyema nebujimine kudi muena lubilu,
15 Muedi wa buta kena ushindama,
16 Udi ne mutshima mukole munkatshi mua bena bukitu

Amosa 3
1 Nuenu Bena Isalele, unvuai dî edi didi Yehowa unuhisha nʼadi, dia kunuisha nʼadi bonso ba mu tshisamba tshinakahatula mu buloba bua Ejipitu, ne,
2 Nuenu nkayenu mbanakumanya
3 Bantu babidi badi bendela hamue,
4 Nyama wa ntambue udi ukungula muitu,
5 Nyunyu udi ukuatshibua mu buteyi hanshi,
6 Bielabo mpungi wa nvita mu musoko,
7 Bulelela, Yehowa Nzambi kena wenza bualu,
8 Nyama wa ntambue udi mukungule;
9 Elai dî ku nsubu ya bakelenge ba mu Ashedodo,
10 Yehowa udi wamba ne, Bantu aba
11 Nunku Yehowa Nzambi udi wamba ne,
12 Yehowa udi wamba ne, Bu mudi mulami wa mikoko udihula maboko abidi anyi tshihesa tshia ditshu mukana mua nyama wa ntambue, nunku nebahandishile Bena Isalele badi bashikame mu Samalea ku ditumba dia bulalu, ne ha biata bia mu Damaseke.
13 Mukelenge Yehowa, Nzambi wa misumba, udi wamba ne,
14 mu dituku dinatumina Bena Isalele makenga bua malu mabi abo,
15 Nenshihe nsubu udibo balala mu tshikondo tshia mashika,

Amosa 5
1 Unvuai dî edi dia muadi dindi
2 Muana mukashi utshidi kamama,
3 Bualu bua Mukelenge Yehowa udi wamba ne,
4 Bua Yehowa udi wambila Bena Isalele dî edi ne,
5 kadi kanukebi Betele,
6 Kebai Yehowa, nenuikale ne muoyo;
7 nuenu mbadi bakudimuna kulumbulula kuakane bua kuluaku anu bululu,
8 Yeye wakafuka mitoto ya Tshidiminanzashi ne ya Nyama ne Mbua ne Muntu,
9 Yeye udi utuma dibutuka kudi bena bukitu,
10 Badi ne muntu udi ubabela ku tshibuelelu tshia musoko lukuna,
11 Nudi nudiata muntu muhele muinshi mua makasa enu,
12 Bualu bua ndi mumanye ne malu mabi enu adi mavule,
13 Nunku muntu udi ne budimu neahuwe mu tshikondo atshi,
14 Kebai malu makane, kanukebi malu mabi,
15 Kinai malu mabi, nangai malu mimpe,
16 Nunku Yehowa, Nzambi wa misumba,
17 Yehowa udi wamba ne, Mu madimi onso a tumuma tua nvinyo nemuikale muadi;
18 Mulau unukuate nuenu badi ba sue dituku dia Yehowa dimuenekele!
19 Didi bu muntu munyeme nyama wa ntambue
20 Kanuena bamanye ne dituku dia Yehowa nedikale mîdima, kadiyi ne munya, anyi?
21 Ndi mbenga bidia bienu bia disanka, bidi bintonda;
22 Bulelela, nansha binuafila milambu yenu kundi mudi nungoshila,
23 Umukai kundi ne mutoyi wa misambu yenu,
24 Kadi kulumbulula kuakane kuhueke hanshi bu mai,
25 Nuenu Bena Isalele, nuakulua budi ne milambu ya nyama minshihela ne ya bia kudia bidimu makumi anai mu tshihela, anyi?
26 Kadi nuakuambula ntenta wa mukelenge wenu, ne tshitekelu tshia mpingu yenu, mutoto wa nzambi wenu binuakudifudila.
27 Nunku meme nennutume mu buhika mu buloba budi buhita Damaseke kule. Yehowa, Nzambi mene wa misumba, udi wamba dî edi.

Amosa 6
1 Mulau wikale kudi badi bashikame talalâ mu Siona bu badi kabayi ne bualu,
2 Hitai tô ne ku Kalene, nutangile;
3 Nuenu badi bakeba kusemesha dituku adi dibi kule,
4 Badi balala ha malalu a mibanga,
5 Badi bimba misambu ya tshimbimba ne lunzenze,
6 Badi banuina nvinyo mu malonga,
7 Bua mianda ai nebadianjile kubuela mu buhika,
8 Mukelenge Yehowa wakuditshiha, yeye muine;
9 Halua tshikondo atshi, bikala bantu dikumi mu nsubu umue, nebafue biabo.
10 Halua muanʼabo ne muntu mufue bua kumuambula, yeye mene ngudi ne bua kuosha tshitalu tshiandi, ne hambeye kuhatula mifuba yandi ha mbelu, neakose munga muntu udi munda mua nsubu ne, Muntu mukuabo udi nʼebe, anyi? Wakonkeye nunku neitabe ne, Nansha. Hashishe, mukuabo neamuambile ne, Huwa biebe talalâ, bualu bua katuena bamanye mua kutela dina dia Yehowa.
11 Nunku monai, Yehowa udi wela mukenji ne,
12 Tubalu tudi mua kunyemena lubilu ha mabue, anyi?
13 Nuenu nudi nusankila mu tshintu tshia tshianana,
14 Yehowa, Nzambi wa misumba, udi wamba ne,

Amosa 7
1 Mukelenge Yehowa wakandesha tshikena kumona etshi: Monai, yeye wakadi utuma mikumbi ku tshibangidilu tshia kutoloka kuibidi kua bia kudia; monai, kuakadi kutoloka kuakalonda bia kudia biakanowelabo mukelenge.
2 Hakashikishayi kudia mashinde onso a ha buloba, nakamba ne,
3 Yehowa wakakudimuna mutshima wandi bua muanda eu;
4 Mukelenge Yehowa wakandesha tshikena kumona etshi: Monai, Mukelenge Yehowa wakela dî bua kutuma kahia; ne kakakamisha mai manene, kakadi kamba kuhidisha buloba kabidi.
5 Hashishe, nakamba ne,
6 Yehowa wakakudimuna mutshima wandi bua muanda eu;
7 Yeye wakandesha tshikena kumona etshi: Monai, Yehowa wakadi muimane ha mutu ha lumbu luakaludikabo diambedi ne nshinga udi ne tshiamu tshia plomb, luidi elu lua plomb luikale mu tshianza tshiandi
8 Yehowa wakankonka ne, Amosa, udi umona tshinyi? Nakamba ne, Luidi lua plomb. Ka Yehowa wakamba ne,
9 Bitendelelu bia Isaka bia ha mikuna nebibutudibue;
10 Hashishe, Amasaya, muakuidi wa ku Betele, wakatumina Yalobama, mukelenge wa Isalele, dî ne, Amosa wakulelela tshifufu tshibi munkatshi mua tshisamba tshia Bena Isalele; buloba kabuena mua kulama mei andi onso.
11 Bualu bua Amosa udi wamba ne,
12 Amasaya wakambila Amosa kabidi ne, Wewe mumonyi wa malu, ya biebe, nyemene ku buloba bua Yuda, diaku bidia, ambaku mei ebe a buprofete.
13 Kadi kuedi dî diebe kabidi mu Betele tshiendelele; bualu bua eu mmuaba wa tshijila wa mukelenge, ne mbalasa wandi wa bukelenge udimu.
14 Mu diba adi Amosa wakakudimuna Amasaya di ne, Tshiena muprofete, ne tshiena muana wa muprofete; kadi ndi mulami wa bimuna ne musangishi wa tumuma tua mitshi yê sycomore.
15 Yehowa wakangangata hanakadi ndama bimuna, ne Yehowa wakangambila ne, Ya biebe, ambila bantu banyi Bena Isalele dî diebe dia buprofete.
16 Wewe unvua dî dia Yehowa;
17 Nunku Yehowa udi wamba ne,

3 Mukelenge Yehowa udi wamba ne,
4 Unvuai bualu ebu, nuenu badi badiata bakengi mu makasa,
5 nuambe ne, Ngondo neafue diba kai
# bua tumanye mua kuhana manvua?

6 bua tusumbe bahele ku tshiamu tshia argent
7 Yehowa wakuditshiha ku bulengele bua Yakoba ne,

Amosa 9

7 Yehowa udi ukonka ne, Nuenu Bena Isalele,
