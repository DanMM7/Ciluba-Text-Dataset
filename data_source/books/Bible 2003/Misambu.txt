Misambu 1
1 Disanka didi kudi muntu
2 Kadi disanka diandi didi mu mikenji ya Yehowa,
3 Yeye udi bu mutshi
4 Bantu babi kabena nunku nansha,
5 Nunku bantu babi kabena bajalame halua kulumbuluisha kuabo.
6 Bualu bua Yehowa udi mumanye nshila wa bantu bakane,

Misambu 2
1 Bualu kai ba mu bisamba bia bende badi badisangisha hohamue bua bafune bulobo,
2 Bakelenge ba ha buloba badi badijadika,
3 Tutshibule nkanu yabo bituha,
4 Yeye udi mushikame mu diulu udi useka, Mukelenge udi uhahetula.
5 Hashishe, yeye neakule nʼabo mu tshiji tshiandi,
6 Meme hanyi nakujadika Mukelenge wanyi ha mutu ha Siona, mukuna wanyi wa tshijila.
7 Nengambile bantu mukenji wandi;
8 Ulombe, nenkuhe bantu ba bisamba bia bende bu buhianyi buebe, ne ba kunfudilu kua buloba bu bantu bebe.
9 Wewe neubatshibule ne dikombo dia tshiamu; neubasunsulule bu mudibo basunsula luesu lua mufumbi wa ngesu.
10 Nunku, nuenu bakelenge, nuikale ne meji; nuyishibue, nuenu bakokeshi ba ha buloba.
11 Nukuatshile Yehowa mudimu ne dishina, nusanke nʼandi ne nuzakale diatshimue.
12 Nuluangane mishiku ne muana wandi wa balume, bua yeye kikadi ne tshiji,

Misambu 3
1 Yehowa, mona mudi bena lukuna banyi basangane nunku!
2 Ba bungi badiku badi bambila mutshima wanyi ne,
3 Kadi wewe, Yehowa, udi ngabu udi undama nseke yonso,
4 Ndi ndila kudi Yehowa ne dî dianyi dikole,
5 Nakalala hanshi, nakadila tulu;
6 Tshiena ntshina mbombo ya bantu
7 Juka bieba, Yehowa,
8 Luhandu ludi lufuma kudi Yehowa.

Misambu 4

3 Kadi mimanye ne, Yehowa wakudijidila muntu muakane bua bualu buandi;
4 Nuikale ne tshiji, kanuenji malu mabi;
5 Nufile milambu ya buakane,
6 Ba bungi badi bamba ne, Muntu kayi watulesha diakalengele?
7 Wewe Wakuteka disanka mu mutshima wanyi,
8 Nendale hanshi, nendale tulu mu ditalala kabidi,
