Nsumuinu 1
1 Nsumuinu ya Solomo, muana
2 Bua bantu bamanye lungenyi ne diyisha,
3 Bua bahete diyisha dia mua kuenza bienzedi bia meji,
4 Bua kuha muntu udi kayi mutantamane mu lungenyi budimu,
5 Muena meji unvue biandi, adiunde mu malu adibo bamuyisha;
6 bua kujingululeye lusumuinu ne tshifuanyikishi,
7 Ditshina dia Yehowa ntshibangidilu tshia lungenyi;
8 Muanʼanyi, unvue diyisha dia tatu webe,
9 Bualu bua biobi nebikale tshitotolo tshilengele tshia ku mutu webe,
10 Muanʼanyi, biakuteya bantu babi, kuitabushi nansha.
11 Biambabo ne, Lua kutudi, tualamine muntu bua kumatshisha mashi andi hanshi,
12 tubamine hatshidibo ne muoyo bu mudi Muaba wa Bafue wenza,
13 Netusangane biuma bionso bia mushinga mukole,
14 Biwaya nʼetu,
15 Muanʼanyi, kuendi nʼabo mu nshila umue nansha,
16 Bua makasa abo adi aya lubilu ku malu mabi,
17 Kuanda bukuondo ku mesu kua
18 ne bantu aba badi balamina mashi abo nkayabo,
19 Nshila ya bonso badi ne lukuka lua kunyenga bintu ku bukole idi nunku;
20 Meji adi ela dî dikole mu musesu,
21 Adi ela dî ku masangu a nshila ya mitoyi.
22 Nuenu batshimbakanyi, nenunange butshimbakane musangu bule munyi?
23 Andamukai bua kunvua kubela kuanyi.
24 Bualu bua nakunubikila, ne nuenu nuakuhidia kunvua. Nakuolola tshianza tshianyi, kakuakadi muntu wakuitaba.
25 Nuakulengulula lungenyi luonso lunakunulesha kabidi, kanuakadi basue kunyua kubela kuanyi.
26 Meme kabidi nenseke halua dikenga dienu;
27 hanukuata buowa bu mudi tshihuhu tshibika,
28 Hashishe nebambikile, kadi tshiena ngitaba;
29 Bualu bua bakakula lungenyi,
30 Kabakasua kuitabusha lungenyi lunakabalesha,
31 Bua malu â nebadie mamuma adi bienzedi biabo bikuama, nebukute ne malu adibo bahangidisha.
32 Bualu hua dihingana tshianyima dia batshimbakanyi nedibashihe,
33 Kadi muntu yonso udi ungunvuila neashikame talalâ,

Nsumuinu 2

15 Badi bakonyangasha nshila yabo,
16 Bua bualu ebu budi bua kukuhandisha mu bianza bia mukashi wa bende,
# mu bianza bia mukashi udi muena

# nshila yandi idi ihueka tô ne ku bantu

20 Bua bualu ebu budi bua wewe

22 Kadi nebajimishe bantu babi ha buloba,
