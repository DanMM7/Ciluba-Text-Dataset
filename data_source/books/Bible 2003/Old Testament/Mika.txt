Mika 1
1 Dî dia Yehowa diakalua kudi Mika, Muena Molesheta, mu matuku a Yotama, Ahaza ne Hezikiya, bakelenge ba Yuda, bualu bua Samalea ne Yelushalema.
2 Unvuai, nuenu bantu, buonso buenu;
3 Monai, Yehowa udi uhatuka mu muaba wandi,
4 Mikuna neyivingutuke muinshi mua makasa andi,
5 Bua kutomboka kua Bena Yakoba malu â onso adi alua,
6 Bua malu â nenvuishe Samalea bu mushiki wa mabue mu budimi,
7 Nebatshibule mpingu yabo yonso misonga bituha, neboshe bintu biabo bionso biakafilabo kudi mpingu mu kahia,
8 Bua muanda eu nengase muadi, nendile,
# nendile ne kudila bu kua nyunyu wa nfiondo.
9 Bualu bua mputa wakumutahabo kena mua kuondahibua,
10 Kanuambi bualu ebu mu Gata, kanudidi nansha.
11 Hita biebe,
12 Muana mukashi udi mushikame mu Malota udi uhampakana
13 Suika makalu ku tubalu tudi tuenda lubilu,
14 Nunku bûdi nʼabu mbua kufila tshintu tshia kulayangana nʼatshi
15 Wewe, muana mukashi udi mushikame mu Malesha,
16 Dikungula mutu, kosa nsuki yebe yonso,

Mika 2
1 Mulau wikale kudi badi belangana meji a kuenza bibi,
2 Badi bakuma mitshima yabo ku madimi a bakuabo, badi bânyenga;
3 Bua malu â Yehowa udi wamba ne,
4 Mu dituku adi nebanusendeke,
5 Nunku mu disangisha dia Yehowa kanuena nuikala ne muntu
6 Badi bambila baprofete ne, Lekelai tshiakulakula tshienu;
7 Nebamanye mua kunukonka, nuenu Bena Yakoba, ne,
8 Kadi anu makelela bantu banyi bakubika bu bena lukuna kundi.
9 Nudi nuihata bakashi ba bantu banyi
10 Jukai, yai bienu!
11 Bianudinga muntu ne mei a mashimi, wamba ne,
12 Bulelela, nensangishe bana benu bonso, Yakoba;
13 Muhandi wa lumbu neadianjile ku mpala kuabo;

Mika 3
1 Nakamba kabidi ne,
2 Nuenu nudi ne buimpe lukuna ne nudi nunanga bubi;
3 nudi nudia munyinyi wa bantu banyi,
4 Hashishe, nebadile kudi Yehowa,
5 Yehowa udi wamba dî edi bualu bua baprofete
6 Nunku butuku nebunuidile, kanuyi numona kabidi bikena kumona,
7 Bamonyi ba malu nebatatakane,
8 Kadi meme hanyi, ndi mule tente ne bukole
9 Ndi nnusengelela bua nunvue bienu muanda eu,
10 Badi bibaka Siona ne mashi,
11 Badi ha mutu ha musoko budi balumbulula bilumbu anu bua mbueshilu,
12 Nunku bua bualu buenu nebabundulule Siona ne lukasu

Mika 4
1 Mu matuku alualua mukuna wa nsubu wa Yehowa
2 Ba mu bisamba bia bungi bia bantu nebalue, nebambe ne,
3 Yeye nealumbulule bilumbu bu munkatshi mua bantu bu bungi,
4 Kadi nebashikame, mu ntu ne muntu, muinshi mua muonshi wandi wa tumuma tua nvinyo
5 Bualu bua ba mu bisamba bionso batshidi benda
6 Yehowa udi wamba ne,
7 Nenyishe badi benda bazobele difuka dia tshisamba,
8 Wewe, tshibumba tshia balami ba mikoko yanyi,
9 Bualu kai udi ubandisha mei ebe a muadi mpindiu?
10 Ukenge hebe, tantamana, ulele, wewe muana mukashi wa Siona,
11 Katataka bisamba bia bungi bia bantu
12 Kadi kabena bamanye meji adi mu mutshima wa Yehowa,
13 Bika biebe, ubashihule,

Mika 5
1 Mpindiu udisangishe mu misumba, wewe muana mukashi, muena misumba;
2 Kadi wewe, Betelehema Efelata,
3 Nunku yeye neabafile mu bianza bia badi nʼabo lukuna
4 Yeye neajalame, neadishe mikoko yandi mu bukole bua Yehowa,
5 Yeye eu neikale ditalala dietu.
6 Nebabutule buloba bua Ashû ne muele wa nvita,
7 Difuka dia Yakoba nedikale
8 Difuka dia Yakoba nedikale munkatshi mua bisamba bia bantu,
9 Tshianza tshiebe tshitekeshe badi baluangana nʼebe,
10 Yehowa udi wamba ne, Mu dituku adi
11 Nenshihe misoko minene ya mu buloba buenu,
12 Nengumushe bia kutempa nʼabi mbuku mu tshianza tshienu;
13 Nentshibule mpingu yenu ne bibumba bienu bia tshijila
14 Nenjule mitshi yenu ya Bashela idi munkatshi muenu,
15 Nendisombuele ne njiya ne tshiji tshikole

Mika 6
1 Unvuai dî didi Yehowa wamba,
2 Nuenu mikuna minene, unvuai mpata idi Yehowa nʼai;
3 Bantu banyi, nakunuenzela tshinyi?
4 Bualu bua nakanuluhula mu buloba bua Ejipitu,
5 Bantu banyi, vulukai bienu mpindiu bualu buakahangidisha Balaka, mukelenge wa Bena Moaba, bua kuenza;
6 Ndue ne tshintu kai ku mpala kua Yehowa
7 Yehowa neasanke bua binunu bia bimpanga,
8 Wewe kampanda, bakukulesha bualu budi buakane;
9 Dî dia Yehowa didi diedibua mu musoko,
10 Biuma biakuhetabo ku bibi bitshidi mu nsubu ya bantu babi, anyi?
11 Ndi mumanye mua kuitabusha muntu udi ne tshihiminu tshidi tshidinga,
12 Bualu bua bantu babanji ba mu musoko eu
13 Nunku meme kabidi nennutahe,
14 Nenudie, kadi kanuena nukuta;
15 Nenukune maminu, kadi kanuena nuahuola;
16 Bualu bua nudi nutumikila mikenji ya Omeli,

Mika 7
1 Bualu bukole buakunkuata!
2 Muntu muakane wakujimina ha buloba,
3 Bianza biabo bidi ku malu mabi bua kuaenza lukasa;
4 Udi uhita bakuabo buimpe udi bu dieba,
5 Kueyemenyi muena mutumba nʼebe,
6 Bualu bua muana wa balume udi uhetula tatuʼandi,
7 Kadi meme hanyi nentangishe mesu anyi kudi Yehowa;
8 Kusanki bua dikenga dianyi, wewe muena lukuna wanyi;
9 Nengitabushe tshiji tshia Yehowa tshidiye nʼatshi kundi,
10 Hashishe, umena lukuna wanyi neamone muanda eu,
11 Dituku dia kuibaka ngumbu yebe kabidi nedifike!
12 Mu dituku adi nebalue kûdi,
13 Kadi buloba nebulue tshikulu
14 Ulombele bantu bebe ne mulangala webe wa bulami,
15 Bu munakenza mu matuku anuakuluhuka mu buloba bua Ejipitu,
16 Ba mu bisamba bia bantu nebamone malu â, nebakuatshike bundu
17 Nebalake dinfuenkenya bu nyoka
18 Neanyi udi Nzambi bu wewe, udi ubajimishila bubi,
19 Yeye neatufuile luse kabidi,
20 Neuleshe Yakoba lulamatu luebe,
