SEFANYA 1
1 Dî dia Yehowa diakalua kudi Sefanya, muana wa Kushi, muana wa Gedaliya, muana wa Amalia, muana wa Hezikiya mu matuku a Yoshiya, muana wa Amona, mukelenge wa Yuda.
2 Yehowa udi wamba ne, Nembutule bintu bionso
3 Nembutule bantu ne nyama,
4 Nengolole tshianza tshianyi bua kukuma Bena Yuda
5 ne badi batendelela bintu bia mu diulu
6 Nengumushe kabidi badi balekela kulonda Yehowa
7 Huwa biebe ku mpala kua Mukelenge Yehowa,
8 Mu dituku didibo bafila mulambu wa Yehowa
9 Mu didiku adi nenkengeshe
10 Yehowa udi Wamba ne, Mu dituku adi
11 Asai miadi, nuenu badi bashikame mu Maketesha,
12 Mu tshikondo atshi nenkebekebe mu Yelushalema ne minda,
13 Biuma biabo nebinyengibue kudi bakuabo,
14 Dituku dinene dia Yehowa didi ha buihi;
15 Dituku adi ndituku dia njiya,
16 ndituku dia kuela kua mpungi ne kua mbila ya nvita
17 Nentumine bantu makenga,
18 Biamu biabo, nansha bia argent, nansha bia or,

SEFANYA 2
1 Nudisangishe hohamue, e, nudisangishe hohamue,
2 diambedi mukenji kauyi muanze kukola,
3 Kebai Yehowa, nuenu bena kalolo bonso ba ha buloba,
4 Bualu bua Gaza neashale tshikulu,
5 Mulau wikale kudi bantu badi bashikame
6 Ku muelelu wa mai manene nekuikale miaba ya mashinde
7 Buloba budi ku muelelu wa mai manene
8 Nakunvua bibingu bia Bena Moaba,
9 Yehowa wa misumba, Nzambi wa Isalele mene, udi wamba ne,
10 Mianda ai neyilue kudibo bualu bua kudisua kuabo,
11 Yehowa neabakuatshishe buowa;
12 Nuenu kabidi, Bena Atiopa,
13 Yeye neolole tshianza tshiandi bua kukuma ba ku luseke lua nôde,
14 Bisumbu bia nyama nebilale hanshi munkatshi muau,
15 Musoko eu wakadi bantu basankila bikole,

SEFANYA 3
1 Mulau wikale kudi musoko udi mutomboke ne munyanguke,
2 Kawakunvuila dî,
3 Bakokeshi ba munkatshi muau
4 Baprofete babo badi babuluke
5 Yehowa udi muakane munkatshi muau;
6 Wakushiha bisamba bia bantu;
7 Nakadiamba munda muanyi ne,
8 Nunku Yehowa udi wamba ne, Nungindile,
9 Bualu bua nenkudimune muaku wa bantu ba bende
10 Ku miaba idi dishiya dia misulu ya mu Atiopa
11 Mu dituku adi kuena ufuishibua bundu
12 Bualu bua nenshiye bantu bakengeshibue ne bahele munkatshi muenu,
13 Ba mu Isalele batshidi bashala
14 Imba biebe, wewe muana mukashi wa Siona;
15 Yehowa wakûmusha bilumbu biebe,
16 Mu dituku adi nebambile Yelushalema ne,
17 Yehowa Nzambi webe udi munkatshi muenu,
18 Nensangishe hohamue badi bajinga dituku dia tshijila
19 Mona, mu tshikondo atshi nenshikishe tshilumbu
20 Mu tshikondo atshi nendue nʼenu,
