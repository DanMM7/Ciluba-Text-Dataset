Nahum 1
1 Di bualu bua Ninewe Mukanda wa tshikena kumona tshia Nahuma, Muena Elekosha
2 Yehowa udi Nzambi wa mukau ne udi udisombuela,
3 Yehowa kena ukuata tshiji lukasa, udi ne bukole bua bungi,
4 Udi ubulukila mai manene, udi wakandisha,
5 Mikuna minene idi ikanka ku mpala kuandi,
6 Udi mua kuimana ku mpala kua tshiji tshiandi nganyi?
7 Yehowa udi muimpe,
8 Kadi yeye neabutule muaba wa badi nʼandi lukuna
9 Nudi nuelela Yehowa tshifufu kai?
10 Badi bajingakane bu mingonge ya meba,
11 Umue wakahatuka kûdi,
12 Yehowa udi wamba dî edi ne,
13 Mpindiu nentshibule mutshi wandi wa tshikokedi udi ha nshingu hebe,
14 Yehowa wakuela mukenji bua bualu buebe ne,
15 Monai, ha mikuna hadi makasa a messager

Nahum 2
1 Mubutudi udi ulua kukubunda
2 Bualu bua Yehowa udi ujadika butumbi bua Yakoba
3 Ngabu ya bena bukitu bandi idi mikunze-kunzû,
4 Makalu a mata adi e nza diyoyo mu misesu,
5 Mukelenge wa Ashû udi uvuluka bilobo biandi,
6 Bibi bia ku nkila ya misulu bidi biunzuluka,
7 Bakukuata mukelenge mukashi,
8 Kale Ninewe wakadi mule tente ne bantu,
9 Haulai biamu bia argent!
10 Musoko eu udi mutuhu, kauyi bantu, kauyi bintu! Mutshima udi uteketa, binu bidi bikanka,
11 Buina bua nyama ya ntambue
12 Nyama wa ntambue wakasunsuluila bana bandi munyinyi,
13 Yehowa wa misumba udi wamba ne, Mona, ndi nkuhidia, nenshidishe makalu ebe mu kahia, muele wa mata neumane bana bebe ba ntambue, bintu biebe biwakunyenga, nembiumushe ha buloba, bantu kabena bunvua kabidi dî dia ba-messager bebe.

Nahum 3
1 Mulau wikale kudi musoko eu udi umatshisha mashi a bantu hanshi,
2 Tshiona tshia nfimbu ne tshiona tshia nkata ya makalu bidi biunvuika,
3 Bena tubalu badi bendesha tubalu tuabo lubilu,
4 Malu â akulua bua masandi mavule a mukashi wa masandi,
5 Yehowa wa misumba udi wamba ne, Mona, meme ndi nku hidia,
6 Nenkuele bintu bia bukoya hamʼbidi hebe,
7 Bua mianda ai mundi yonso udi ukumona neakunyeme, wamba ne,
8 No-amona eu,
9 Atiopa ne Ejipitu biakadi bukole buandi,
10 Kadi bakaya nʼandi kukuabo,
11 Wewe kabidi neukuatshike maluvu,
12 Ngumbu yebe ya mata neyikale bu mitshi ya nfigi
13 Mona, bena nvita bebe
14 Suna mai bua kuikala nʼau mu matuku a kutshintshimika kua musoko,
15 Mu muaba eu kahia nekakushidishe,
16 Bangendʼa mushinga bebe
17 Bakokeshi bebe badi bu mikumbi,
18 Wewe mukelenge wa Ashû,
19 Kakuena diondaha dia mputa webe,
