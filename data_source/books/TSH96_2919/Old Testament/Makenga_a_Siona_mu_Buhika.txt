Lamentations 1
1 Monai musoko wakadi tente ne bantu
2 Yeye udi udila bikole bufuku,
3 Bakuya ne Yuda mu buhika bua kuikaleye ne dikenga
4 Nshila ya ku Siona idi idila muadi,
5 Bamukengeshi bakumutshimuna,
6 Bulengele buonso bua muana mukashi wa Siona
7 Mu matuku a makenga andi ne a kanyinganyinga kandi
8 Yelushalema wakenzela Nzambi bibi,
9 Bukoya buandi buakadi ku bilulu biandi;
10 Muena lukuna wandi wakuhaula
11 Bantu bandi bonso badi hatua mikemu
12 Monai, nuenu honso badi bahita mu nshila,
13 Ku muaba wandi wa mu diulu yeye wakatuma kahia hanshi,
14 Mibi yanyi yakusuikibua
15 Mukelenge wakuyisha bena nvita banyi bakole bonso
16 Bua malu â onso udi ndila;
17 Siona udi wolola bianza biandi ne kakuena mumusambi;
18 Yehowa udi mubinge,
19 Nakubikila banangi banyi,
20 Yehowa, tangila biebe, bualu bua ndi mu ntatu,
21 Bakunvua mundi ntua mikemu,
22 Mibi yabo yonso imueneke ku mesu kuebe,

Lamentations 2
1 Mona mudi Mukelenge mubuikile muana mukashi wa
2 Mukelenge wakubutula nsubu yonso ya Yakoba,
3 Yeye wakuhempa lusengu luonso lua Isalele
4 Wakunana buta buandi bu mudi umena lukuna wenza,
5 Mukelenge wakulua bu muena lukuna,
6 Wakusasula tshitendelelu tshiandi ku bukole, tshitandatanda tshia mu budimi;
7 Mukelenge wakuhidia tshioshelu tshiandi,
8 Yehowa wakahangidisha bualu bua kubutula
9 Bibi biandi biakubuela mu buloba;
10 Bakulu ba muana mukashi wa Siona
11 Mesu anyi adi mûle tente ne binsonshi,
12 Hatshidibo baleuka mu misesu
13 Nenkuambile tshinyi, nenkuidikishe ne tshinyi,
14 Bua bualu buebe baprofete bebe bakumona
15 Bantu bonso badi bahita mu nshila
16 Bena lukuna bebe bonso bakubutula mukana muabo
17 Yehowa wakuenza bualu ebu buakuhangidisheye bua kuenza;
18 Mitshima yabo yakadila kudi Mukelenge,
19 Juka, asa muadi mu butuku,
20 Yehowa, utangile uwakuenzela mudiye!
21 Bansongalume ne bakulumpe badi balala
22 Wakumbikidila buowa bunnyunguluke nseke yonso,

Lamentations 3
1 Meme ndi muntu wakumona dikenga
2 Yeye wakundombela ne wakungendesha mu mîdima,
3 Bulelela, wakunkengesha ne tshianza tshiandi ku musangu ne ku musangu
4 Wakumanyisha munyinyi wa mubidi wanyi ne dikoba dianyi;
5 Wakungomekela ntatu,
6 Wakunshikika mu miaba ya midima
7 Wakunnyungulula ne lumbu, nunku tshiena mua kuhatuka;
8 Bulelela, banadila, hanakeba dikuatshisha,
9 Yeye wakujika nshila yanyi ne mabue masonga, wakukuonyangasha nshila yanyi.
10 Yeye udi bu nkashama kundi udi ungalamina,
11 Yeye wakunsesuisha mu nshila wanyi,
12 Wakunana buta buandi, waku nteka kulu
13 Wakasa mutshima wanyi mikete
14 Nakulua bu tshintu tshidibo baseka kudi bantu bonso,
15 Wakungusha tente ne nyongangandu,
16 Wakutshibula menu anyi ne nsoka mikese;
17 Wakûmusha ditalala dia mu mutshima wanyi, wakuditeka kule;
18 Nunku nakuamba hanyi ne, Bukole buanyi buakujimina,
19 Uvuluke dikenga dianyi ne kanyinganyinga kanyi,
20 Mutshima wanyi utshidi ubivuluka,
21 Ndi nvuluka muanda eu,
22 Malu makane a Yehowa kaena ashika tshiendelele,
23 bidi bihia-bihia ku dinda ku dinda,
24 Mutshima wanyi udi wamba ne, Yehowa udi buhianyi buanyi,
25 Yehowa udi ne luse kudi badi bamuindila,
26 Bidi bimpe bua muntu atekemene ne indile biandi
27 Bidi bimpe bua muntu ambule biandi mutshi wa tshikokedi
28 Ashikame biandi nkayandi ahuwe talalâ,
29 Atue mishiku yandi mu buloba,
30 Afile ditama diandi kudi udi umututa;
31 Bualu bua Mukelenge kena wihata
32 Kadi hamukengesheye,
33 Bualu bua kena usanka bua kubakengesha,
34 Bualu bua badiatabo bena lukanu bonso ha buloba
35 hasesukabo ku kulumbulula kuakane kua muntu
36 hanyangabo tshilumbu tshia muntu ne kulumbulula kubi,
37 Muntu kai udi mua kuela dî ne, Bualu buenjibue,
38 Makenga ne masanka kabiena bifuma
39 Nunku bualu kai muntu utshidi ne muoyo
40 Tukebulule mu mitshima yetu,
41 Tubandishe mitshima yetu ne bianza diatshimue
42 Tuakushiha mikenji yebe ne tuakukutombokela;
43 Wewe wakudibuikila ne tshiji, wakutuihata mu nshila,
44 Wakudibuikila ne ditutu,
45 Wakutuvuisha bu tshintu tshia hatuhu tshidibo bimasha
46 Bena lukuna betu bonso bakubulula mukana muabo
47 Buowa ne dijimba bidi ku mpala kuetu,
48 Mesu anyi adi ahatula binsonshi bu misulu ya mai
49 Mesu anyi adi uhuekesha binsonshi,
50 tô ne hatangila Yehowa wa mu diulu hanshi,
51 Mutshima wanyi udi unyingalala bua malu adi mesu anyi amone,
52 Bena lukuna banyi bakunkina hatuhu, kahayi ne tshilumbu tshia kumbanda nʼatshi,
53 Bakungela mu tshina bua kujimisha muoyo wanyi,
54 Mai akahita ha mutu banyi;
55 Yehowa, nakabikila dina diebe,
56 Wewe wakunvua dî dianyi ne,
57 Wewe wakasemena ha buihi nʼanyi mu dituku dinakukubikila;
58 Wewe, Mukelenge, wakungakuila ku bilumbu bia mu mutshima wanyi;
59 Wewe, Mukelenge, wakumona malu mabi akungenzelabo;
60 Wewe wakumona disombuela diabo
61 Yehowa, wakumpua bihendu biabo
62 mei adi ahatuka mukana mua bambanda
63 Tangila hashikamabo, hajukabo;
64 Yehowa, wewe ne ubafute difutu
65 Neubahe mitshima mikole,
66 Yehowa, wewe ne ubihate ne tshiji,

Lamentations 4
1 Monai, tshiamu tshia or tshiakubuitshidila!
2 Bana balume ba Siona ba mushi nga mukole,
3 Nyama ya mibuabua mene mene idi yamusha bana bai mabele;
# bu nyunyu ya nfiondo
4 Ludimi lua muana utshidi wamua mabele
5 Bakadi badia bia kudia bishema
6 Dikenga diakukuata muana mukashi wa bana betu bua mibi yandi
7 Bakokeshi bandi bakadi ne mitshima mihite neige butoke,
# mibidi yabo yakadi ne lukunjilakunjila luhite dibue dia corail
# dinkenke diai diakadi bu dia dibue dia saphir.
8 Kadi mpindiu mpala yabo idi ihita makala a kahia bufike;
9 Diakalengele dia badi bashihibue ku muele wa nvita
10 Bianza bia bakashi badi ne mitshima ya luse
11 Yehowa wakabakengesha tô ne hakashika tshiji tshiandi,
12 Bakelenge ba ha buloba
13 Malu â akulua bualu bua mibi ya baprofete bandi
14 Bakadi babubuta bu bantu bafofo mu misesu;
15 Bakabela mbila ne, Umukai kutudi! Nudi banyanguke!
16 Tshiji tshia Yehowa tshiakubamuangalasha;
17 Mesu etu akufula hatuakadi bindile, batekemena dikuatshisha;
18 Batukebi bakadi benda batulonda bua kututatshisha,
19 Batuihatshi bakahita nyunyu ya mikanku ya mulu
20 Mukelenge muedibue manyi kudi Yehowa, wakakuatshibua mu majimba abo,
21 Wewe muana mukashi wa Edome, udi mushikame mu buloba bua Uza,
22 Wewe muana mukashi wa Siona, dikenga diebe bua bubi diakushika,

Lamentations 5
1 Yehowa, uvuluke bualu buakutukuata;
2 Bakufila buhianyi buetu kudi bantu ba bende,
3 Tuakulua bu bana ba nshiya, bu badi kabayi ne batatuʼabo;
4 Tudi tusumba mai atudi tunua;
5 Batuihatshi badi batutua tshianza mu dikoshi;
6 Tuakuolola bianza bietu kudi Bena Ejipitu,
7 Batatuʼetu bakenza malu mabi, kabenaku kabidi;
8 Bahika badi batukokesha,
9 Tudi tukeba kujiuka hatuaheta bia kudia bietu,
10 Dikoba dietu didi ditema bu luesu ludi heku,
11 Badi bakuata bakashi ku bukole mu Siona,
12 Badi balembeleshe bakokeshi kulu bakudika ku bianza biabo;
13 Bansongalume badi benza mu dimu wa kuhela blé ha dibue,
14 Bakulu kabena bashikama kabidi mu bilumbuluidi bia ku
15 Disanka dia mitshima yetu diakujimina,
16 Tshifulu tshia butumbi tshiakutuka ku mutu kuetu;
17 Bua bualu ebu mitshima yetu yakuteketa;
18 bualu bua Mukuna wa Siona wakulua bu tshihela tshia hatuhu,
19 Wewe, Yehowa, udi mushikame mu nkuasa webe wa butumbi tshiendelele;
20 Bualu kai udi musue kutuhita muoyo tshiendelele,
21 Utuhingashe kûdi kabidi, Yehowa, ne tuetu netuhingane;
