1 Chronicles 1
1 Adama, Shete, Enosha,
2 Kenana, Mahalalele, Yelede,
3 Hanoka, Metushela, Lameke,
4 Noa, Sheme, Hama, Yefeta.
5 Bana balume ba Yefeta: Gomê ne Magoga ne Madai ne Yawana ne Tubala ne Mesheke ne Tilasa.
6 Bana balume ba Gomê: Ashakenaza ne Difata ne Togâma.
7 Bana balume ba Yawana: Elisha ne Tâshighi ne Bena Kiti ne Lodanima.
8 Bana balume ba Hama: Kusha ne Miselayima ne Puta ne Kanâna.
9 Bana balume ba Kusha: Seba ne Hawila ne Sabeta ne Lâma ne Sabeteka. Bana balume ba Lâma: Sheba ne Dedana.
10 Kusha wakalela Nimeloda, wakabanga kuikala muntu mukole mu buloba.
11 Miselayima wakalela Ludima ne Anamima ne Lehabima ne Nafetuhima
12 ne Patalusima ne Kasaluhima (kuakafuma Bena Peleshete) ne Kafetolima.
13 Kanâna wakalela Sidona, muanʼa bute wandi, ne Hete
14 ne Bena Yebuse ne Bena Amole ne Bena Gîgashi,
15 ne Bena Hiwi ne Bena Âka ne Bena Sina
16 ne Bena Âwada ne Bena Semâ ne Bena Hamata.
17 Bana balume ba Sheme: Elama ne Ashû ne Apakashada ne Luda ne Alama ne Uza ne Hula ne Getê ne Mesheke.
18 Apakashada wakalela Shela; ne Shela wakalela Ebê.
19 Ebe wakalela bana balume babidi; dina dia mukuabo Pelege; mu matuku andi bakabanyangana buloba; dina dia muanʼabo mulume diakadi Yoketana.
20 Yoketana wakalela Alamodada ne Shelefe ne Hasâ-maweta ne Yela
21 ne Hadolama ne Uzala ne Dikela
22 ne Ebala ne Abimaele ne Sheba
23 ne Ofî ne Hawila ne Yobaba. Aba bonso mbana balume ba Yoketana.
24 Sheme, Apakashada, Shela,
25 Ebê, Pelege, Leu,
26 Seluga, Nahô, Tela,
27 Abalama (udi Abalahama mene).
28 Bana balume ba Abalahama: Isaka ne Yishemaele.
29 Etu ntukanunuina tuabo: muanʼa bute wa Yishemaele wakadi Nebayota; hashishe Kedâ ne Adabele ne Mibesama,
30 Mishema ne Duma, Masa, Hadada ne Tema,
31 Yetû, Nafisha ne Kedema. Aba mbana balume ba Yishemaele.
32 Bana balume ba Ketula, mukashi muhika mubakibue kudi Abalahama: yeye wakalela Zimelana ne Yokeshana ne Medana ne Midiyana ne Ishebaka ne Shua. Bana balume ba Yokeshana: Sheba ne Dedana.
33 Bana balume ba Midiyana: Efa ne Efê ne Hanoka ne Abida ne Eleda. Aba bonso bakadi bana balume ba Ketula.
34 Abalahama wakalela Isaka. Bana balume ba Isaka: Esau ne Isalele.
35 Bana balume ba Esau: Elifaza ne Luele ne Yeusha ne Yalama, ne Kola.
36 Bana balume ba Elifaza: Temana ne Omâ, Sefi ne Gatama, Kenaza ne Timena ne Amaleka.
37 Bana balume ba Luele: Nahata, Zela, Shama ne Miza.
38 Bana balume ba Sei: Lotana ne Shobala ne Sibiona ne Ana ne Dishona ne Ezê ne Dishana.
39 Bana balume ba Lotana: Holi ne Homama; Timena wakadi muanʼabo mukashi ne Lotana.
40 Bana balume ba Shobala: Abana ne Manahata ne Ebala ne Shefi ne Onama. Bana balume ba Sibiona: Aya ne Ana.
41 Bana balume ba Ana: Dishona. Bana balume ba Dishona: Hamalana ne Eshebana ne Yitelana ne Kelana.
42 Bana balume ba Ezê: Bilehana ne Zawana ne Yakana. Bana balume ba Dishana: Usa ne Alana.
43 Aba mbakelenge bakakokesha mu buloba bua Edome diambedi mukelenge kayi muanze kukokesha Bena Isalele: Bela, muana mulume wa Beô; dina dia musoko wandi diakadi Dinehaba.
44 Bela wakafua ne Yobaba, muana mulume wa Zela wa mu Bosela, wakakokesha mu muaba wandi.
45 Yobaba wakafua ne Hushama, wa mu buloba bua Bena Temana, wakakokesha mu muaba wandi.
46 Hushama wakafua ne Hadada, muana mulume wa Bedada, wakashiha Midiyana mu buloba bua Moaba, wakakokesha mu muaba wandi; dina dia musoko wandi diakadi Awita.
47 Hadada wakafua ne Samala wa mu Maseleka wakakokesha mu muaba wandi.
48 Samala wakafua ne Shaula wa mu Lehobota hehi ne musulu wa Pelata wakakokesha mu muaba wandi.
49 Shaula wakafua ne Bâla-hanana, muana mulume wa Akabo, wakakokesha mu muaba wandi.
50 Bâla-hanana wakafua ne Hadada wakakokesha mu muaba wandi; dina dia musoko wandi diakadi Pai; dina dia mukashi wandi diakadi Mehetabela, muana wa bakashi wa Mateledi, muana wa bakashi wa Me-zahaba.
51 Hadada wakafua biandi.
52 kalala Oholibama, kalala Ela, kalala Pinona,
53 kalala Kenaza, kalala Temana, kalala Mi-bezâ,
54 kalala Magadiele, kalala Hama. Etu tuakadi tulala tua mu Edome.

1 Chronicles 2
1 Aba mbana balume ba Isalele: Lubena, Shimeona, Lewi, ne Yuda, Yisakâ ne Zebuluna,
2 Dana, Yosefe ne Benyamina, Nafatale, Gada ne Ashê.
3 Bana ba balume ba Yuda: Ele ne Ônana ne Shela: muana mukashi wa Shua, Muena Kanâna, wakamulelela aba basatu. Ele, muanʼa bute wa Yuda, wakadi muntu mubi ku mesu kua Yehowa; yeye wakamushiha.
4 Tamâ, mukashi wa muanʼandi, wakamulelela Pelese ne Zela. Bana ba balume ba Yuda bonso bakadi batanu.
5 Bana ba balume ba Pelese: Heselona ne Hamula.
6 Bana ba balume ba Zela: Zimeli ne Etana ne Hemana ne Kalakola ne Dala; buonso buabo batanu.
7 Bana ba balume ba Kâmi; Akâ, mutatshi wa Isalele, wakenza bualu bubi ne tshintu tshia tshijila.
8 Bana ba balume ba Etana: Azâya.
9 Bana ba balume ba Heselona bakaleleye bakadi Yelamele ne Lama ne Kelubai.
10 Lama wakalela Aminadaba ne Aminadaba wakalela Nashona, muntu munene wa munkatshi mua Bena Yuda;
11 Nashona wakalela Salama ne Salama wakalela Boaza;
12 Boaza wakalela Obeda, ne Obeda wakalela Yishai;
13 Yishai wakalela muanʼa bute wandi Eliaba ne Abinadaba muana muibidi ne Shimea muana muisatu,
14 Netanele muana muinai ne Ladai muana muitanu,
15 Osema muana muisambombo ne Davidi muana wa muanda mutekete;
16 bana babo ba bakashi bakadi Seluya ne Abigayila. Bana ba balume ba Seluya bakadi Abishai ne Yoaba ne Asahele, bana basatu.
17 Abigayila wakalela Amasa; tatu wa Amasa wakadi Yete, Muena Yishemaele.
18 Kaleba, muana wa balume wa Heselona, wakalela bana ne mukashi wandi Azuba ne mukashi wandi Yeliota; bana bandi ba balume bakadi Yeshê ne Shobaba ne Âdona.
19 Azuba wakafua, ne Kaleba wakabaka Efelata, wakamulelela Hû.
20 Hû wakalela Uli ne Uli wakalela Bezalele.
21 Hashishe Heselona wakaya kudi muana mukashi wa Maki, tatu wa Gilada, wakamuangata bu mukashi wandi hakadiye ne bidimu makumi asambombo; yeye wakamulelela Seguba.
22 Seguba wakalela Yaî, wakadi ne misoko makumi abidi ne isatu mu buloba bua Gilada.
23 Geshû ne Alama bakanyenga misoko ya bena Yai ne Kenata ne misoko yai yonso bu misoko makumi asambombo. Aba bonso bakadi bana ba balume ba Maki, tatu wa Gilada.
24 Hakafua Heselona mu Kaleba-efalata, hashishe Abiya, mukashi wa Heselona, wakamulelela Ashehû, tatu wa Tekoa.
25 Bana ba balume ba Yelamele, muanʼa bute wa Heselona, bakadi Lama, muanʼa bute wandi, ne Buna ne Olena ne Ozema ne Ahiya.
26 Yelamele wakadi ne mukashi mukuabo, dina diandi Atala; yeye wakadi mamu wa Onama.
27 Bana ba balume ba Lama, muanʼa bute wa Yelamele, bakadi Mâsa ne Yamina ne Ekê.
28 Bana ba balume ba Onama bakadi Shamai ne Yada. Bana ba balume ba Shamai: Nadaba ne Abishû.
29 Dina dia mukashi wa Abishû diakadi Abihayila; yeye wakamulelela Abana ne Moliya.
30 Bana ba balume ba Nadaba: Selede ne Apaima; kadi Selede wakafua kayi ne bana ba balume.
31 Bana ba balume ba Apaima: Yishi. Bana ba balume ba Yishi: Sheshana. Bana ba balume ba Sheshana: Ahalai.
32 Bana ba balume ba Yada, muanʼabo wa balume ne Shamai: Yete ne Yonatana; Yete wakafua kayi ne bana ba balume.
33 Bana ba balume ba Yonatana: Pelete ne Zaza. Aba mbana ba balume ba Yelamele.
34 Sheshana kakadi ne bana ba balume, anu bana ba bakashi. Sheshana wakadi ne muena mudimu, Muena Ejipitu, dina diandi Yaha.
35 Sheshana wakafila muanʼandi mukashi, kudi Yaha, muena mudimu wandi, bu mukashi wandi; yeye wakamulelela Atai.
36 Atai wakalela Natana ne Natana wakalela Zabada,
37 Zabada wakalela Efelala, ne Efelala wakalela Obeda,
38 ne Obeda wakalela Yehu, ne Yehu wakalela Azâya,
39 ne Azâya wakalela Helese, ne Helese wakalela Eleasa,
40 ne Eleasa wakalela Sisemai, ne Sisemai wakalela Shaluma,
41 ne Shaluma wakalela Yakameya, ne Yakameya wakalela Elishama.
42 Bana ba, balume ba Kaleba, muanʼabo wa balume ne Yelamele, bakadi Mesha, muanʼa bute wandi tatu wa Zifa; ne bana ba balume ba Malesha: Hebelone.
43 Bana ba balume ba Hebelone: Kola ne Tapua ne Lekeme ne Shema.
44 Shema wakalela Lahama, tatu wa Yôkeama; Lekeme wakalela Shamai.
45 Muana wa balume wa Shamai wakadi Maona; Maona wakadi tatu wa Bete-sû.
46 Efa, mukashi muhika mubakibue kudi Kaleba, wakamulelela Halana ne Mosa ne Gazeza; Halana wakalela Gazeza.
47 Bana ba balume ba Yadai: Legeme ne Yotana ne Geshana ne Pelete ne Efa ne Shâfa.
48 Mâka, munga mukashi muhika mubakibue kudi Kaleba, wakamulelela Shebê ne Tîhana.
49 Yeye wakamulelela kabidi Shâfa, tatu wa Mademana, ne Shewa tatu wa Makebena ne tatu wa Gibea; muana wa bakashi wa Kaleba wakadi Akasa.
50 Aba mbana balume ba Kaleba,
51 Salama, tatu wa Beteleheme, Halefe, tatu wa Bete-gadê.
52 Shobala, tatu wa Kîyata-yalima, wakadi ne bana ba balume kabidi, Haloe, tshituha tshia Bena Menuhota.
53 Meku a Kîyata-yalima akadi Bena Yitela ne Bena Puta ne Bena Shumati ne Bena Mishete; Bena Solea ne Bena Eshetaola bakafuma kudibo.
54 Bana ba balume ba Salama: Beteleheme ne Bena Netofa, Atalota-bete-yoaba ne tshituha tshia Bena Manahata, Bena Sola mene.
55 Au mmeku a bafundi akashikama mu Yabesa: Bena Tila ne Bena Shimeata ne Bena Sukata. Aba mBena Kene bakafuma kudi Hamata, tatu wa ba mu nsubu wa Lekaba.

1 Chronicles 3
1 Bana ba balume ba Davidi bakamulelelabo mu Hebelone: Amenona, muanʼa bute, muana wa Ahinoama, Muena Yizelele; muana muibidi Danyele, muana wa Abigayila, Muena Kâmele;
2 muana muisatu Abashaloma, muana wa Mâka, muana wa bakashi wa Talemai, mukelenge wa mu Geshû; muana muinai Adoniya, muana wa Hagita;
3 muana muitanu Shefatiya, muana wa Abitala; muana muisambombo Itelema, muana wa mukashi wandi Egela:
4 hakamulelela bana basambombo mu Hebelone kuakakokesheye bidimu muanda mutekete ne ngondo isambombo. Yeye wakakokesha mu Yelushalema bidimu makumi asatu ne bisatu;
5 bakamulelela mu Yelushalema: Shimea ne Shobaba ne Natana ne Solomo, bana banai kudi Bata-shua, muana wa bakashi wa Amiele,
6 ne Ibeha ne Elishama ne Elifelete
7 ne Noga ne Nefege ne Yafia
8 ne Elishama ne Eliada ne Elifelete, buonso buabo tshitema.
9 Aba bonso mbana balume ba Davidi ha mutu ha bana balume ba bakashi bahika babakibue kudiye; Tamâ wakadi muanʼabo mukashi.
10 Muana wa balume wa Solomo wakadi Lehabama, Abiya wakadi muanʼandi, Asa wakadi muanʼandi, ne Yehoshafata wakadi muanʼandi,
11 Yolama wakadi muanʼandi, Ahazaya wakadi muanʼandi, ne Yoasha wakadi muanʼandi,
12 Amasaya wakadi muanʼandi, ne Azaya wakadi muanʼandi, ne Yotama wakadi muanʼandi,
13 Ahaza wakadi muanʼandi, Hezikiya wakadi muanʼandi, ne Manashe wakadi muanʼandi,
14 Amona wakadi muanʼandi, ne Yoshiya wakadi muanʼandi.
15 Bana ba balume ba Yoshiya: Yohanana muanʼa bute wandi, Yehoyakima muana muibidi, Sedekiya muana muisatu ne Shaluma muana muinai.
16 Bana ba balume ba Yehoyakima: Yekoniya ne Sedekiya.
17 Bana ba balume ba Yekoliya, muena lukanu: Shalatiele
18 ne Malekilama ne Pedaya ne Shenasâ ne Yekamiya, Hoshama ne Nedabiya.
19 Bana ba balume ba Pedaya: Zelubabela ne Shimei. Bana ba balume ba Zelubabela: Meshulama ne Hananiya; (Shelomita wakadi muanʼabo wa bakashi),
20 Hashuba ne Ohela ne Belekiya ne Hasadeya ne Yushaba-heseda, buonso buabo batanu.
21 Bana ba balume ba Hananiya: Pelatiya ne Yeshaya; muanʼandi wa balume Lefaya, muanʼandi wa balume Anana, muanʼandi wa balume Obadiya, muanʼandi wa balume Shekaniya.
22 Bana ba balume ba Shekaniya: Shemaya. Bana ba balume ba Shemaya: Hatusha ne Igala ne Baliya ne Nealiya ne Shafata, basambombo bonso.
23 Bana ba balume ba Nealiya: Eleyehoenai ne Hizikiya ne Azalikama, basatu bonso.
24 Bana ba balume ba Eleyehoenai: Hodawiya ne Eleyashiba ne Pelaya ne Akuba ne Yohanana ne Delaya ne Anani, muanda mutekete bonso.

1 Chronicles 4
1 Bana ba balume ba Yuda: Pelese, Heselona ne Kâmi ne Hula ne Shobala.
2 Leaya, muana wa Shobala, wakalela Yahata; Yahata wakalela Ahumai ne Lahada. Au mmeku a Bena Solea.
3 Aba mbana ba tatu wa Etama: Yizelele ne Yishema ne Yidebasha; dina dia muanʼabo wa bakashi diakadi Haselele-poni;
4 Penuele, tatu wa Gedô, ne Ezê, tatu wa Husha. Aba mbana ba balume ba Hû, muanʼa bute wa Efelata, tatu wa Beteleheme.
5 Ashehû, tatu wa Tekoa, wakadi ne bakashi babidi, Hela ne Nâla.
6 Nâla wakamulelela Ahuzama ne Hefê ne Temeni ne Hâhashatali. Aba bakadi bana ba balume ba Nâla.
7 Bana ba balume ba Hela bakadi Selete, Yisheâ ne Etenana.
8 Hakosa wakalela Anuba ne Sobeba ne ba mu meku a Ahahele, muana wa balume wa Haluma.
9 Yabesa wakatamba bana babo mu butumbi; mamu wandi wakamuidika dina ne, Yabesa, wamba ne, Nakamulela ne kanyinganyinga.
10 Yabesa wakatendelela Nzambi wa Isalele wamba ne, Nakadi kusua bua wewe kunsankisha ne kulehesha mikalu yanyi ne bua tshianza tshiebe tshikale nʼanyi, bua wewe kundama bua tshibuedi mu malu mabi ne bua meme tshikadi ne kanyinganyinga! Nzambi wakamuha malu akalombeye.
11 Keluba, muanʼabo wa balume ne Shuha, wakalela Mehî wakadi tatu wa Eshetona.
12 Eshetona wakalela Bete-lafa ne Pasea ne Tehina, tatu wa Inahasha. Aba mbantu ba Leka.
13 Bana ba balume ba Kenaza; Oteniele ne Selaya. Bana ba balume ba Oteniele: Hatata.
14 Meonotai wakalela Ofela; ne Selaya wakalela; Yoaba, tatu wa Bena Ge-halashima; bualu bua bobo bakadi bansenda.
15 Bana ba balume ba Kaleba, muana wa Yefune: Ilu, Ela ne Nâma; bana ba balume ba Ela: Kenaza.
16 Bana ba balume ba Yehalelele: Zifa ne Zifaha, Tileya ne Asalele.
17 Bana ba balume ba Ezela: Yete ne Melede ne Efê ne Yalona; mukashi wandi wakamulelela kabidi Mîyama ne Shamai ne Yisheba, tatu wa Eshetemoa.
18 Mukashi wandi, Muena Yuda, wakalela Yelede, tatu wa Gedô, ne Hebê, tatu wa Soko, ne Yekutiele, tatu wa Zanoa. Aba mbana ba balume ba Bitiya, muana wa bakashi wa Palo, wakabakibua kudi Melede.
19 Bana ba balume ba mukashi wa Hodiya, muanʼabo wa bakashi ne Nahama, bakadi batatu ba Keila, Muena Gâma, ne Eshetemoa, Muena Mâka.
20 Bana ba balume ba Shimona: Amenona ne Lina, Bene-hanana ne Tilona. Bana ba balume ba Ishi: Zoheta ne Bene-zoheta.
21 Bana ba balume ba Shela, muana wa Yuda: Ele, tatu wa Leka, ne Lâda, tatu wa Malesha ne ba mu nsubu wa bakumi ba bilulu bia lin, ba mu nsubu wa Ashabea;
22 Yolama ne Bena Kozeba ne Yoasha ne Salafa wakakokesha mu buloba bua Moaba, ne Yashubi-lehema. Eku nkulondolola kua kale kale.
23 Aba mbafumbi ba bivuadi ne bakadi bashikama mu Netaima ne Gedela; bakashikamaku ne mukelenge bua mudimu wandi.
24 Bana ba balume ba Shimeona: Nemuele ne Yamina, Yaliba, Zela, Shaula;
25 Shaluma muanʼandi, Mibesama muanʼandi, Mishema muanʼandi.
26 Bana ba balume ba Mishema: Hamuele muanʼandi, Zakû muanʼandi, Shimei muanʼandi.
27 Shimei wakadi ne bana ba balume dikumi ne basambombo ne bana ba bakashi basambombo; kadi bana babo kabakadi ne bana ba bungi, ne bobo bonso kabakavulangana ba bungi bu muakenza bana ba Yuda.
28 Bobo bakashikama mu Bê-sheba ne mu Molada ne mu Hasâ-shuala
29 ne mu Bileha ne mu Eseme ne mu Tolada
30 ne mu Betuele ne Hôma ne mu Sikelaga,
31 ne mu Bete-makabota ne mu Hasâsu-sima ne mu Bete-bili ne mu Shâla-yima. Ebi biakadi bimenga biabo tô ne ku bukokeshi bua Davidi.
32 Misoko yabo yakadi Etama ne Ayina, Limona ne Tokene ne Ashana, misoko itanu;
33 ne misoko yabo yonso yakadi ha buihi ne bimenga biobimue tô ne ku Bâla. Ei yakadi miaba yabo yakashikamabo, ne bobo bakafunda kulondolola kuabo.
34 Meshobaba ne Yameleke ne Yoasha, muana wa balume wa Amasaya,
35 ne Yoele ne Yehu, muana wa balume wa Yoshibeya, muana wa balume wa Selaya, muana wa balume wa Asiele,
36 ne Eleyehoenai ne Yâkoba ne Yeshohaya ne Asaya ne Adiele ne Yesimiele ne Benaya
37 ne Ziza, muana wa balume wa Shifi, muana wa balume wa Alono, muana wa balume wa Yedaya, muana wa balume wa Shimeli, muana wa balume wa Shemaya;
38 bantu aba batuakutela menʼabo nunku bakadi bakelenge ba mu meku abo ne ba mu nsubu yabo bakatamba kuyisangana.
39 Bakamuangalaka tô ne ku tshibuelelu tshia Gedô ku luseke lua ku esete kua tshibanda bua kukebabo mashinde a kudisha nʼau bimuna biabo.
40 Bakasangana mashinde a bungi ne mimpe, buloba abu buakadi bunene, buakadi kabuyi ne diyoyo, buakadi talalâ; bantu bakadi bashikamaku diambedi bakadi bantu ba Hama.
41 Badi ne mena abo mafunda nunku bakalua mu matuku a Hezikiya, mukelenge wa Yuda, bakashiha ntenta yabo ne Bena Meunima bakadi bashikamaku, bakababutula tô ne ku dituku edi, bakashikama mu miaba yabo; bualu bua bakasanganamu mashinde a kudisha nʼau bimuna biabo.
42 Bakuabo babo, bana ba balume ba Shimeona mene bu nkama itanu, bakaya ku mukuna wa Seî, bakadi ne Pelatiya ne Nealiya ne Lefaya ne Uziele, bana ba balume ba Ishi, bu tupitene tuabo.
43 Bakashiha difuka dia Bena Amaleka bakahanduka diambedi, badi bashikamaku tô ne ku dituku edi.

1 Chronicles 5
1 Bana ba balume ba Lubena, muanʼa bute wa Isalele (yeye wakadi muanʼa bute; kadi bualu bua yeye wakanyanga bulalu bua tatu wandi, bakaha bana ba balume ba Yosefe, muana wa Isalele, bintu biandi bia buanʼa bute; kulondolola kuandi kakuena bu kua buanʼa bute.
2 Bualu bua Yuda wakatamba bana babo bukole ne mukelenge wakafuma kudiye; kadi bintu bia buanʼa bute biakadi bia Yosefe).
3 Bana ba balume ba Lubena, muanʼa bute wa Isalele: Hanoka ne Palu, Heselona ne Kâmi.
4 Bana ba balume ba Yoele: Shemaya muanʼandi, ne Gogo muanʼandi, ne Shimei muanʼandi,
5 Mika muanʼandi, ne Leaya muanʼandi, ne Bâla muanʼandi,
6 ne Bela muanʼandi; Tigelata-pilenesê wakaya nʼandi mu buhika; yeye wakadi mukelenge wa Bena Lubena.
7 Bana babo ba mu meku abo, hakabalabo kulondolola kuabo, bakadi tulala Yiele ne Zekâya,
8 ne Bêla, muana wa balume wa Azaza, muana wa balume wa Sheme, muana wa balume wa Yoele, wakashikama mu Aloe tô ne ku Nebo ne Bâla-meona;
9 ne ku esete bakamuangalaka tô ne ku tshibuelelu tshia tshihela ku musulu wa Pelata, bualu bua bimuna biabo biakavulangana mu buloba bua Gilada.
10 Mu matuku a Shaula bobo bakaluangana ne Bena Hageli, bakabashiha; bakashikama mu ntenta yabo mu buloba buonso ku esete kua Gilada.
11 Bana ba balume ba Gada bakashikama ha buihi nʼabo mu buloba bua Bashana ku Saleka;
12 Yoele wakadi ha mutu habo ne Shafama wakamulonda, ne Yanai ne Shafata mu Bashana.
13 Bana babo ba mu nsubu wa batatuʼabo: Mikaele ne Meshulama ne Sheba ne Yolai ne Yakana ne Zia ne Ebê, muanda mutekete bonso.
14 Aba bakadi bana ba balume ba Abihayila, muana wa balume wa Huli, muana wa balume wa Yaloa, muana wa balume wa Gilada, muana wa balume wa Mikaele, muana wa balume wa Yeshishai, muana wa balume wa Yado, muana wa balume wa Buza;
15 Ahi, muana wa balume wa Abediele, muana wa balume wa Guni, wakadi ha mutu ha nsubu ya batatuʼabo.
16 Bakashikama mu Gilada mu Bashana ne mu misoko yai ne mu miaba yonso ya ku matshika a Shalona tô ne ku mpenge yabo.
17 Buonso buabo bakabadibua mu kulondolola kuabo mu matuku a Yotama, mukelenge wa Yuda, ne mu matuku a Yalobama, mukelenge wa Isalele.
18 Bana ba balume ba Lubena ne ba Bena Gada ne ba tshituha tshia tshisamba tshia Manashe, bantu ba bukitu, bantu bakadi ne bukole bua kutuala nʼabu ngabu ne muele wa nvita, ne bua kuela nʼabu buta, ne bakadi ne dikima dia nvita, bakadi binunu makumi anai ne binai ne nkama muanda mutekete ne makumi asambombo, bakadi bena nvita.
19 Bobo bakaluangana ne Bena Hageli ne Yetû ne Nafisha ne Nodaba.
20 Bakuabo bakakuatshishangana nʼabo, ne bobo ne bakadi nʼabo bakahita Bena Hageli bukole; bualu bua bobo bakadila kudi Nzambi mu nvita, ne yeye wakabunvuila, bualu bua bobo bakamueyemena.
21 Bobo bakanyenga bimuna biabo; tumelo tuabo bu binunu makumi atanu, ne mikoko bu binunu nkama ibidi ne makumi atanu, ne nyama ya mpunda binunu bibidi, ne bantu binunu lukama.
22 Ba bungi bakashihibua bualu bua nvita ei yakadi ya Nzambi. Bobo bakashikama mu miaba yabo tô ne hakayabo mu buhika.

1 Chronicles 10
1 Bena Peleshete bakaluangana nvita ne Bena Isalele; Bena Isalele bakanyema ku mpala kua Bena Peleshete, bakadishinda hanshi bafue ku mukuna wa Gileboa.

4 Hashishe Shaula wakambila muambudi wa ngabu wandi ne, Hatula muele wa nvita webe, untahe, unsompole; bua bantu aba badi kabayi batengula kabalu ha buihi nʼanyi bua kutekabo. Kadi muambudi wa ngabu wandi wakahidia; bualu bua yeye wakatamba kutshina. Nunku Shaula wakahatula muele wa nvita wandi, wakadishindahu.
5 Hakamona muambudi wa ngabu wandi ne, Shaula wakamana kufua, yeye kabidi wakadishinda ha muele wandi, wakafua.
6 Nunku Shaula wakafua ne bana bandi balume basatu; bonso ba mu nsubu wandi bakafua diatshimue.
7 Hakatangila Bena Isalele bakadi bashikama mu tshibanda ne, Bakadi ku mukuna bakanyema, ne Shaula ne bana bandi balume bakafua, bakahatuka mu misoko yabo, bakanyema kabidi; Bena Peleshete bakatua, bakashikamamu.
8 Hakatshiabu, hakalua Bena Peleshete bua kunyengabo bintu bia bantu bafue, bakasangana Shaula ne bana bandi ba balume bahone hanshi ku mukuna wa Gileboa.
9 Bakamunyenga bintu biandi; bakatuma mutu wandi ne bintu biandi bia nvita mu buloba buonso bua Bena Peleshete bua kumanyishabo nzambi yabo ne bantu babo bonso lumu elu.
10 Bakateka bintu biandi bia nvita mu nsubu wa nzambi yabo; bakakumina mutu wandi mu nsubu wa Dagona.

12 hashishe bena bukitu bonso bakabika, bakangata tshitalu tshia Shaula ne bia bana bandi ba balume, bakatua nʼabi ku Yabesha, bakajika mifuba yabo muinshi mua mitshi ya telebinta ya mu Yabesha, bakajila bia kudia matuku muanda mutekete.
13 Nunku Shaula wakafua bualu bua bubi buandi buakenzeleye Yehowa ne bua dî dia Yehowa diakahidieye; ne bualu bua yeye wakakeba lungenyi kudi muena buloshi,

1 Chronicles 12
1 Aba mbantu bakatua kudi Davidi ku Sikelaga hakadiye musokome bualu bua Shaula, muana wa Kishi; bakadi munkatshi mua bena bukitu bandi, bakadi bakuatshisha bandi mu nvita.

3 Ahiezê wakadi kapitene kabo; hashishe Yoasha, ne bana balume ba Shemâ, Muena Gibea, ne Yeziele ne Pelete, bana balume ba Asemaweta, ne Belaka ne Yehu, Muena Anatota,

10 Mishemana muinai, ne Yelemiya muitanu,
11 ne Atai muisambombo, ne Eliele wa muanda mutekete,
12 ne Yohanana wa muanda mukulu, ne Elezabada wa tshitema,

16 Bantu ba Benyamina ne ba Yuda bakalua ku musoko wa lumbu wa Davidi.

21 Bobo bakahitshisha Davidi kudi bisumbu bia bena tshinyangu; bualu bua buonso buabo bakadi bena bukitu, bakadi tupitene tua masalayi.
22 Bantu bakalua kudi Davidi ku difuku ku dituku bua kunukuatshisha; bakalua tshisumbu tshinene tshia masalayi bu misumba ya Nzambi.
23 Ebu mbungi bua bakadi ku mutu kua bena nvita bakalua kudi Davidi mu Hebelone bua kukudimunabo bukelenge bua Shaula kudiye bu muakadi dî dia Yehowa.
24 Ba munkatshi mua Bena Yuda bakadi bambila ngabu ne mafuma bakadi bena nvita binunu bisambombo ne nkama muanda mukulu.
25 Ba munkatshi mua Bena Shimeona bena nvita ba bukitu bakadi binunu muanda mutekete ne lukama.
26 Ba munkatshi mua Bena Lewi binunu binai ne nkama isambombo.

39 Bakashala ne Davidi matuku asatu, bakadia ne bakanua nʼandi; bualu bua bana babo bakamana kubalongoluela bia kudia.

1 Chronicles 13
1 Davidi wakahunga dî ne tupitene tua bantu tshinunu ne tua bantu lukama ne muntu yonso wakadi ku mutu kua bantu bakuabo.
2 Davidi wakambila Bena Isalele bonso ne, Binuelangana meji ne mbimpe, ne biasua Yehowa Nzambi wetu nunku, tubikile bana betu bonso badi bashala mu nseke yonso ya buloba bua Isalele ne bakuidi ne Bena Lewi badi mu misoko yabo ne ku mpenge ya misoko yabo, badisangishe kutudi;
3 tuye bietu, tuangate mushete wa Nzambi wetu, tulue nʼau munkatshi muetu; bualu bua tuetu katuakûkeba mu matuku a Shaula.
4 Bantu bonso bakamba ne, Netuenze nunku; bua bualu ebu buakadi buimpe ku mesu kuabo.
5 Nunku Davidi wakabikila Bena Isalele bonso hamue ku Shihô, kasulu kakese ka ku luseke lua Ejipitu tô ne ku tshibuelelu tshia Hamata, bua kuangatabo mushete wa Nzambi ku Kîyata-yalima.
6 Davidi ne Bena Isalele bakaya ku Bâla, udi Kîyata-yalima mene, ku luseke lua Yuda, bua kuangatabo mushete wa Nzambi, udi mushikame ku mutu kua bakeluba kudibo babikila Dina dia Yehowa.

8 Davidi ne Bena Isalele bonso bakimba misambu ne bisanji ne nzenze ne nkumbi ne makasa ne mpungi ne bukole buabo buonso.
9 Hakafikabo ku tshihehuidi tshia Kidona, Uza wakolola tshianza tshiandi bua kukuateye mushete; bualu bua ngombe yakadishinda hanshi.
10 Tshiji tshia Yehowa tshiakatema kudiye, yeye wakamushiha bualu bua yeye wakolola tshianza tshiandi bua kulengeye mushete; yeye wakafuilaku ku mpala kua Yehowa.
11 Davidi wakafua tshiji bualu bua Yehowa wakenzela Uza nunku; yeye wakabikila muaba au ne, Pelese-uza tô ne ku dituku edi.
12 Davidi wakatshina Nzambi dituku adi, wakamba ne, Nenye ne mushete wa Nzambi kuanyi bishi?
13 Nunku Davidi kakaya ne mushete wa Nzambi ku musoko wa Davidi, kadi wakasesuka, wakaya nʼau ku nsubu wa Obeda-edome, Muena Giti.
14 Mushete wakashala kudi ba mu nsubu wa Obeda-edome ngondo isatu; Yehowa wakavudishila ba mu nsubu wa Obeda-edome ne bintu biabo bionso disanka.

1 Chronicles 17
1 Hakadi Davidi mushikame mu nsubu muandi, Davidi wakambila Natana, muprofete ne, Mona, meme ndi mushikame mu nsubu wa mitshi ya cèdre, kadi mushete wa tshihungidi tshia mei a Yehowa udi mushale mu ntenta.
2 Natana wakambila Davidi ne, Wenze malu onso adi mu mutshima webe; bualu bua Nzambi udi nʼebe.
3 Butuku abu mene dî dia Nzambi diakalua kudi Natana diamba ne,
4 Ya, Wambile Davidi muntu wanyi ne, Yehowa udi Wamba ne, Wewe kuena ungibakile nsubu bua meme kushikamamu nansha;
5 bualu bua meme tshiena muanji kushikama mu nsubu katshia nahatuka ne Bena Isalele tô ne ku dituku edi, kadi nakendakana ku ntenta ne ntenta ne ku tshisombelu ne tshisombelu.
6 Mu miaba yonso inakendakana ne Bena Isalele bonso nakambila umue wa balumbuluishi bonso ba Bena Isalele unaka mbila bua kulameye bantu banyi ne, Wewe kuakungibakila nsubu wa cèdre bualu kai, anyi?
7 Nunku newambile muntu wanyi Davidi ne, Yehowa wa mu diulu udi wamba ne, Nakakubikila mu mpangu ya mikoko hawakadi ulama mikoko, nakakuvuisha mukelenge ku mutu kua bantu banyi Bena Isalele;
8 nakadi nʼebe kuonso kuwakaya, nakashiha bena lukuna bebe bonso ku mpala kuebe; nenkuvuishe dina dinene bu mena a badi ne butumbi hanshi ha buloba.
9 Nensunguile bantu banyi Bena Isalele muaba, nembajadike bimpe, bua bobo bashikame mu muaba wabo, kabena bumushibue kabidi; bena tshimbambila kabena babahaula kabidi bu muakenzabo diambedi;
10 bu munakenza ku dituku dinakela mukenji wanyi bua balumbuluishi bikale ku mutu kua bantu banyi Bena Isalele, ne nempite bantu bonso bakadi nʼebe lukuna bukole. Ndi nkuambila kabidi ne, Yehowa neakuibakile nsubu.
11 Hakumbana matuku ebe bua wewe kuya kudi bankambua bebe, nenjadike umue wa bana bebe balume badi bakulonda ku nyima; nenjadike bukelenge buandi.
12 Yeye neangibakile nsubu, nenjadike nkuasa wandi wa bukelenge tshiendelele.
13 Meme nengikale tatu wandi, yeye neikale muana wanyi; tshiena ngumusha luse luanyi lujalame kudiye bu munakalûmusha kudi wakadi ku mpala kuebe;
14 luidi nemmujadike mu nsubu muanyi ne mu bukelenge buanyi tshiendelele; nkuasa wandi wa bukelenge neajadikibue tshiendelele.
15 Nunku Natana wakambila Davidi mei â onso, wakamuawila tshikena kumona etshi tshionso.
16 Hashishe mukelenge Davidi wakabuela mu nsubu, wakashikama ku mpala kua Yehowa, wakamba ne, Yehowa Nzambi, meme nganyi ne ba mu nsubu muanyi mbanganyi bua wewe wakulua nʼanyi tô ne ku dituku edi?
17 Nzambi, bualu ebu buakadi bukese ku mpala kuebe; kadi wewe wakuamba bua nsubu wa mundi webe musangu mule, wewe wakuntumbisha bu muntu munene, wewe Yehowa Nzambi.
18 Davidi udi mumanye mua kukuambila mei kai makuabo bua butumbi buwakuvuisha muntu webe? Bualu bua wewe udi mumanye muntu webe.
19 Yehowa, bua muntu webe, ne bua malu adi mu mutshima webe, wewe wakuenza malu â onso manene bua kumumanyisha malu â onso.
20 Yehowa, kakuena mukuabo bu wewe, kakuena Nzambi mukuabo ku mpala kuebe, bu mudi malu onso atuakunvua ku matshu etu.
21 Tshisamba kai tshikuabo tshia hanshi ha buloba tshidi bu bantu bebe Bena Isalele, tshiakaya Nzambi kudihikula bu bantu bandi mene, bua kubavuisha dina dinene ku malu manene ne a kukema hawakihata bisamba bikuabo ku mpala kua bantu bebe bawakahikula mu Ejipitu?
22 Bualu bua wewe wakasungula bantu bebe Bena Isalele bua kubavuisha bantu bebe tshiendelele; ne wewe, Yehowa, wakalua Nzambi wabo.
23 Yehowa, dî diwakamba bua mundi webe ne bua nsubu wandi, dijadikibue tshiendelele; wenze bu muwakuamba.
24 Dina diebe dijadikibue, ditumbishibue tshiendelele, ne, Yehowa wa misumba udi Nzambi wa Isalele, Nzambi mene kudi Bena Isalele; ne nsubu wa Davidi muntu webe udi mujadikibue ku mpala kuebe tshiendelele.
25 Bualu bua wewe, Nzambi wanyi, wakumanyisha muntu webe ne, Nenkuibakile nsubu; nunku muntu webe wakadi ne dianyisha bua kutendeleleye ku mpala kuebe.
26 Katataka, Yehowa, wewe udi Nzambi ne wakalaya muntu webe bualu ebu buimpe;
27 katataka uvudishile nsubu wa muntu webe disanka, bua kushaleye ku mpala kuebe tshiendelele; bualu bua tshiudi uvudishile disanka, Yehowa, netshikale ne disanka tshiendelele.

1 Chronicles 18
1 Ku nyima kua malu â Davidi wakaluangana ne Bena Peleshete, wakabahita bukole, wakanyenga Gata ne misoko yakadi ha buihi mu bianza bia Bena Peleshete.

3 Davidi wakahita Hadadezê, mukelenge wa Zoba, bukole tô ne ku Hamata, hakayeye bua kujadika bukelenge buandi kukala kua musulu wa Pelata.
4 Davidi wakamunyenga tshinunu tshia makalu ne bena nvita binunu muanda mutekete bakadi benda ha nyima ha tubalu ne bena nvita binunu makumi abidi bakadi benda hanshi; Davidi wakakosa tubalu tuabo tua makalu mijilu yatu ya bikono, kadi wakasungula tukuabo tuakakumbana ku makalu andi lukama.
5 Hakafuma Bena Sulia ku Damaseke bua kukuatshishabo Hadadezê, mukelenge wa Zoba, Davidi wakashiha Bena Sulia binunu makumi abidi ne bibidi.
6 Hashishe Davidi wakateka masalayi andi mu Damaseke wa mu Sulia; Bena Sulia bakatua bahika ba Davidi, bakafila milambu kudiye. Yehowa wakaha Davidi bakole bua kutamba bena lukuna bandi kuonso kuakayeye.

8 Davidi wakanyenga biamu bia mitaku bia bungi ku Tibehata ne ku Kunu, misoko ya Hadadezê; Solomo wakatula nʼabi tshiowelu tshia tshiamu tshia mitaku ne makunshi ne mpanza ya tshiamu tshia mitaku.
9 Hakunvua Tou, mukelenge wa Hamata, ne, Davidi wakashiha masalayi onso a Hadadezê, mukelenge wa Zoba,
10 yeye wakatuma Hadolama muanʼandi mulume kudi mukelenge Davidi bua kumuela muoyo, ne bua kumuvudishileye disanka bualu bua yeye wakadi muluangane ne Hadadezê, wakamuhita bukole; bualu bua Hadadezê wakadi uluangana ne Tou; yeye wakalua ne mpanza ya mishindu yonso ya or ne ya argent ne ya tshiamu tshia mitaku.

12 Bualu bukuabo kabidi, Abishai, muana wa Seluya, wakashiha Bena Edome binunu dikumi ne muanda mukulu mu Tshibanda tshia Luehu.
13 Wakateka masalayi mu misoko ya Edome; Bena Edome bonso bakalua bahika ba Davidi. Yehowa wakaha Davidi bukole bua kutamba bena lukuna bandi kuonso kuakayeye.
14 Davidi wakakokesha Bena Isalele bonso mu kulumbulula kuimpe ne mu buakane kudi bantu bandi bonso.
15 Yoaba, muana wa Seluya, wakadi ku mutu kua masalayi andi onso; Yehoshafata, muana wa Ahiluda, wakadi mufundi wa malu a tshisamba;
16 Sadoka, muana wa Ahituba, ne Abimeleke, muana wa Ebeyatâ, bakadi bakuidi; Shawesha wakadi secrétaire;
17 Benaya, muana wa Yehoyada, wakadi ku mutu kua Bena Kelete ne Bena Pelete; bana balume ba Davidi bakadi tuhita ha buihi ne mukelenge.

1 Mudimu wa balami ba bibi: ba Bena Kola, Meshelemeya, muana wa Kole, wa bana ba Asafa.
2 Meshelemeya wakalela bana balume: Zekâya muanʼa bute, Yediele muibidi, Zebadaya muisatu, Yatamele muinai,
3 Elama muitanu, Yehohanana muisambombo, Eleyahoenai wa muanda mutekete.
4 Obeda-edome wakalela bana balume: Shemaya muanda bute, Yehozabada muibidi, Yoa muisatu, Sakâ muinai, Netanele muitanu,
5 Amiele muisambombo, Yisakâ wa muanda mutekete, Peuletai wa muanda mukulu; bualu bua Nzambi wakamusankisha.
6 Shemaya muanʼandi wa balume wakalela bana ba balume kabidi bakakokesha mu nsubu wa tatu wabo bualu bua bobo bakadi bantu ba bukitu.
7 Bana ba Shemaya: Oteni ne Lefaele ne Obeda ne Elezabada, bana babo Elihu ne Semakaya bakadi bena bukitu.
8 Aba bonso bakadi bana ba Obeda-edome; bobo ne bana babo balela ne bana babo bakadi ne bukole bua kuenza nʼabu mudimu; ba Obeda-edome bakadi makumi asambombo ne bantu bahidi.
9 Meshelemeya wakadi ne bana balume ne bana babo, bena bukitu dikumi ne muanda mukulu.
10 Hosa wa bana balume ba Melali wakadi ne bana balume: Shimeli wakadi kahita (yeye kakadi muanʼa bute kadi tatu wandi wakamuhingisha kahita),
11 Hilekiya muibidi, Tebalaya muisatu, Zekâya muinai; bana balume bonso ba Hosa ne bana babo bakadi dikumi ne basatu.
12 Munkatshi mua bantu aba bakasungula balami ba bibi, tuhita mene tuakadi ne mudimu watu bu bana babo mu nsubu wa Yehowa.
13 Buonso buabo ne banene ne bakese bakela nshobo bu muakadi nsubu ya batatuʼabo, ku tshibi ne ku tshibi.
14 Nshobo ya ku esete yakakuata Shelemeya. Bakelela muanʼandi Zekâya, muena meji makane, nshobo; nshobo yandi yakakuata ku nôde.
15 Yakakuata Obeda-edome ku sude; ne bana bandi ku tshiluminu.
16 Yakakuata Shupima ne Hosa ku wesete, ku tshibi tshia Shalekete ku tshibandilu tshidi tshibandaku, muntu ne muntu mulamate ku kutentekela kuandi.
17 Bena Lewi basambombo bakadi ku esete; banai ku node ku dituku ku dituku; banai ku Sude ku dituku ku dituku, ne babidi babidi ku tshilaminu.
18 Ku Pâbâ ku wesete banai ku tshibandilu, ne babidi ku Pâbâ.
19 Ei yakadi milongo ya balami ba bibi, ba bana balume ba Bena Kola ne ba Melali.
20 Ahiya wa Bena Lewi wakadi mulami wa bibutshilu bia mu nsubu wa Nzambi ne wa bibutshilu bia bintu bia tshijila.
21 Bana ba Lâdana, bana ba Bena Gêshona ba Lâdana, bakadi ku mutu kua nsubu ya batatuʼabo ba Lâdana, Muena Gêshona: Yehieli.
22 Bana ba Yehieli: Zetama ne Yoele muanʼabo bakadi balami ba bibutshilu bia mu nsubu wa Yehowa.
23 Ba Bena Amalama ne ba Bena Yisehâ ne ba Bena Hebelone ne ba Bena Uziele:
24 Shebuele, muana wa Gêshoma, muana wa Mose, wakadi kahita ka balami ba bibutshilu.
25 Bana babo: Eliezê ne muanʼandi Lehabeya ne Yeshaya muanʼandi ne Yolama muanʼandi ne Zikeli muanʼandi ne Shelomota muanʼandi.
26 Shelomota eu ne bana babo bakadi ku mutu kua bibutshilu bia bintu bionso bia tshijila biakajidilabo kudi Davidi mukelenge ne kudi ba ku mutu kua nsubu ya hatatu babo, ne kudi tupitene tua bantu tshinunu ne tua bantu lukama ne kudi tupitene tua masalayi.
27 Bakajidila bia mu bintu biakanyengabo mu nvita bua kulongololabo nʼabi nsubu wa Yehowa.
28 Bintu bionso biakajidila Samuele, mumonyi wa malu a Nzambi, ne Shaula, muana wa Kishi, ne Abinê, muana wa Nê, ne Yoaba, muana wa Seluya, ne muntu yonso wakajidila bintu, biobi biakadi mu bianza bia Shelomota ne bia bana babo.
29 Ba Bena Yisehâ; Kenanya ne bana bandi balume bakadi ne mudimu wabo ha mbelu ha ntempelo mu Isalele bu bakokeshi ne balumbuluishi.
30 Ba Bena Hebelone: Hashabeya ne bana babo, bena bukitu tshinunu ne nkama muanda mutekete, bakadi ku mutu kua Bena Isalele dishiya dia Yadene ku wesete mu malu onso a Yehowa ne a mudimu wa mukelenge.
31 Wa Bena Hebelone: Yeliya wakadi kahita ka Bena Hebelone mene bu muakadi bikondo bia batatuʼabo. Bakabakeba mu tshidimu tshia makumi anai tshia mu bukelenge bua Davidi, bakasangana munkatshi muabo bena bukitu ku Yazê wa mu Gilada.
32 Bana babo, bena bukitu bakadi binunu bibidi ne nkama muanda mutekete, bakadi ku mutu kua nsubu ya batatuʼabo, bakasungudibua kudi Davidi bua kuikalabo balongolodi ba Bena Lubena ne Bena Gada ne ba tshituha tshia tshisamba tshia Bena Manashe mu malu onso a Nzambi ne a mukelenge kabidi.

1 Chronicles 27
1 Bena Isalele mu bungi buabo, bakadi ku mutu kua nsubu ya batatuʼabo, ne tupitene tua bantu tshinunu ne tua bantu lukama, ne bakokeshi bakadi bakuatshila mukelenge wabo mudimu mu milongo yonso ya mu ngondo ne ngondo ya mu ngondo yonso ya mu tshidimu, mu tshisumbu ne tshisumbu muakadi bantu binunu makumi abidi ne binai.
2 Yashobama, muana wa Zabediele, wakadi ku mutu kua tshisumbu tshia ngondo wa kumudilu; mu tshisumbu tshiandi muakadi bantu binunu makumi abidi ne binai.
3 Yeye wakadi umue wa bana ba Pelese, wakadi ku mutu kua tupitene tuonso tua mu ngondo wa kumudilu.
4 Dodai, Muena Ahoa, wakadi ku mutu kua tshisumbu tshia mu ngondo muibidi; Mikelota wakadi ku mutu kuabo; mu tshisumbu tshiandi muakadi bantu binunu makumi abidi ne binai.
5 Benaya, muana wa Yehoyada muakuidi, wakadi ku mutu kua tshisumbu tshia mu ngondo muisatu; mu tshisumbu tshiandi muakadi bantu binunu makumi abidi ne binai.
6 Benaya eu wakadi umue wa bantu aba bakole makumi asatu, wakadi ku mutu kuabo mene; mu tshisumbu tshiandi muakadi muanʼandi Amizabada.
7 Asahele, muanʼabo ne Yoaba, wakadi kapitene ka mu ngondo muinai; Zebadaya muanʼandi wakamulonda ku nyima; mu tshisumbu tshiandi muakadi bantu binunu makumi abidi ne binai.
8 Shamehuta, Muena Yisela, wakadi kapitene ka tshisumbu tshia mu ngondo muitanu; mu tshisumbu tshiandi muakadi bantu binunu makumi abidi ne binai.
9 Ila, muana wa Ikeshe, Muena Tekoa, wakadi kapitene ka tshisumbu tshia mu ngondo muisambombo; mu tshisumbu tshiandi muakadi bantu binunu makumi abidi ne binai.
10 Helese, Muena Pelona wa bana ba Efelayima, wakadi kapitene ka tshisumbu tshia mu ngondo wa muanda mutekete; mu tshisumbu tshiandi muakadi bantu binunu makumi abidi ne binai.
11 Sibekai, Muena Husha wa Bena Zela, wakadi kapitene ka tshisumbu tshia mu ngondo wa muanda mukulu; mu tshisumbu tshiandi muakadi bantu binunu makumi abidi ne binai.
12 Abiezê, Muena Anatota wa Bena Benyamina, wakadi kapitene ka tshisumbu tshia mu ngondo wa tshitema; mu tshisumbu tshiandi muakadi bantu binunu makumi abidi ne binai.
13 Mehalai, Muena Netofa wa Bena Zela, wakadi kapitene ka tshisumbu tshia mu ngondo wa dikumi; mu tshisumbu tshiandi muakadi bantu binunu makumi abidi ne binai.
14 Benaya, Muena Pilatona wa Bena Efelayima, wakadi kapitene ka mu tshisumbu tshia mu ngondo wa dikumi ne umue; mu tshisumbu tshiandi muakadi bantu binunu makumi abidi ne binai.
15 Heledai, Muena Netofa wa Oteniele, wakadi kapitene ka tshisumbu tshia mu ngondo wa dikumi ne muibidi; mu tshisumbu tshiandi muakadi bantu binunu makumi abidi ne binai.
16 Tupitene tua bisamba bia Bena Isalele: Eliezê, muana wa Zikeli, wakadi ku mutu kua Bena Lubena; Shefataya, muana wa Mâka, wakadi ku mutu kua Bena Shimeona;
17 Hashabeya, muana wa Kemuele, wakadi ku mutu kua Bena Lewi; Sadoka wakadi ku mutu kua bana ba Alona;
18 Elihu, muanʼabo ne Davidi, wakadi ku mutu kua Bena Yuda; Omeli, muana wa Mikaele, wakadi ku mutu kua Bena Yisakâ;
19 Yishemaya, muana wa Obadaya, wakadi ku mutu kua Bena Zebuluna; Yelemota, muana wa Azazele, wakadi ku mutu kua Bena Nafatale;
20 Hoshea, muana wa Azazaya, wakadi ku mutu kua Bena Efelayima; Yoele, muana wa Pedaya, wakadi ku mutu kua tshituha tshia Bena Manashe;
21 Ido, muana wa Zekâya, wakadi ku mutu kua tshituha tshia Bena Manashe ba mu Gilada; Yâsiele, muana wa Abinê, wakadi ku mutu kua Bena Benyamina;
22 Azalele, muana wa Yelohama, wakadi ku mutu kua Bena Dana. Etu ntupitene tua bisamba bia Bena Isalele.
23 Kadi Davidi kakabala bantu bakadi kabayi bakumbane bidimu makumi abidi; bualu bua Yehowa wakabalaya ne, Nenkumbashe Bena Isalele bu mitoto ya mu diulu.
24 Yoaba, muana wa Seluya, wakabanga kutubala, kadi yeye kakashikisha mudimu eu; tshiji tshia Yehowa tshiakahatukila Bena Isalele bua bualu ebu; kabakafunda bungi buabo mu mukanda wa kulondolola kua malu a mukelenge Davidi.
25 Azemaweta, muana wa Adiele, wakadi ku mutu kua bibutshilu bia mukelenge; Yonatana, muana wa Uziya, wakadi ku mutu kua bibutshilu bia mu madimi ne bia mu bimenga ne bia mu misoko mikese ne bia mu nsubu ya mukelenge;
26 Ezeli, muana wa Keluba, wakadi ku mutu kua mudimu wonso wa madimi;
27 Shimei, Muena Lamata, wakadi ku mutu kua madimi a tumuma tukese; Zabedi, Muena Shefemi, wakadi ku mutu kua bintu biakakumbana mu madimi a tumuma tukese biakatekabo mu bitekelu bia nvinyo;
28 Bâla-hanana, Muena Gedê, wakadi ku mutu kua mitshi ya diye ne ya sycomore ya mu bibanda; Yoasha wakadi ku mutu kua bitekelu bia manyi;
29 Shitelai, Muena Shalona, wakadi ku mutu kua bimuna biakadishabo mu Sifiona; Shafata, muana wa Adelai, wakadi ku mutu kua bimuna bia mu bibanda;
30 Obila, Muena Yishemaele, wakadi ku mutu kua tumelo; Yedeya, Muena Melonita, wakadi ku mutu kua nyama ya mpunda; Yaziza, Muena Hageli, wakadi ku mutu kua mikoko.
31 Aba bonso bakadi ku mutu kua bintu bia Davidi.
32 Yonatana, manseba wa Davidi, muntu wa lungenyi lule ne mufundi kabidi, wakadi mufidi wandi wa meji; Yehiele, muana wa Hakemoni, wakadi mukuatshishi wa bana ba mukelenge;
33 Ahitofele wakadi mufidi wa meji wa mukelenge; Hushai, Muena Eleke, wakadi mulunda wa mukelenge;
34 ku nyima kua Ahitofele kuakadi Yehoyada, muana wa Benaya, ne Ebeyatâ; Yoaba wakadi kapitene ka masalayi a mukelenge.

1 Chronicles 28
1 Davidi wakabikila bantu banene bonso ba mu Isalele, bakadi ku mutu kua bisamba, ne tupitene tua bisumbu bia bantu tuakakuatshila mukelenge mudimu mu milongo yatu, ne tupitene tua bantu tshinunu ne tua bantu lukama, ne bakadi ku mutu kua bintu bionso bia mukelenge ne bimuna biandi ne bia bana bandi ne tuhita ne bena bukitu mu Yelushalema.
2 Hashishe mukelenge Davidi wakajuka hanshi, wakimana ku makasa andi, wakamba ne, Unvuai, bana betu ne bantu banyi; meme nakadi ne bualu mu mutshima wanyi bua kuibakila mushete wa tshihungidi tshia mei a Yehowa nsubu wa kuikisha ne tshitekelu tshia muinshi mua makasa a Nzambi wetu; nakabanga kulongolola bintu bia kuasa nʼabi nsubu.
3 Kadi Nzambi wakangambila ne, Wewe kuena wibakila dina dianyi nsubu nansha, bualu bua wewe udi muena nvita, wakamatshisha mashi hanshi.
4 Kadi Yehowa, Nzambi wa Isalele, wakansungula munkatshi mua bonso ba mu nsubu wa tatu wanyi, wakampingisha mu bukelenge bua Isalele tshiendelele; bualu bua yeye wakasungula Yuda bua kuikaleye ku mutu; wakasungula tshiota tshia tatu wanyi mu tshisamba tshia Yuda; munkatshi mua bana ba tatu wanyi yeye wakalesha diakalengele kundi bua kumpingisha mu bukelenge bua Isalele;
5 ne munkatshi mua bana banyi balume (Nzambi wakumpa bana ba bungi) yeye wakasungula Solomo muanʼanyi bua kushikameye mu nkuasa wa bukelenge bua Yehowa ku mutu kua Bena Isalele.
6 Yeye wakangambila ne, Solomo muanʼebe neibake nsubu wanyi ne biendedi bianyi; bualu bua meme nakamusungula bua kuikaleye muanʼanyi, ne meme nengikale tatu wandi.
7 Nenjadike bukelenge buandi tshiendelele, biananukileye kutumikila mikenji yanyi ne mei anyi bu mudibi lelu.
8 Nunku ku mesu kua Bena Isalele bonso ba mu tshisumbu tshia Yehowa, ne mu matshu a Nzambi wetu, nuenze, nukebe mikenji yonso ya Yehowa Nzambi wetu; bua nuenu nuhiane buloba ebu buimpe, bua nuenu nubushile bana benu bu buhianyi buabo tshiendelele.
9 Wewe, muanʼanyi Solomo, Umanye Nzambi wa tatuʼebe, umukuatshile mudimu ne mutshima mutoke ne didikokela kudiye; bualu bua Yehowa udi ukebakeba mitshima ya bantu bonso; udi ujingulula meji onso adibo bela munda muabo; biwamukeba, neumuhete; kadi biwamulekela, yeye neakuhidie tshiendelele.
10 Udimuke biebe, bualu bua Yehowa wakukusungula bua wewe kuibaka nsubu udi mua kuikala muaba wandi wa tshijila; wikale ne bukole, wenze nsubu eu.
11 Hashishe Davidi wakaha Solomo muanʼandi luidi lua mbalanda wa ntempelo, ne lua nsubu wandi, ne lua bibutshilu biandi, ne lua nsubu ya ku mutu, ne lua nsubu ya munda muandi, ne lua muaba wa tshibuikilu tshia or;
12 ne luidi lua ngenyi yandi yonso ya biendedi bia mu nsubu wa Yehowa, ne ya nsubu yandi yonso, ne ya bibutshilu bia mu nsubu wa Yehowa, ne ya bitekelu bia bintu bia tshijila;
13 ne ya milongo ya bakuidi ne Bena Lewi, ne ya midimu yonso ya mu nsubu wa Yehowa, ne ya mpanza yonso ya mu nsubu ya Yehowa.
14 Wakidikisha bujitu bua mpanza ya or, ne bua mpanza ya mishindu yonso; ne bua mpanza ya argent, ne bua mpanza ya mishindu yonso.
15 Wakidikisha bujitu bua bitekelu bia minda bia or ne minda yabi kabidi, ne bua bitekelu bia minda bia argent wakidikisha bujitu bua tshitekelu ne tshitekelu ne bua muinda ne muinda.
16 Wakidikisha bujitu bua mesa a bidia bilesha, ku mesa ne mesa; ne bua argent ya mesa ne mesa;
17 ne bua or ya bitubuidi bia munyinyi ne malonga ne makoho, ne bujitu bua or ya bihanza bionso, ne bua bihanza bia argent kabidi.
18 Wakidikisha bujitu bua or ya tshioshelu tshia musenga wa manananshi ne or ya kuenza nʼai luidi lua dikalu dia bakeluba bakadi bolola mahuahua abo, bakabuikila mushete wa tshihungidi tshia mei a Yehowa.
19 Davidi wakamba ne, Nakamanya malu au onso ku tshifundidi tshia tshianza tshia Yehowa tô ne ku bintu bionso bia mu luidi elu.
20 Davidi wakambila Solomo muanʼandi ne, Wikale ne bukole ne mutshima wa dikima, wenze bualu ebu; kutshinyi, kuhangi bualu ebu; bualu bua Yehowa Nzambi, Nzambi wanyi mene, udi nʼebe; yeye kena ukuhanga, kena ukulekela tô ne hawashikisha mudimu eu wonso wa mu nsubu wa Yehowa.
21 Tangila milongo ya bakuidi ne ya Bena Lewi ya mu midimu yonso ya mu nsubu wa Nzambi; muntu yonso udi ne mutshima wa kuenza ne lungenyi lua kuenza midimu yonso neikale nʼebe; tupitene ne bantu bonso nebatumikile mikenji yebe yonso kabidi.

1 Chronicles 29
1 Davidi mukelenge wakambila tshisumbu tshia bantu ne, Solomo muanʼanyi, wakasungulabo kudi Nzambi nkayandi, utshidi nsongalume mutekete, ne mudimu eu udi munene; bualu bua mbalasa eu kena wa muntu, udi wa Yehowa Nzambi.
2 Meme nakamana kulongolola bintu bia mu nsubu wa Nzambi wanyi ne bukole buonso bundi nʼabu, or ya bintu bia tshiamu tshia or, ne argent ya bintu bia tshiamu tshia argent, ne tshiamu tshia mitaku tshia bintu bia tshiamu tshia mitaku, ne tshiamu tshifike tshia bintu bia tshiamu tshifike, ne mitshi ya bintu bia mitshi; ne mabue a onyx ne mabue adibo balengesha nʼau bintu ne mabue a mishindu yonso ne mabue onso a mushinga mukole ne mabue manene matoke
3 Bualu bukuabo kabidi, bualu bua meme nakateka mutshima wanyi kudi nsubu wa Nzambi wanyi, meme nakafila mu nsubu wa Nzambi wanyi bibutshilu bianyi bia or ne bia argent ku mutu kua bintu binakalongolola bua muaba eu wa tshijila;
4 bu ntalanta
5 ya or ya bintu bia or, ne ya argent ya bintu bia argent ya midimu yonso ya bantu badi ne lungenyi lule. Muntu kai neadijidile lelu eu kudi Yehowa?
6 Hashishe bakadi ku mutu kua nsubu ya batatuʼabo, ne bakadi ku mutu kua bisamba bia Bena Isalele, ne tupitene tua bantu tshinunu ne tua bantu lukama, ne bakadi ku mutu kua midimu ya mukelenge bakafila bintu ne disanka.
7 Bakafila bua mudimu wa mu nsubu wa Nzambi ntalanta ya or binunu bitanu ne dariques
8 Bakadi ne mabue a mushinga mukole, bakafila mu bibutshilu bia nsubu wa Yehowa mu tshianza tshia Yehiele, Muena Gêshona.
9 Hashishe bantu bakasanka bualu bua bobo bakadifila bintu biabo; bualu bua bakafila bintu biabo ne mitshima mitoke kudi Yehowa; mukelenge Davidi wakatamba kusanka kabidi.
10 Nunku Davidi wakavudishila Yehowa disanka ku mpala kua bantu bonso; Davidi wakamba ne, Yehowa, Nzambi wa Isalele tatu wetu, wikale ne disanka tshiendelele.
11 Yehowa, bunene ne bukole ne butumbi ne kutamba ne bulengele butambe mbiebe; bualu bua bintu bionso bia mu diulu ne bia ha buloba mbiebe; Yehowa, bukelenge mbuebe, ne wewe udi ubandishibua ku mutu kua bionso.
12 Bubanji ne butumbi bidi bifuma kûdi, wewe udi ukokesha bionso; bukole ne bunene bidi mu tshianza tshiebe; tshianza tshiebe tshidi tshivuisha bantu bunene ne bukole.
13 Nunku, Nzambi wetu, tudi tukusakidila, tudi tutumbisha dina diebe dia butumbi.
14 Kadi meme nganyi ne bantu banyi mbanganyi, bua tuetu tuakadifidila bintu bietu nunku? Bualu bua bintu bionso bidi bifuma kûdi, tuakafila anu bintu biebe kûdi.
15 Bualu bua tuetu tudi benyi ku mpala kuebe, tudi anu bu bahitshi ba mu buloba bu muakadi bankambua betu; matuku etu a ha buloba adi bu mundidimbi, katuena ne ditekemena dia kushalaku.
16 Yehowa Nzambi wetu, bintu ebi bionso bituakulongolola bua kukuibakila nʼabi nsubu wa dina diebe dia tshijila biakafuma mu tshianza tshiebe, bionso bidi biebe mene.
17 Nzambi wanyi, ndi mumanye kabidi ne wewe udi uteta mitshima ya bantu, udi usanka mu butoke bua mutshima. Meme nakudifidila bintu ebi bionso mu butoke bua mutshima wanyi; nakumona bantu bebe bonso badi kunoko badifidila bintu biabo ne disanka.
18 Yehowa, Nzambi wa Abalahama ne wa Isaka ne wa Yakoba, bankambua betu, witabushe bua bantu bebe bikale ne meji â onso munda muabo tshiendelele, ujadike mitshima yabo kûdi;
19 uvuishe Solomo muanʼanyi mutshima mutoke, bua kutumikileye mikenji yebe ne mei ebe ne mikandu yebe, bua kuenzeye malu â onso, ne bua kuibakeye mbalasa eu unakabanga kulongolola bintu.
20 Davidi wakambila bantu bonso ne, Tuvudishilai Yehowa Nzambi wetu disanka. Bantu bonso bakavu-dishila Yehowa, Nzambi wa bankambua babo, disanka, bakinama mitu yabo, bakatendelela Yehowa, bakakukuata mukelenge.
21 Bakafila milambu kudi Yehowa; bakafila milambu ya kuosha ha kahia kudi Yehowa, ne lunkelu kabidi bakafila tshinunu tshia balume ba ngombe ne tshinunu tshia bimpanga ne tshinunu tshia bana ba mikoko ne milambu idibo banua; Bena Isalele bonso bakafila milambu ya bungi;
22 bakadia, bakanua ku mpala kua Yehowa dituku adi ne disanka dinene.
23 Hashishe Solomo wakashikama mu nkuasa wa Yehowa bu mukelenge mu muaba wa Davidi tatu wandi, wakadi ne diakalengele; Bena Isalele bonso bakamutumikila.
24 Bonso bakadi ku mutu kua bantu, ne bena bukitu bonso, ne bana balume bonso ba mukelenge Davidi bakadikokela kudi mukelenge Solomo.
25 Yehowa wakatamba kudiundisha Solomo ku mpala kua Bena Isalele bonso, wakamuambika butumbi buakatamba bua bakelenge bonso ba mu Isalele bakadi badianjila ku mpala kuandi.
26 Davidi, muana wa Yishai, wakadi ukokesha mu Isalele.
27 Matuku onso akakokesheye mu Isalele akadi bidimu makumi anai; wakakokesha mu Hebelone bidimu muanda mutekete; wakakokesha mu Yelushalema bidimu makumi asatu ne bisatu.
28 Wakafua muntu mununu mukole, wakadi ne bubanji ne butumbi; Solomo muanʼandi wakakokesha mu muaba wandi.
29 Bienzedi bia mukelenge Davidi, bia kumudilu ne bia kunshikidilu, monai, bidi bifunda mu mei a Samuele, mumonyi wa malu a Nzambi, ne mu mei a Natana muprofete, ne mu mei a Gada, mumonyi wa malu a Nzambi, kabidi;
30 ne malu onso a mu bukelenge buandi ne bukole buandi hakadiye ukokesha mu Isalele ne ku mutu kua makelenge onso a mu miaba mikuabo.
