Exodus 1
1 Â mmena a bana balume ba Isalele bakalua mu Ejipitu, muntu ne muntu ne ba mu nsubu wandi bakalua ne Yakoba.
2 Lubena, Shimeona, Lewi ne Yuda,
3 Yisakâ, Zebuluna ne Benyamina,
4 Dana ne Nafatale, Gada ne Ashe
5 Bantu bonso ba munda mua Yakoba bakadi makumi muanda mutekete, Yosefe wakadi mu Ejipitu diambedi
6 Yosefe wakafua ne bana babo bonso, ne bantu bonso ba mulongo au
7 Bena Isalele bakakumbana ba bungi, bakavulangana, bakalua bakole be, bakula mu buloba tente.
8 Mukelenge muhia muhia wakahingana mu bukelenge bua Ejipitu, wakadi kayi mumanye Yosefe.
9 Yeye wakambila bantu bandi ne, Tangilai, Bena Isalele badi batuhita bungi ne bukole.
10 Tuye kuenza budimuke, kabalu ba bungi, halua nvita, kabayi kudi bantu badi nʼetu lukuna bua kuhingana nʼetu nvita, kabumuki mu buloba buetu.
11 Bakabatekela tuhita, bakabakengesha ne mudimu. Bakashala Palô misoko ya kutekelamu bintu biandi, mena ai Pitoma ne Lamesese.
12 Hakabakengeshabo nunku, Bena Isalele bakakumbana ba bungi, bakatangadika Mitshima ya Bena Ejipitu yakanyingalala bualu bua Bena Isalele.
13 Bena Ejipitu bakenzesha Bena Isalele mudimu bikole.
14 Bakabakengesha ne mudimu mukole, mudimu wa tushola ne buloba busoha ne midimu yonso ya mu madimi. Bakabenzesha mudimu mukole.
15 Mukelenge wa Ejipitu wakambila baleleshi ba bakashi ba Bena Ebelu, dina dia umue wabo ne, Shifela, ne dina dia mukuabo ne, Pua ne,
16 Hanuaya kuenzela bakashi ba Bena Ebelu mudimu wa baleleshi, ne hanuabatangila mu buledi, bialelabo muana wa balume, nuenu nenumushihe, kadi bialelabo muana wa bakashi, numushiye ne muoyo.
17 Kadi baleleshi bakatshina Nzambi, kabakenza muakabambila mukelenge wa Ejipitu, kadi bakasungila bana balume.
18 Mukelenge wa Ejipitu Wakabikila baleleshi, wakabambila ne, Bualu kai nuakuenza nunku, nuakusungila bana ba balume?
19 Baleleshi bakambila Palo ne, Bualu bua bakashi ba Bena Ebelu kabena bu bakashi ba Bena Ejipitu bobo badi ne bukole bua kudilelela, ne bakamana kulela diambedi baleleshi kabayi banze kulua kudibo.
20 Nzambi wakenzela baleleshi bimpe, bantu bakakumbana ba bungi, bakalua bakole be,
21 Bualu bua baleleshi bakatshina Nzambi, yeye wakabaha bana.
22 Palo wakelela bantu bandi bonso mukenji ne, Bialelabo muana mulume, nenumuele mu musulu, kadi nensungile bana bakashi.

Exodus 2
1 Muntu wa tshisamba tshia Lewi wakabaka mukashi, muana wa Lewi.
2 Mukashi wakimita difu, wakalela muana wa balume, hakamumoneye ne muana muimpe, wakamusokoka ngondo isatu.
3 Hakadiye kayi mua kumusokoka kabidi, wakamuangatshila tshisaka tshia ntuhu, wakatshilaba bitume
4 Muanʼabo mukashi wakimana kule, bualu bua kumanya muenzelabo muakunyʼandi.
5 Muana wa bakashi wa Palô wakalua kuowa mai mu musulu au, bantu bandi bakashi bakendakana ku muelelu wa mai, yeye wakamona tshisaka mu malenge, wakatuma muntu wandi ne, Ya utshiangate.
6 Yeye wakatshisulula, wakamona muana, ne muana wakadila Yeye wakafuka muana luse, wakamba ne, Yeye udi muana wa Bena Ebelu.
7 Mukuluʼandi, muana wa bakashi, wakambila muana wa bakashi wa Palô ne, Nye kukubikidila muamushi wa muana wa mu bakashi ba Bena Ebelu, alue kukuamushila muana mabele, anyi?
8 Muana wa bakashi wa Palo wakamuambila ne, Ya biebe Nsongakashi wakaya, Wakabikila mamu wa muana.
9 Muana wa bakashi wa Palô wakamuambila ne, Wangate muana eu, umungamushile mabele, nenkufute Mukashi wakangata muana, wakamuamusha mabele.
10 Muana wakakola, mamuʼandi wakalua nʼandi kudi muana wa bakashi wa Palo, yeye wakalua bu muana wandi mene. Yeye wakamuidika dina ne, Mose, wakamba ne, Bualu bua nakamukoka mu mai.
11 Mu matuku au, hakakola Mose, yeye wakaya kudi bana babo, wakamona mudimu wabo mukole, wakamona Muena Ejipitu ukuma Muena Ebelu, muanʼabo.
12 Wakakenzakana, kakamona muntu, wa kashiha Muena Ejipitu, wakamusokoka mu lusenga.
13 Wakaya biandi dituku dibidi, wakamona balume babidi ba Bena Ebelu baluangana, yeye wakakonka muntu. Wakenza bualu bubi ne, Udi ukumina mukuenu tshinyi?
14 Yeye wakamba ne, Wakukuteka mukelenge wetu ne mulumbuluishi wetu nganyi? Udi ukeba kunshiha bu muwakushiha Muena Ejipitu, anyi? Mose wakatshina, wakamba ne, Bulelela, bualu ebu buakumanyibua.
15 Kakamanya Palô bualu ebu, wakakeba kushiha Mose. Kadi Mose wakanyema ku mesu kua Palo, wakikala mu buloba bua Midiyana, wakashikama hehi ne dina dia mai.
16 Mubuki wa Midiyana wakadi ne bana ba bakashi muanda mute kete bobo bakaha kusuna mai, bakûsha luvu lua mai tente bua kunuisha mikoko ya tatuʼabo mai.
17 Balami ba mikoko bakalua, bakabihata, kadi Mose wakabika, wakabasankisha, wakanuisha mikoko yabo mai.
18 Hakaluabo kudi Luele, tatuʼabo, yeye wakamba ne, Nuakuhingana lubilu lelu bualu kai?
19 Bakamba ne, Muena Ejipitu wakatusungila mu tshianza tshia balami, wakatukokela mai kabidi, wakanuisha mikoko.
20 Wakakonka bana bandi ba bakashi ne, Yeye udi kudi kunyi? Bualu kai nuakushiya mulume ku nyima? Numubikile, alue biandi kudia bidia.
21 Mose wakasua kushikama ne mulume au, ne yeye wakaha Mose Sipola, muanʼandi wa bakashi.
22 Yeye wakalela muana wa balume, wakamuidika dina ne, Geshoma, wakamba ne, Nakadi mushikame mu buloba bua benyi.
23 Hakalela matuku a bungi, mukelenge wa Ejipitu wakafua Bena Isalele bakafua mikemu bualu bua buhika, bakadila, muadi wabo wakafika kudi Nzambi bualu bua buhika buabo.
24 Nzambi wakumpa mikemu yabo, ne Nzambi wakafuka mulayi wandi kudi Abalahama ne kudi Isaka ne kudi Yakoba.
25 Nzambi wakamona Bena Isalele Nzambi wakamanya dikenga diabo.

Exodus 3
1 Mose wakadi ulama mikoko ya Yetelo tatu andi-muenu, mubuki wa mu Midiyana, wakaya ne mikoko ku nyima kua tshihela, wakalua ku mukuna wa Nzambi, dina diau Holeba.
2 Muanjelo wa Yehowa wakamueneka kudiye mu ludimi lua kahia, luakadi luluhuka mu katshi, wakamona talala, kahia kakadi kosha katshi, katshi kakakahia.
3 Mose wakamba ne, Nenye kutangila bualu ebu bua kukema ne, Munyi katshi kakena kahia.
4 Bakamumona Yehowa uya kutangila bualu ebu. Nzambi wakamubikila mu katshi, wakamba ne, Mose, Mose Yeye wakitaba ne, Ndiku.
5 Nzambi wakamba ne, Kulu ha buihi, umushe bisabata biebe ku makasa, bualu bua muaba udi muimane udi buloba bua tshijila.
6 Wakamba kabidi ne, Ndi bianyi Nzambi wa tatuʼebe, Nzambi wa Abalahama, Nzambi wa Isaka, Nzambi wa Yakoba Mose wakayikila mesu andi, wakatshina kutangila Nzambi.
7 Yehowa wakamba ne, Nakutangila dikenga dia bantu banyi badi mu Ejipitu bulelela, nakunvua muadi wabo bualu bua tuhita tuabo, bualu bua ndi mumanye kanyinganyinga kabo.
8 Nakulua hanshi bua kubasungila mu bianza bia Bena Ejipitu, ne bua kubaluhula mu buloba abu, ne bua kulua nʼabo mu buloba buimpe ne bunene, mu buloba budi ne mabele ne buitshi bia bungi, mu muaba wa Bena Kanana ne wa Bena Hiti ne wa Bena Amole ne wa Bena Pelese ne wa Bena Hiwi ne wa Bena Yebuse.
9 Katataka, tangila, muadi wa Bena Isalele Wakafika kundi, Hakatangila kabidi dikenga diabo didi Bena Ejipitu babakengesha nʼadi.
10 Lua biebe, nenkutume kudi Palo, Ulue biebe ne bantu banyi, bana ba Isalele, badi mu Ejipitu.
11 Mose wakambila Nzambi ne, Meme ndi nganyi bua kuya kudi Palo ne bua buhatula Bena Isalele mu Ejipitu?
12 Nzambi wakamba ne, Bulelela, nengikale nʼebe, nenkuhe tshimanyinu etshi tshidi tshilesha ne nakukutuma hawahatula bantu badi mu Ejipitu, nenutendelele Nzambi ha mukuna eu.
13 Mose wakambila Nzambi ne, Hanaya bianyi kudi Bena Isalele, hanabambila ne, Nzambi wa batatuʼenu wakantuma kunudi, nebankonke ne, Dina diandi nganyi, nembambile tshinyi?
14 Nzambi wakambila Mose ne, MEME NDI MUNDI. Nzambi wakamba kabidi ne, Ambila Bena Isalele ne, NDI MUNDI wakuntuma.
15 Nzambi wakambila Mose kabidi ne, Wewe newambile Bena Isalele ne, Yehowa, Nzambi wa bankambua benu, Nzambi wa Abalahama, Nzambi wa Isaka ne Nzambi wa Yakoba wakuntuma kunudi edi ndina dianyi tshiendelele, etshi ntshivulukidi tshianyi kudi bantu bonso.
16 Ya usangishe bakulu ba Bena Isalele, ubambile ne, Yehowa, Nzambi wa bankambua benu, Nzambi wa Abalahama, Nzambi wa Isaka ne Nzambi wa Yakoba wakamueneka kundi, wakungambila ne, Nakulua kunudi, Hakatangila mudibo banuenzela mu Ejipitu.
17 Nakumana kuamba ne, Nennuhatule mu dikenga dia Ejipitu, nendue nʼenu mu buloba bua Bena Kanana ne bua Bena Hiti ne bua Bena Amole ne bua Bena Pelese ne bua Bena Hiwi ne bua Bena Yebuse, mu buloba budi ne mabele ne buitshi bia bungi.
18 Bobo nebunvue dî diebe, wewe ne bakulu ba Bena Isalele nenufile kudi mukelenge wa Ejipitu, nenumuambile ne, Yehowa, Nzambi wa Bena Ebelu, wakusangakena nʼetu Tudi tukutendekena ne, Witabushe bua tuetu kuya luendu lua matuku asatu mu tshihela bua kushihela Yehowa Nzambi wetu nyama.
19 Ndi mumanye ne, Mukelenge wa Ejipitu kena witabusha bua nuenu kuya, neitabushe anu ne tshianza tshikole.
20 Nengolole tshianza tshianyi, nembakengeshe ne malu a kukema; ne ku nyima kua malu â yeye neanulekele.
21 Nempe bantu aba dikasa dimpe kudi Bena Ejipitu, ne hayabo, kabena baye ne bianza bituhu.
22 Kadi mukashi ne mukashi nealombe mukashi udi ushikama ha buihi nʼandi, ne mukashi udi ushikama mu nsubu wandi, bintu bia argent ne bia or ne bilulu, nuenu nenubiluatshishe bana benu ba balume ne bana benu ba bakashi, nunku nenuhaule Bena Ejipitu.

Exodus 4
1 Mose wakandamuna ne, Kadi bobo kabena bangitabusha, kabena bunvua di dianyi, bobo nebambe ne, Yehowa kakamueneka kûdi nansha.
2 Yehowa wakamuambila ne, Udi ne tshintu kai mu tshianza tshiebe? Yeye wakamba ne, Mulangala.
3 Yehowa wakamba ne, Wele hanshi Nunku wakawela hanshi ne wowo wakakudimuka nyoka, Mose wakamunyema.
4 Yehowa wakambila Mose ne, Olola tshianza tshiebe, umukuate ku mukila, yeye wakolola tshianza tshiandi, wakamukuata ne nyoka wakakudimuka mulangala kabidi.
5 Yehowa wakamba ne, Bualu ebu nebubitabushishe ne, Yehowa, Nzambi wa bankambua babo, Nzambi wa Abalahama, Nzambi wa Isaka ne Nzambi wa Yakoba wakamueneka kudi.
6 Yehowa wakamuambila kabidi ne, Teka tshianza mu tshiadi tshiebe. Yeye wakateka tshianza tshiandi mu tshiadi, ne hakatshiumusheye, monai, tshianza tshiandi tshiakadi ne nsudi mutoke bu neige.
7 Yehowa wakamba ne, Teka tshianza tshiebe mu tshiadi kabidi. Yeye wakateka tshianza mu tshiadi kabidi, hakatshiumusheye mu tshiadi, tshianza tshiandi tshiakakudimuka bu muakadi mubidi wandi.
8 Bikalabo kabayi bakuitabusha, kabayi bunvue di dia tshimanymu tshia kumudilu, bobo nebitabushe dî dia tshimanymu tshia kunshikidilu.
9 Bikalabo kabayi bitabusha bimanyinu ebi bibidi, kabayi bunvue dî diebe, wewe newangate mai a mu musulu, wele ha buloba bûme, ne mai awakangata mu musulu neakudimuke mashi ha buloba bûme.
10 Mose wakambila Yehowa ne, Yehowa, meme tshiena mua kuakula bimpe, nansha diambedi, nansha katshia wabanga kuakula ne muntu webe, bualu bua meme ndi muena tshikukumina.
11 Yehowa wakamukonka ne, Mufuki wa mukana mua bantu nganyi? Udi ufuka bena kamama ne bena mahaha ne bantu badi mua kumona ne bantu badi bafofo nganyi? Meme Yehowa, tshiena ngenza malu â, anyi?
12 Nunku, ya biebe, meme nengikale ne mukana muebe, meme nenkuyishe mei andi musue wewe kuamba.
13 Mose wakamba ne, Yehowa, nda nkusengelela ne, Utume muntu ûdi musue kutuma.
14 Tshiji tshia Yehowa tshiakalua kudi Mose, wakamuambila ne, Alona, muanʼenu mulume, muena Lewi, kenaku, anyi? Ndi mumanye ne, Yeye udi mua kuakula bimpe. Yeye ulualua mu nshila bua kusangakena hebe, ne hakatangileye, yeye neasanke mu mutshima wandi.
15 Wewe neuyikile handi, neuteke mei mukana muandi, meme nengikale ne mukana muebe ne mukana muandi, nenkuyishe malu anudi mua kuenza.
16 Yeye neikale muakuidi webe kudi bantu, yeye neikale bu mukana muebe ne wewe newikale bu Nzambi kudiye.
17 Wangate mulangala eu mu tshianza tshiebe, ûdi wenza nʼau bimanyinu ebi.
18 Mose wakahingana kudi Yetelo tatuʼandi-muenu, wakamuambila ne, Ndi musue kuhingana kudi bana betu badi mu Ejipitu, bua kubamona ne bobo batshidi ne muoyo Yetelo wakambila Mose ne, Ya biebe talala.
19 Yehowa wakambila Mose mu Midiyana ne, Ya biebe, uhingane mu Ejipitu bualu bua bantu bonso bakasua kukushiha bakafua.
20 Mose wakangata mukashi wandi ne bana bandi balume, wakabateka ha nyima ha mpunda, wakahingana mu buloba bua Ejipitu. Mose wakaya ne mulangala wa Nzambi mu tshianza tshiandi.
21 Yehowa wakambila Mose ne, Hawahingana mu Ejipitu, wenza ku mesu kua Palô malu a kukema onso anakuteka mu tshianza tshiebe, kadi meme nenkoleshe mutshima wa Palô, ne yeye kena ulekela bantu.
22 Wewe newambile Palô ne, Yehowa udi Wamba ne, Isalele udi muanʼanyi wa balume, muanʼa bute wanyi,
23 ne meme nakamana kukuambila ne, Ulekela muanʼanyi wa balume, bualu bua kunkuatshila mudimu, kadi wewe wakahidia kulu ulekela, nunku meme nenshihe muana webe, muanʼa bute webe.
24 Hakadibo baya mu nshila wa ku nsubu wa tshilala benyi, Yehowa wakasangakena nʼandi, wakamba kumushiha.
25 Sipola wakangata dibue ditue, wakatengula muan andi mulume, wakateka dikoba ku makasa a Mose, wakambila Mose ne, Bulelela, wewe udi bayanyi wa mashi.
26 Yeye wakalekela Mose. Mukashi wakamba ne, Wewe udi bayanyi wa mashi bualu bua ku tengula.
27 Yehowa wakambila Alona ne, Ya biebe mu tshihela bua kusangakena ne Mose Yeye wakaya, wakasangakena handi mu mukuna wa Nzambi, wakatuangana nʼandi mishiku.
28 Mose wakambila Alona mei onso akamuambila Yehowa ne bimanyinu bionso biakamuheye.
29 Mose ne Alona bakaya, bakasangisha bakulu bonso ba Bena Isalele.
30 Alona wakabambila mei onso akadi Yehowa wambila Mose, ne yeye wakenza bimanyinu ku mesu kua bantu.
31 Bantu bakitabusha ne hakunvuabo ne, Yehowa wakulua kudi Bena Isalele, wakumona dikenga diabo, bakinama mitu yabo, bakatendelela.

Exodus 5
1 Ku nyima kua malu â Mose ne Alona bakalua kudi Palô, bakamuambila ne, Yehowa, Nzambi wa Isalele, udi Wamba ne, Ulekele bantu banyi, baye mu tshihela bua kudia bidia ku mesu kuanyi.
2 Palo wakamba ne, Yehowa nganyi? Tshiena ngitabusha di diandi bua kulekela Bena Isalele. Meme tshiena mumanye Yehowa ne tshiena ndekela Bena Isalele.
3 Bakamba ne, Nzambi wa Bena Ebelu wakasangakena hetu, witabushe ne tuetu tuye luendu lua matuku asatu mu tshihela bua kushihela. Yehowa Nzambi wetu nyama, bua yeye katukengeshi ne masama anyi muele wa mata.
4 Mukelenge wa Ejipitu wakabambila ne, Bualu kai, nuenu Mose ne Alona, nudi nulekesha bantu midimu yabo, yai bienu ku midimu yenu.
5 Palô wakamba ne, Monai, bantu bakukumbana ba bungi mu buloba, ne nuenu nudi nulekesha midimu yabo.
6 Mu dituku adi mene Palo wakela di kudi tuhita tua bantu ne tupitene tuabo ne,
7 Kanulu bantu bisonsa bia kuenza nʼabi tushola bu munuakenza diambedi, bobo badikebele bisonsa.
8 Bobo nebenze bungi bua tushola buakenzabo diambedi, kanuena nuhuekesha mudimu, nansha kakese, bualu bua bobo badi bafuba, badi bamba ne, Tudi basue kuya kushihela Nzambi wetu nyama.
9 Nudiundishe mudimu wabo, bawenze, nunku kabitabushi mei a mashimi.
10 Tuhita tua bantu ne tupitene tuakaya kudi bantu, tuakabambila ne, Palo wakamba ne, Tshiena nnuha bisonsa.
11 Yai bienu, nudikebele bisonsa kunudi mua kubisangana, kadi kabena bahuekesha mudimu wenu.
12 Nunku bantu bakadi bamuangalaka buloba bua Ejipitu buonso bua kukeba bisonsa.
13 Tuhita tuakabenzesha ne, Nushikishai mudimu wenu wa buatshia-buatshia bu munuakenza hanuakadi ne bisonsa.
14 Tuhita tua Palô tuakakuma bantu bakadi ku mutu kua Bena Isalele, tuakamba ne, Bualu kai kanuena nushikisha mudimu wenu ne makelela ne lelu bu munuakenza diambedi?
15 Bantu bakadi ku mutu kua Bena Isalele bakalua, bakadila kudi Palo ne, Udi wenzela bantu bebe nunku bualu kai?
16 Kabena bakesha bantu bebe bisonsa, ne badi bamba ne Enzai tushola, mona, badi bakulua bantu bebe, kadi dihila didi kudi Bena Ejipitu bebe.
17 Kadi yeye wakabambila ne, Nudi bafuba, nudi bafuba, ne bua bualu ebu nudi nuamba ne, Witabushe bua tuye kushihela Yehowa nyama!
18 Yai bienu, enzai mudimu, katuena tunuha bisonsa, kadi nuenu nenulue ne bungi bua tushola bu mutuakuamba.
19 Bantu bakadi ku mutu kua Bena Isalele bakamona ne, Tudi ne tshilumbu tshikole Hakambabo ne, Katuena tuhuekesha bungi bua tushola butudi tulomba buatshia-buatshia.
20 Bakasangakena ne Mose ne Alona mu nshila hakaluhukabo kudi Palô,
21 bobo bakabambila ne, Yehowa anutangile, anulumbuluishe, bualu bua nuakutuvuisha bu tshintu tshidi tshi tua tshibi ku mesu kua Palô ne ku mesu kua bantu bandi, nuakuteka muele wa nvita mu bianza biabo wa kutushiha nʼau.
22 Mose wakahingana kudi Yehowa, wakamba ne, Yehowa, wewe wakenzela bantu aba bibi nunku bualu kai? Wakantuma bualu kai?
23 Hanakalua kudi Palô mu dina diebe, yeye wakenzela bantu aba bibi, ne wewe kuena muanze kusungila bantu bebe nansha kakese.

Exodus 6
1 Yehowa wakambila Mose ne, Katataka wewe mutangile bu mundi ngenzela Palô, bualu bua yeye neabalekele ku tshianza tshikole, neabihate mu buloba buandi ne tshianza tshikole.
2 Nzambi wakambila Mose ne, Meme ndi Yehowa.
3 Nakamueneka kudi Abalahama ne kudi Isaka ne kudi Yakoba bu Nzambi wa Bukole Buonso, kadi bobo kabakamanya dina dianyi Yehowa nansha
4 Meme kabidi nakajadika nʼabo tshihungidi tshianyi bua kubaha buloba bua Kanana, buloba buakadibo bashikamamu bu benyi.
5 Bualu bukuabo, nakunvua mikemu ya Bena Isalele badi mu butaka kudi Bena Ejipitu, ndi muvuluke tshihungidi tshianyi.
6 Nunku wambile Bena Isalele ne, Ndi Yehowa, ne meme nennuhatule muinshi mua majitu a Bena Ejipitu, nennusungile ku buhika buabo, nennuhikule ne diboko diolola ne dilumbulula dikole.
7 Nennuangate bu bantu banyi ne meme nengikale Nzambi wenu, nuenu nenumanye ne, Meme udi Yehowa, Nzambi wenu, udi unuhatula muinshi mua majitu a Bena Ejipitu.
8 Meme nendue nʼenu mu buloba bunakalaya Abalahama ne Isaka ne Yakoba, nennuhebu bu buhianyi ndi Yehowa.
9 Mose wakambila Bena Isalele nunku kadi bobo kabakatumikila dî dia Mose bualu bua kanyinganyinga ka mutshima, ne bua dikenga dia buhika.
10 Yehowa wakambila Mose ne,
11 Ya biebe kudi Palô, mukelenge wa Ejipitu, umuambile ne, Lekela Bena Isalele bahatuke mu buloba buebe.
12 Mose wakamba ku mesu kua Yehowa ne, Mona, Bena Isalele kabena banze kutumikila dî dianyi, Palô neunvuile muena tshikukumina kudi kunyi?
13 Yehowa wakambila Mose ne Alona mei a kuya nʼau kudi Bena Isalele ne kudi Palo, mukelenge wa Ejipitu, bualu bua kuhatula Bena Isalele mu buloba bua Ejipitu.
14 Aba mbantu badi ku mutu kua nsubu ya batatuʼabo. Bana ba balume ba Lubena, muanʼa bute wa Isalele Hanoka ne Palu, Heselona ne Kâmi, â mmeku a Lubena.
15 Bana ba balume ba Shimeona Yemuele ne Yamina ne Obada ne Yakina ne Soha ne Shaula, muana wa balume wa mukashi wa Bena Kanana, â mmeku a Shimeona.
16 Mena a bana ba balume ba Lewi ku bikondo biabo adi nunku Geshona ne Kohata ne Melah, bidimu bia Lewi biakadi lukama makumi asatu ne muanda mutekete.
17 Bana ba balume ba Geshona: Libini ne Shimei, ku meku abo.
18 Bana ba balume ba Kohata: Amalama ne Yisehâ ne Hebelone ne Uziele, bidimu bia Kohata biakadi lukama ne makumi asatu ne bisatu.

Exodus 7
1 Yehowa wakambila Mose ne, Nakukuvuisha bu Nzambi ku mesu kua Palô, ne Alona mukuluʼebe neikale muakuidi webe.
2 Wewe newambe mei onso andi nkuambila, ne Alona mukuluʼebe neambile Palo ne, Ulekele Bena Isalele mu buloba buebe.
3 Nenkoleshe mutshima wa Palô, nennudishe bimanyinu bianyi ne malu anyi a kukema mu buloba bua Ejipitu.
4 Kadi Palô kena ukunyuila, ne meme nenteke tshianza tshianyi ha Ejipitu, nempatule bantu banyi Bena Isalele mu buloba bua Bena Ejipitu ku dilumbulula dikole.
5 Bena Ejipitu nebamanye ne meme ndi Yehowa, hangolola tshianza tshianyi ha Ejipitu ne hanahatula Bena Isalele munkatshi muabo.
6 Mose ne Alona bakenza bu muakamba Yehowa, bakenza nunku.
7 Mose wakadi ne bidimu makumi muanda mukulu, ne Alona wakadi ne bidimu makumi muanda mukulu ne bisatu, hakadibo bakula ne Palô.
8 Yehowa wakambila Mose ne Alona ne,
9 Hanuambila Palo ne, Nundeshe bualu bua kukema, hashishe, wewe wambile Alona ne, Angata mulangala webe, uwele hanshi ku mesu kua Palô ne mulangala neukudimuke nyoka.
10 Mose ne Alona bakaya kudi Palô, bakenza bu muakamba Yehowa: Alona wakela mulangala wandi hanshi ku mesu kua Palô ne bantu bandi, ne mulangala wakakudimuka nyoka.
11 Palô wakabikila bena meji ne bena muhongo ne bena bilengulengu ba mu Ejipitu, bakenza muomumue ne manga abo.
12 Muntu ne mundi wakela mulangala wandi hanshi, wakakudimuka nyoka, kadi mulangala wa Alona wakatuma milangala yabo.
13 Mutshima wa Palo wakakoleshibua, yeye kakabunvuila, bu muakamba Yehowa.
14 Yehowa wakambila Mose ne, Mutshima wa Palô udi ne tshitshu, yeye udi uhidia kulekela bantu.
15 Ya biebe kudi Palô ne dinda, hayeye ku mai, wimane kukala kua musulu bua kusangakena nʼandi, uye ne mulangala wakakudimuka nyoka au mu tshianza tshiebe.
16 Umuambile ne, Yehowa, Nzambi wa Bena Ebelu, wakuntuma kudi ne, Ulekele bantu banyi, baye bua kuntendelela mu tshihela, ku diambedi to ne mpindiu wewe kuena muanze kunvua.
17 Yehowa udi wamba ne, Mu bualu ebu wewe neumanye ne, Meme ndi Yehowa tangila, nenkume mai adi mu musulu ne mulangala udi mu tshianza tshianyi, ne mai onso neakudimuke mashi.
18 Minyinyi ya mu mai idi mu musulu neyifue, mai neanunke muhuya mubi, mai a mu musulu neatonde Bena Ejipitu.
19 Yehowa wakela dî kudi Mose ne, Wambile Alona ne, Angata mulangala webe, olola tshianza tshiebe ha mutu ha mai a mu Ejipitu, ku mutu kua misulu yabo ne tusulu tuabo ne mashiba abo, mai onso akudimuke mashi, mashi neikale mu buloba bua Ejipitu buonso ne mu milondo ya mitshi ne mu milondo ya mabue.
20 Mose ne Alona bakenza bu muakamba Yehowa, Alona wakabisha mulangala, wakakuma mai akadi mu musulu, ku mesu kua Palô ne ku mesu kua bantu bandi, mai onso akadi mu musulu akakudimuka mashi.
21 Minyinyi ya mu mai yakadi mu musulu bakafua, musulu wakamuha muhuya mubi, ne Bena Ejipitu kabakamanya mua kunua mai a mu musulu, mashi akadi mu buloba bua Ejipitu buonso,
22 Bena bilengulengu ba mu Ejipitu bakenza muomumue ne manga abo, mutshima wa Palô wakakoleshibua, yeye kakabunvuila, bu muakamba Yehowa.
23 Palô wakanyunguluka, wakaya mu nsubu wandi, kakateka bualu ebu mu mutshima wandi.
24 Bena Ejipitu bonso bakumbula mena hehi ne musulu, bakakeba mai a kunua, bualu bua kabakamanya mua kunua mai a mu musulu.
25 Matuku akakumbana muanda mutekete ku nyima kua dituta dia mai a mu musulu kudi Yehowa.

Exodus 8
1 Yehowa wakambila Mose ne, Ya biebe kudi Palô, umuambile ne, Yehowa udi Wamba ne, Lekela bantu banyi baye kuntendelela.
2 Biwahidia kubalekela, nentume biula mu buloba buebe.
3 Musulu newikale tente ne biula, neubuele mu nsubu webe ne mu nsubu webe wa bulalu ne ha bulalu buebe, ne mu nsubu ya bahika bebê ne kudi bantu bebe, ne mu nfulu yenu ne mu binu bienu.
4 Biula nebilue hamʼbidi hebe ne hamʼbidi ha bantu bebe ne hamʼbidi ha bahika bebe bonso.
5 Yehowa wakela dî kudi Mose ne, Wambile Alona ne, Olola tshianza tshiebe ne mulangala webe ha mutu ha misulu ne ha mutu ha tusulu ne mashiba bualu bua kutuma biula mu buloba bua Ejipitu.
6 Alona wakolola tshianza tshiandi ku mutu kua mai a mu Ejipitu, biula biakabaha, biakûsha buloba bua Ejipitu tente.
7 Bena bilengulengu bakenza muomumue ne manga abo, bakatuma biula mu buloba bua Ejipitu.
8 Palo Wakabikila Mose ne Alona, wakamba ne, Nundombele Yehowa, yeye umushe biula kundi ne kudi bantu banyi, nendekele bantu baye kushihela Yehowa nyama.
9 Mose wakambila Palô ne, Wikale ne butumbi ebu kundi, ndituku kai diudi musue meme nkulombele bua kumusha biula kûdi ne kudi bantu bebe ne kudi bahika bebe ne mu nsubu yenu, bishale anu mu musulu nkayau?
10 Yeye wakamba ne, Makelela Mose wakamba ne, Bualu buikale bu mu dî diebe, bua kukumanyisha ne, Kakuena nzambi mukuabo bu Yehowa Nzambi wetu.
11 Biula nebiumuke kûdi ne ku nsubu yebe, ne kudi bahika bebe ne kudi bantu bebe, nebishale anu mu musulu nkayau.
12 Mose ne Alona bakaluhuka kudi Palô. Mose wakalomba Yehowa bua buila biakatumeye kudi Palô.
13 Yehowa wakenza bu mu dî dia Mose, biula biakafua mu nsubu ne mu mbanza ne mu madimi.
14 Bakabisangisha mu bisumbu, ne buloba buakanunka muhuya mubi.
15 Kadi bakatangila Palô ne, Bualu buakushika, wakakolesha mutshima wandi, kakabinvuila, bu muakamba Yehowa.
16 Yehowa wakela dî kudi Mose ne, Wambile Alona ne, Olola mulangala webe, ukume dinfuenkenya dia buloba, dinfuenkenya nedikudunuke nkusu ya mu mitu mu buloba buonso bua Ejipitu.
17 Bakenza nunku, Alona wakolola tshianza tshiandi ne mulangala wandi, wakakuma dinfuenkenya dia buloba ne nkusu yakadi kudi bantu ne kudi nyama, dinfuenkenya dionso dia ha buloba diakakudimuka nkusu mu buloba bua Ejipitu buonso.
18 Bena bilengulengu bakenza nunku ne manga abo bua kutuma nkusu, kadi kabakamanya mua kuenza, nkusu yakadi kudi bantu ne kudi nyama.
19 Bena bilengulengu bakambila Palo ne, Bualu ebu budi bu munu wa Yehowa, kadi mutshima wa Palô wakakoleshibua, yeye kakabunvuila, bu muakamba Yehowa.
20 Yehowa wakambila Mose ne, Ubike ne dinda dionso, uye kudi Palô hayeye ku mai, umuambile ne, Yehowa udi Wamba ne, Ulekele bantu banyi, baye kuntendelela.
21 Biwahidia kulekela bantu banyi, nentumine bisumbu bia tuishi kûdi ne kudi bahika bebe ne kudi bantu bebe ne mu nsubu yenu, nsubu ya Bena Ejipitu neyikale ne bisumbu bia tuishi tente, ne hanshi ha buloba kabidi.
22 Mu dituku adi nendame buloba bua Goshene budi bantu banyi bashikame, bisumbu bia tuishi kabiena bifikaku, bualu bua kukumanyisha ne, Meme ndi Yehowa hanshi ha buloba.
23 Nenteke ditahulula hankatshi ha bantu banyi ne bantu bebe, nengenze tshimanyinu etshi makelela.
24 Yehowa Wakenza nunku, ne bisumbu binene bia tuishi biakabuela mu nsubu wa Palô ne mu nsubu ya bena mudimu bandi, tuishi tuakahaula buloba bua Ejipitu buonso.
25 Palô Wakabikila Mose ne Alona, wakamba ne, Yai bienu, nushihele Nzambi wenu nyama mu buloba bua Ejipitu.
26 Mose wakamba ne, Katuena mua kuenza nunku, bituashihela Yehowa Nzambi wetu nyama, netuenze bualu budi bufuisha Bena Ejipitu bundu, bituenza bualu budi bufuisha Bena Ejipitu bundu ku mesu, bobo kabena batukuma ne mabue, anyi?
27 Netuye luendu lua matuku asatu mu tshihela, netushihele Yehowa Nzambi wetu nyama bu muatuambileye.
28 Palô wakamba ne, Nennulekele, nuye mu tshihela bua kushihela Yehowa Nzambi wenu nyama, kadi kanuyi kule, nundombele Nzambi.
29 Mose wakamba ne, Mona, nengumuke kûdi, nendombe Yehowa ne yeye neamushe bisumbu bia tuishi kudi Palô ne kudi bena mudimu bandi ne kudi bantu bandi makelela, kadi Palô katu dingi kabidi bua kulekela bantu baye kushihela Yehowa nyama.
30 Mose wakaluhuka kudi Palô, wakalomba Yehowa.
31 Yehowa wakenza bu mu dî dia Mose yeye wakumusha bisumbu bia tuishi kudi Palô ne kudi bena mudimu bandi ne kudi bantu bandi, nansha kishi kamue kakashala.
32 Palô wakakolesha mutshima wandi musangu eu kabidi, yeye bakalekela bantu bua kuyabo.

Exodus 9
1 Yehowa wakambila Mose ne, Ya biebe kudi Palô, umuambile ne, Yehowa, Nzambi wa Bena Ebelu, udi Wamba ne, Ulekele bantu banyi, baye kuntendelela.
2 Biwahidia kubalekela, ne biwabakanda,
3 Yehowa neateke tshianza tshiandi ha bimuna bidi mu budimi, ha tubalu ne nyama ya mpunda ne nyama ya tumelo ne ngombe ne mikoko, dishina dikole nedibikuate.
4 Yehowa neatahulule bimuna bia Bena Isalele ne bimuna bia Bena Ejipitu, nansha tshintu tshikese tshia Bena Isalele netshifue.
5 Yehowa wakamana ku sungula dituku, makelela Yehowa nênze bualu ebu mu buloba.
6 Hakatshiabu Yehowa Wakenza bualu ebu; bimuna bionso bia mu Ejipitu biakafua, kadi bimuna bia Bena Isalele kabiakafua nansha tshimue.
7 Palô wakatuma mundi, wakamona ne, Nansha tshintu tshimue tshia bimuna bia Bena Isalele tshiakafua. Mutshima wa Palô wakakoleshibua, yeye bakalekela bantu.
8 Yehowa wakambila Mose ne Alona ne, Nuangate manza a butue bua mu tshikutu, Mose abumiamine mulu ku mesu kua Palô.
9 Butue nebulue bu dinfuenkenya dikese mu buloba bua Ejipitu buonso, butue nebuenzeshe mputa ya mbalanga hamʼbidi ha bantu ne hamʼbidi ha nyama mu buloba bua Ejipitu buonso.
10 Bakangata butue bua mu tshikutu, bakimana ku mpala kua Palô, Mose wakabumiamina mulu, buakenzesha bantu ne nyama mputa ya mbalanga hamʼbidi.
11 Bena bilengulengu kabakamanya mua kuimana ku mpala kua Mose bualu bua mputa ya mbalanga, mputa yakadi hamʼbidi ha bena bilengulengu ne hamʼbidi ha Bena Ejipitu bonso.
12 Yehowa wakakolesha mutshima wa Palô, yeye kakabunvuila, bu muakambila Yehowa Mose.
13 Yehowa wakambila Mose ne, Bika ne dinda dionso, wimane ku mpala kua Palô, umuambile ne, Yehowa, Nzambi wa Bena Ebelu udi wamba ne, Ulekele bantu banyi, baye kuntendelela.
14 Musangu eu nentume milau yanyi yonso ku mutshima webe ne kudi bena mudimu bebe ne kudi bantu bebe, bua kukumanyisha ne, Kakuena nzambi mukuabo bu meme mu buloba buonso.
15 Bu meme muolole tshianza tshianyi, mufume disama dikole kudi bantu bebe, wewe wakadi kujimina ha buloba.
16 Kadi bulelela, nakukubisha bua kukulesha bukole buanyi ne bua dina dianyi dimanyishibue ha buloba buonso.
17 Wewe utshidi udibandisha kudi bantu banyi, udi uhidia kubalekela munyi?
18 Tangila, makelela ha diba edi mene nemmatshishe mabue a nvula a bungi be, kabena banze kutangila mabue a nvula nunku ku tshibangidilu tshia bukelenge bua Ejipitu tô ne katataka,
19 Nunku utumine bantu lubilu, basangishe bimuna biebe ne bintu bionso biudi nʼabi mu budimi, muntu yonso ne nyama yonso bidi bishala mu budimi bidi kabiyi bihingana mu nsubu, mabue a nvula neahone hamʼbidi babi, nebifue.
20 Muntu yonso wakatshina di dia Yehowa munkatshi mua bena mudimu ba Palô wakatuma bantu bandi, bakasangisha bimuna biandi mu nsubu.
21 Yeye wakadi kayi muitabushe di dia Yehowa wakashiya bantu bandi ne bimuna biandi mu mpata.
22 Yehowa wakambila Mose ne, Olola tshianza tshiebe mulu, bua mabue a nvula ikale mu buloba bua Ejipitu buonso, ne ha muntu ne ha nyama ne ha midioko yonso ya mu budimi, mu buloba bua Ejipitu buonso.
23 Mose wakolola mulangala wandi mulu ne Yehowa wakatuma makubakuba ne mabue a nvula, miele ya nvula yakakuma ha buloba, Yehowa wakama tshisha mabue a nvula ha buloba bua Ejipitu.
24 Mabue a nvula ne miele ya nvula biakabuelakana, biakatamba bikole, kabakatangila bualu abu mu buloba bua Ejipitu katshia bualua tshisamba tshia bantu.
25 Mabue a nvula akashika bintu bionso bia mu buloba bua Ejipitu biakadi mu budimi, ne bantu ne nyama, mabue a nvula akashika midioko yonso ya mu budimi ne mitshi yonso.
26 Anu mu buloba bua Goshene muakadi Bena Isalele, mabue a nvula kakadiku.
27 Palô wakatuma di, wakabikila Mose ne Alona, wakabambila ne, Nakuenza bualu bubi musangu eu, Yehowa udi muakane ne meme ne bantu banyi tudi bantu babi.
28 Nundombele Yehowa, bualu bua makubakuba ne mabue a nvula biakututonda, nennulekele, kanuena nushala kabidi.
29 Mose wakamuambila ne, Hanaluhuka mu musoko eu, nengolole bianza bianyi kudi Yehowa, makubakuba neashike, mabue a nvula neashike kabidi, bualu bua kukumanyisha ne buloba budi bua Yehowa.
30 Kadi ndi muma nye ne wewe ne bantu bebe kanuena banze kuikala ne ditshina dia Yehowa Nzambi.
31 Mitshi ya lin ne ya orge yakashihibua, bualu bua orge yakadi mu miehu ne lin yakadi ne bilongo.
32 Kadi blê ne bintu bikuabo kabiakadi bifue, kabiakanza kukola nansha.
33 Mose wakaluhuka mu musoko kudi Palô, wakolola bianza biandi kudi Yehowa, ne makubakuba ne mabue a nvula biakashika, nvula kakamata ha buloba kabidi.
34 Hakatangila Palô nvula ne mabue a nvula ne makubakuba biakashika, yeye wakatamba kuenza bibi, wakakolesha mutshima wandi ne yeye ne bantu bandi.
35 Mutshi ma wa Palô wakakoleshibua, yeye kakunvuila Bena Isalele, bu muakambila Yehowa Mose.

Exodus 10
1 Yehowa wakambila Mose ne, Ya biebe kudi Palô, bualu bua nakakolesha mutshima wandi ne mitshima ya bantu bandi bua kulesha bimanyinu bianyi munkatshi muabo.
2 Wewe newambile muan ebe wa balume mu matshu, ne muana wa muanʼebe wa balume, malu anakuenza mu buloba bua Ejipitu, ne bimanyinu bianyi binakuenza munkatshi muabo, bua nuenu numanye ne, Meme ndi Yehowa.
3 Mose ne Alona bakaya kudi Palô, bakamuambila ne, Yehowa, Nzambi wa Bena Ebelu, udi Wamba ne. Wewe neuhidie kudihuekesha ku mesu kuanyi musangu bule munyi? Ulekele bantu banyi baye kuntendelela.
4 Biwahidia kulekela bantu hanyi, makelela nentumine mikumbi mu buloba buebe,
5 neyikale ha mutu ha buloba buonso, kabena mua kutangila buloba, yoyo neyidie bintu biakushala ku mabue a nvula, nenudie mitshi yenu yonso idi idiunda mu budimi.
6 Neyule nsubu yebe tente ne nsubu ya bantu bebe ne nsubu ya Bena Ejipitu bonso, nansha batatu benu nansha bankambua benu kabakatangila bualu ebu katshia babuela mu buloba ebu to ne katataka Mose wakanyunguluka, wakaluhuka kudi Palô.
7 Bantu ba Palô bakamukonka ne, Muntu eu neikale buteyi kutudi musangu bule munyi? Ulekele bantu baye kutendelela Yehowa Nzambi wabo, wewe kuena mumanye ne, Buloba bua Ejipitu buakushihibua, anyi?
8 Bakalua ne Mose ne Alona kudi Palo, ne yeye wakabambila ne, Yai bienu nutendelele Yehowa Nzambi wenu kadi badi bamba kuya mbanganyi?
9 Mose wakamba ne, Netuye ne bana bakese ne bantu banunu, ne bana betu ba balume ne bana betu ba bakashi, netuye ne mikoko yetu ne ngombe yetu, bualu bua tudi ne bualu bua kudia bidia ku mesu kua Yehowa.
10 Yeye wakabambila ne, Yehowa ashale nʼenu binanulekela ne bana benu bakese, bualu bua bualu bubi budi munda muenu.
11 Nansha, nuenu bantu balume nudi mua kuya kutendelela Yehowa, bualu bua nudi basue kuenza nunku. Bakabihata ku mesu kua Palô.
12 Yehowa wakambila Mose ne, Olola tshianza tshiebe ha buloba bua Ejipitu, bua kutuala mikumbi mu buloba bua Ejipitu, neyidie bintu bionso biakashala ku mabue a nvula.
13 Mose wakolola mula ngala wandi ha buloba bua Ejipitu, ne Yehowa wakatuma luhehele luaku esete dituku adi to ne hakatshia butuku, ne dinda luhehele lua ku esete luakatuma mikumbi.
14 Mikumbi yakamuangalaka buloba bua Ejipitu buonso, yakatamba bungi, kakuakadi mikumbi bu ei diambedi, ne mikuabo bu ei kayena ilua kabidi.
15 Yakabuikila buloba buonso, to ne hakalua buloba midima, yakadia midioko yonso ya mu madimi ne mamuma onso a ku mitshi, biakashala ku mabue a nvula, nansha tshintu tshikese nansha midioko nansha mutshi biakashala mu buloba bua Ejipitu buonso.
16 Hashishe Palô wakabikila Mose ne Alona lubilu, wakamba ne, Nakuenzela Yehowa Nzambi wenu bualu bubi ne nuenu kabidi.
17 Nundekelele bubi buanyi anu musangu eu, nundombele Yehowa Nzambi wenu, yeye angumushile bualu ebu bua lufu.
18 Yeye wakaluhuka kudi Palo, wakalomba Yehowa.
19 Yehowa wakatuma luhehele lukole lua ku wesete, bakabandisha mikumbi, luakayihata mu Dishiba Dikunze, nansha mukumbi umue wakashala mu buloba bua Ejipitu.
20 Kadi Yehowa wakakolesha mutshima wa Palô, yeye kakalekela Bena Isalele.
21 Yehowa wakambila Mose ne, Olola tshianza tshiebe mulu, midima ikale mu buloba bua Ejipitu, bantu babubute mu midima.
22 Mose wakolola tshianza tshiandi mulu ne midima mikole be yakadi mu buloba bua Ejipitu to matuku asatu.
23 Ka bakamanya mua kutangilangana, nansha muntu wakumuka mu muaba wandi tô matuku asatu, kadi munya wakadi mu nsubu ya Bena Isalele bonso.
24 Palô wakabikila Mose, wakamuambila ne, Yai bienu nutendelele Yehowa, shiyai anu mikoko ne ngombe, bana benu bakese baye nʼenu.
25 Mose wakamba ne, Bualu buakukukuata bua kufila nyama mu bianza bietu ya kuoshila Nzambi, tudi basue kuyishihela Yehowa Nzambi wetu.
26 Netuye ne bimuna bietu kabidi, katuena dishiya nansha mukoko umue ku nyima, bualu bua didi ne bualu bua kutendelela Yehowa Nzambi wetu ne nyama ei; katuena bamanye bintu bidi Yehowa musue anu bakafikaku.
27 Kadi Yehowa wa kakolesha mutshima wa Palo, yeye kakabalekela.
28 Palô wakambila Mose ne, Umuka kundi, udimuke biebe, kuntangidi kabidi, mu dituku diudi utangila mpala wanyi wewe neufue.
29 Mose wakamba ne, Wewe wakuamba bimpe, tshiena utangila mpala webe kabidi.

Exodus 11
1 Yehowa wakambila Mose ne, Nentume mulau umue mukuabo kudi Palo ne ha buloba bua Ejipitu; ne ku nyima yeye neanulekele, hanulekeleye, yeye neanuihate tshiendelele.
2 Wambile bantu mu matshu abo ne, Mulume yonso alombe muena mutumba nʼandi, ne mukashi yonso alombe mukashi muena mutumba nʼandi bilenga bia argent ne bilenga bia or.
3 Yehowa wakaha bantu dikasa dimpe kudi Bena Ejipitu Muntu eu Mose wakadi muntu munene be mu buloba bua Ejipitu ne ku mesu kua bena mudimu ba Palô ne ku mesu kua bantu bandi.
4 Mose wakamba ne, Yehowa udi wamba ne, Mundankulu meme nenye munkatshi mua Ejipitu;
5 banʼa bute bonso ba mu buloba bua Ejipitu nebafue, ku muanʼa bute wa Palô udi ushikama ha nkuasa wandi wa bukelenge tô ne kudi muanʼa bute wa mukashi udi uhela manvua andi, banʼa bute ba bimuna bionso nebafue kabidi.
6 Muadi mukole newikale mu buloba bua Ejipitu buonso; kakuena kuanze kuikala muadi bu eu, ne kawena wikalaku kabidi.
7 Kadi nansha mbua kena mua kubululala umue wa Bena Isalele, nansha muntu nansha nyama, bua nuenu mumanye ne, Yehowa udi utahulula Bena Ejipitu ne Bena Isalele.
8 Bantu bebe bonso nebalue kundi, nebankukuile, nebambe ne, Ya biebe ne bantu bonso badi balonda ku nyima kuebe, ne ku nyima kua bualu ebu nempatuke. Wakaluhuka kudi Palô ne tshiji tshikole.
9 Yehowa wakambila Mose ne, Palô kena ukunvuila nansha, bualu bua kuvudisha malu anyi a kukema mu buloba bua Ejipitu.
10 Mose ne Alona bakenza malu â onso a kukema ku mesu kua Palô, Yehowa wakakolesha mutshima wa Palô, yeye bakalekela Bena Isalele mu buloba buandi.

Exodus 12
1 Yehowa wakambila Mose ne Alona mu buloba bua Ejipitu ne,
2 Ngondo eu neikale tshibangidilu tshia ngondo kunudi, neikale ngondo wa kumudilu kua tshidimu kunudi.
3 Nuambile bantu bonso ba mu Isalele ne, Mu dituku dia dikumi dia ngondo eu muntu ne muntu asungule muana wa mukoko ku nsubu ya batatuʼabo, muana wa mukoko ku nsubu muana wa mukoko ku nsubu.
4 Bikala bantu ba mu nsubu banya-banya, muena nsubu neatahulule muana wa mukoko ne muena nsubu udi hehi nʼandi ku bungi bua bantu badi mu nsubu, bobo babale bungi bua bantu badi mua kudia muana wa mukoko umue.
5 Muana mulume wenu wa mukoko neikale muakane ne wa tshidimu tshimue nebamuangate munkatshi mua mikoko anyi mua mbushi.
6 Nenumulame tô ne ku dituku dia dikumi ne anai dia ngondo eu, bantu bonso ba mu Isalele nebamushihe dilolo.
7 Bobo nebangate mashi, nebâmiamine ku mutshi wa ha mutu ha mushiku wa mbelu ne ku biluwa bibidi bia mushiku wa mbelu wa nsubu mudibo bamudîla.
8 Neboshe munyinyi ku kahia butuku abu, nebaudie ne mampa adi kâyi ne luevene, nebaudie ne matamba adi ne bululu.
9 Kanudi munyinyi mubishe, anyi mulamba mu mai a kahia, kadi nuoshe munyinyi mu kahia, mutu wandi ne bibelu biandi ne bia munda biandi.
10 Kanushiyi tshintu tshikese tô ne ku dinda, kadi biashala bintu dinda nenubioshe mu kahia.
11 Nenudie munyinyi nunku ne bilulu bienu biela mikaya, ne bisabata ku makasa ne mulangala ku bianza bienu nenudie munyinyi lubilu, bualu ebu budi kuhita kua Yehowa.
12 Bualu bua meme nempite buloba bua Ejipitu butuku abu, nenshihe banʼa bute bonso ba mu buloba bua Ejipitu ne ba bantu ne ba nyama; nendumbuluishe nzambi yonso ya Ejipitu, meme ndi Yehowa.
13 Mashi neikale bu tshimanymu tshia mu nsubu munudi, ne hanamona mashi, nempite ha mutu henu, ne mulau kawena unukuata hanashiha bantu ba mu buloba bua Ejipitu.
14 Dituku edi nedikale kunudi bu tshmilukidi, nenudilame bu disanka dia Yehowa, nuenu nenuvuluke dituku edi ne tshivulukidi etshi tshiendelele.
15 Nenudie mampa adi kayi ne luevene to matuku muanda mutekete, dituku dia kumudilu nenumushe luevene mu nsubu yenu, muntu yonso udi udia mampa adi neluevene ku dituku dia kumudilu to matuku muanda mutekete, nebamumushe munkatshi mua Bena Isalele.
16 Dituku dia kumudilu nedikale dituku dia disangisha dia tshijila, ne dituku dia muanda mutekete nedikale dituku dia disangisha dia tshijila, kabenji mudimu mu matuku â, anu mudimu wa kudikebela bia kudia.
17 Nuenu nenudie mampa adi kayi ne luevene dituku adi bualu bua nakahatula bisumbu bienu mu buloba bua Ejipitu, nunku nenujidile dituku edi bu tshivulukidi tshienu tshiendelele.
18 Mu ngondo wa kumudilu ne ku dituku dia dikumi ne anai dilolo nenudie mampa adi kâyi ne luevene to ne ku dituku dia makumi abidi ne dimue dilolo.
19 Kanuikadi ne luevene mu nsubu yenu to matuku muanda mutekete, ne muntu yonso udi udia bintu bia luevene, nebamumushe munkatshi mua tshisumbu tshia Bena Isalele; nansha bikaleye muenyi anyi muntu muledibue mu buloba.
20 Kanudi nansha tshintu tshikese tshia luevene, mu nsubu yenu yonso nenudie anu bintu bidi kabiyi ne luevene.
21 Mose Wakabikila bakulu bonso ba Bena Isalele, wakabambila ne, Nusungule bana ba mikoko bungi buai buikale bungi bua meku enu, nuyishihe bua kuhita kua Yehowa.
22 Nuangate mabeshi a hisopa, nutue mu mashi adi mu dilonga, numianune mashi ku mutshi wa ha mutu ha mushiku wa mbelu ne ku biluwa bibidi bia mushiku wa mbelu, muntu kaluhuki mu nsubu wandi tô ne ha tshia butuku.
23 Yehowa neahite bua kushiha Bena Ejipitu, hamoneye mashi ku mutshi wa ha mushiku wa mbelu ne ku biluwa bia mushiku wa nsubu, neaye biandi ha mutu ha tshibi, neahite, Yehowa neahidishe mushiku ne, Kayi mu nsubu yenu bua kunushiha.
24 Nuenu nuenze bualu ebu bu tshilele tshienu ne tshia bana benu tshiendelele.
25 Hanuafika mu buloba bulualua Yehowa kunuha, bu muakalayeye, nuenze tshilele etshi.
26 Hanukonka bana benu ne, Nudi nuenzela tshilele etshi tshinyi?
27 nenubambile ne, Tshidi tshintu tshituha tshia kuhita kua Yehowa, yeye wakahita ha mutu ha nsubu ya Bena Isalele mu Ejipitu, hakashiheye Bena Ejipitu, ne wakasungila nsubu yetu Bantu bakinama mitu yabo, bakatendelela Yehowa.
28 Bena Isalele bakaya, bakenza nunku, bu muakambila Yehowa Mose ne Alona, mbu muakenzabo.
29 Hakalua mundankulu Yehowa wakashiha banʼa bute bonso ba mu buloba bua Ejipitu, muanʼa bute wa Palô wakadi ushikama ha nkuasa wandi wa bukelenge tô ne ku muanʼa bute wa muntu wa mu nsubu wa lukanu, ne bana bonso ba bute ba bimuna.
30 Palô wakabika mu butuku, yeye ne bantu bandi bonso, ne Bena Ejipitu bonso, muadi munene wakadi mu Ejipitu, kamuakadi nsubu wakadi kayi ne mufue.
31 Palô wakabikila Mose ne Alona mu butuku, wakamba ne, Bikai, yai bienu munkatshi mua bantu banyi, ne nuenu ne Bena Isalele, yai bienu nutendelele Yehowa bu munuakamba.
32 Angatai bisumbu bia mikoko ne bia ngombe, bu munuakamba, yai bienu, nunvudishe disanka kabidi.
33 Bena Ejipitu bakenzesha bantu bualu bua kubihata mu buloba lubilu, bakamba ne, Tudi bantu bafue bonso.
34 Bantu bakangata bidia bia mampa diambedi luevene kaluyi luanze kubuelamu, ne binu bidibo benza nʼabi mampa bakabikuta mu bilulu biabo, bakabiteka ha mahaʼabo.
35 Bena Isalele bakenza bu mu dî dia Mose, bakatamba Bena Ejipitu bilenga bia argent ne bia or ne bilulu.
36 Yehowa wakaha bantu dikasa dimpe ku mesu kua Bena Ejipitu, bakabaka bintu biakabalombabo Bakahaula Bena Ejipitu.
37 Bena Isalele bakaya ku Lamesese tô ne ku Sukota, balume bakenda hanshi bakadi bu bununu nkama isambombo, ne bana kabidi.
38 Tshisumbu tshinene tshia bantu bakuabo tshiakaya nʼabo, ne mikoko ne ngombe ne bimuna bia bungi.
39 Bakatamba mikata ya bidia biakadi kabiyi ne luevene biakaluabo nʼabi, kabakadi ne luevene bualu bua bakahitata mu Ejipitu, kabakamanya mua kushala, ne kabakadianjila kudilongoluela bia kudia.
40 Bena Isalele bakashala mu Ejipitu bidimu nkama mai ne makumi asatu.
41 Hakaleha bidimu nkama mai ne makumi asatu, mu dituku adi mene mene, bisumbu bionso bia bantu ba Yehowa bakaluhuka mu buloba bua Ejipitu.
42 Butuku abu budi butuku budibo basue kuyidika Yehowa, bualu bua yeye wakabahatula mu buloba bua Ejipitu ebu mbutuku bua Yehowa budi Bena Isalele bavuluka ku bikondo biabo bionso.
43 Yehowa wakambila Mose ne Alona ne, Etshi ntshilele tshia kuhita kua Yehowa muenyi kena mua kutshidia.
44 Kadi biasumba muntu muhika wandi ku argent, ne hakamutengulabo, yeye udi mua kutshidia.
45 Muenyi ne muena mudimu wa difutu kabena mua kutshidia.
46 Nebadie munyinyi mu nsubu umue, kanuhatuki ne munyinyi ku nsubu, kanutshibudi nansha mufuba umue.
47 Tshisumbu tshionso tshia Bena Isalele netshienze tshilele etshi.
48 Bikala muenyi mushikame nʼenu, udi musue kuenza tshilele tshia kuhita kua Yehowa, nebatengule bana bandi ba balume bonso, nunku yeye udi mua kutshienza, yeye neikale bu muntu muledibue mu buloba buenu, kadi muntu udi kayi mutengula kena mua kutshidia.
49 Mukenji umue newikale kûdi ne kudi muntu muledibue mu buloba buenu ne muenyi udi ushikama munkatshi muenu.
50 Bena Isalele bonso bakenza bu muakambila Yehowa Mose ne Alona.
51 Mu dituku adi mene mene Yehowa wakumuka ne Bena Isalele mu Ejipitu ne bisumbu biabo.

Exodus 13
1 Yehowa wakambila Mose ne,
2 Unjidile banʼa bute bonso ba Bena Isalele, bonso badianjile kuledibua, ne ba bantu ne ba nyama, bobo mbanyi.
3 Mose wakambila bantu ne, Vulukai dituku edi dinuakuhatuka mu Ejipitu, mu nsubu wa lukanu, bualu bua Yehowa wakanuhatula mu muaba eu ne bukole bua tshianza tshiandi, kanudi bidia bidi ne luevene.
4 Nenuhatuke dituku edi mu ngondo wa Abiba.
5 Halua Yehowa nʼenu mu buloba bua Bena Kanana ne Bena Hiti ne Bena Amole ne Bena Hiwi ne Bena Yebuse buakalayeye batatuʼenu ne, Nennuhebu, buloba budi ne mabele ne buitshi bia bungi, nuenu nuvuluke tshilele etshi mu ngondo eu.
6 Nudie mampa adi kayi ne luevene to matuku muanda mutekete, ne dituku dia muanda mutekete nudie bidia ku mesu kua Yehowa.
7 Nudie anu mampa adi kâyi ne luevene to matuku muanda mutekete, kanuikadi ne mampa a luevene, kabatangidi luevene mu nseke yenu yonso.
8 Mu dituku adi nenuambile muanʼebe wa balume ne, Ebu mbualu buwakungenzela Yehowa hanakaluhuka mu buloba bua Ejipitu.
9 Bualu ebu nebuikale bu tshimanyinu ku tshianza tshiebe ne bu tshivulukidi hankatshi ha mesu ebe, bua dî dia Yehowa dikale mukana muenu, bualu bua Yehowa wakanuhatula mu Ejipitu ne tshianza tshikole.
10 Nunku nuenze bualu, ebu bu tshilele tshienu mu ngondo watshi ku tshidimu ku tshidimu.
11 Halua Yehowa nʼenu mu buloba bua Bena Kanâna buakanulayeye ne batatuʼenu ne hanuheye buloba abu,
12 nuenu nenujidile Yehowa banʼa bute bonso ba bantu ne ba nyama kabidi, bana ba balume mbana ba Yehowa.
13 Nuenu nenuhikule muanʼa bute wa nyama wa mpunda ku muana wa mukoko, ne binuahanga kumuhikulai nenutshibule nshingu wandi, nenuhikule banʼa bute bonso ba bantu kabidi.
14 Hanukonka muanʼenu wa balume ne, Bualu ebu ntshinyi? nemmuambile ne, Yehowa wakatuhatula mu buloba bua Ejipitu ne mu nsubu wa lukanu ku bukole bua tshianza tshiandi.
15 Hakamba Palo kuhidia kundekelu, Yehowa wakashiha banʼa bute bonso ba mu buloba bua Ejipitu ne ba bantu ne ba nyama kabidi, nunku ndi mushihele Yehowa banʼa bute ba balume ba nyama, kadi ndi muhikule banʼa bute ba balume banyi.
16 Bualu ebu nebuikale bu tshimanymu ku tshianza tshiebe ne bu tshmilukidi hankatshi ha mesu ebe bualu bua Yehowa wakabihanda mu Ejipitu ku bukole bua tshianza tshiandi.
17 Bakalekela Palô bantu, Nzambi kakabalombola mu nshila wa ku buloba bua Bena Peleshete, nshila au wakatamba buihi, Nzambi wakamba ne, Bamue bantu nebakudimune mitshima yabo hatangilabo nvita, nebahingane mu Ejipitu.
18 Kadi Nzambi wakabalombola mu nshila wa ku tshihela hehi ne Dishiba Dikunze Bena Isalele bakahatuka mu buloba bua Ejipitu ne bintu bia nvita.
19 Mose wakaya ne mifuba ya Yosefe, bualu bua yeye wakatshihisha Bena Isalele ne, Nzambi nealue kunudi bulelela, nuenu nenuye ne mifuba yanyi.
20 Bakabisha luendu luabo ku Sukota, bakashikama mu Etama, ku butshika bua tshihela.
21 Yehowa wakaya ku mpala kuabo mu ditutu, kubalesha nshila, ne butuku mu difutu diakadi ne dinkenke bua kubatemena mu nshila, bua bobo bamanye mua kuya ne munya ne butuku.
22 Ditutu dia mu munya diakadi ne dinkenke butuku kadiakumuka ku mpala kua bantu.

Exodus 14
1 Yehowa wakambila Mose ne,
2 Wambile Bena Isalele ne, Nuhingane, Shikamai ku mpala kua Pi-hahilota, hankatshi ha Migedola ne mai manene, ku mpala kua Bâla-sefona, Shikamai bienu ku mpala kua muaba eu hehi ne mai manene.
3 Palô neambe bua Bena Isalele ne, Bakuhambuka mu nshila, tshihela tshiakubahumbisha.
4 Meme nenkoleshe mutshima wa Palô, yeye neabalonde, nempete butumbi kudi Palô ne kudi bisumbu biandi bionso, Bena Ejipitu nebamanye ne, Meme ndi Yehowa Bakenza nunku.
5 Lumu bakafika kudi mukelenge wa Ejipitu ne, Bantu bakanyema Palô ne bantu bandi bakakudimuna mitshima yabo, bakamba ne, Tuakenza tshinyi hatuakalekela Bena Isalele ku mudimu wetu?
6 Yeye wakalongolola dikalu diandi, wakaya ne bantu bandi.
7 Wakangata makalu nkama isambombo masungula, ne makalu onso makuabo a mu Ejipitu ne tuhita tua makalu onso.
8 Yehowa wakakolesha mutshima wa Palô, mukelenge wa Ejipitu, yeye wakalonda Bena Isalele, bualu bua Bena Isalele bakahatuka ne tshianza tshikole.
9 Bena Ejipitu bakabalonda ne tubalu dionso ne makalu onso bia Palô Masalayi andi ne bantu ba ha nyima ha tubalu bakabaheta hakadibo bashikama hehi ne mai manene, hehi ne Pi-hahilota ku mpala kua Bâla-sefona.
10 Hakalua Palo ha buihi, Bena Isalele bakabandisha mesu abo, bakamona Bena Ejipitu babalonda ku nyima, bakatamba kutshina, Bena Isalele bakadila kudi Yehowa.
11 Bakambila Mose ne, Bualu bua kakuakadi nkita mu Ejipitu, wewe wakulua nʼetu bua tuetu kubala mu tshihela, anyi? Wakutuenzela nunku tshinyi, bua kutuhatula mu Ejipitu?
12 Edi kadiena di dituakukuambila mu Ejipitu ne, Utulekele bua tukuatshile Bena Ejipitu mudimu, anyi? Bua kukuatshila Bena Ejipitu mudimu budi butamba bua kufua mu tshihela buimpe.
13 Mose wakambila bantu ne, Kanutshinyi, imanai, numone luhandu lua Yehowa lualueye kunuha lelu, bua Bena Ejipitu banuakumona lelu kanuena nubamona kabidi tshiendelele.
14 Yehowa neanuluile nvita yenu, nenuikale holâ.
15 Yehowa wakambila Mose ne, Udi udidila kundi tshinyi? Ambila Bena Isalele ne, Baye ku mpala.
16 Wewe ubishe mujangala webe, wolola tshianza tshiebe ha mai, wahandulule, Bena Isalele nebaye munkatshi mua mai ha buloba bûme.
17 Ne tangila, meme nenkoleshe mitshima ya Bena Ejipitu, nebayemu ku nyima kua Bena Isalele, nengangate butumbi bualu bua Palô ne bisumbu biandi bionso ne makalu andi ne masalayi andi adi enda ha tubalu.
18 Bena Ejipitu nebamanye ne, Meme ndi Yehowa, hanakuangata butumbi buanyi bualu bua Palô ne makalu andi ne masalayi andi adi enda ha tubalu.
19 Muanjelo wa Nzambi, wakadi wenda ku mpala kua Bena Isalele, wakumukaku, wakaya ku nyima kuabo, ne difutu diakumuka ku mpala kuabo kabidi, diakimana ku nyima kuabo.
20 Difutu diakadi hankatshi ha Bena Ejipitu ne Bena Isalele, difutu ne midima biakadiku kadi diakadi ne dinkenke butuku Kabikadianganyi ha buihi to butuku buonso.
21 Mose wakolola tshianza tshiandi ha mai, ne Yehowa wakatuma luhehele lukole lua ku esete, wakahingasha mai butuku abu buonso, buloba bûme buakamueneka kuakadi mai, mai akahanduluka.
22 Bena Isalele bakaya mu bunene bua mai ha buloba bûme, mai akadi bu tshinunu kudibo ku tshianza tshia balume ne ku tshianza tshia bakashi.
23 Bena Ejipitu bakamba kubihata, bakabalonda ku nyima kuabo mu bunene bua mai, tubalu tua Palô bionso ne makalu andi onso ne masalayi andi onso akadi enda ha tubalu.
24 Hakatshia butuku, Yehowa wakatangila bisumbu bia Bena Ejipitu mu ditutu diakadi ne dinkenke dia kahia, wakabatshinyisha.
25 Wakakanga nkata ya makalu, kabakamanya mua kuya bimpe, Bena Ejipitu bakamba ne, Tunyeme bietu ku mesu kua Bena Isalele, bualu bua Yehowa udi ubaluila nvita yabo, udi uluangana ne Bena Ejipitu.
26 Yehowa wakambila Mose ne, Olola tshianza tshiebe ha mai, mai ahingane kabidi ha mutu ha Bena Ejipitu ne ha makalu abo ne ha masalayi abo adi enda ha tubalu.
27 Mose wakolola tshianza tshiandi ha mai, ne hakatshia butuku mai akahingana kabidi ne bukole buau, Bena Ejipitu bakanyema ne Yehowa wakahita Bena Ejipitu bukole mu bunene bua mai.
28 Mai akahingana, akabanda ha mutu ha makalu ne ha mutu ha masalayi akadi enda ha tubalu, ha mutu ha bisumbu bionso bia Palô, biakabalonda mu bunene bua mai, nansha umue wabo wakahandika.
29 Kadi Bena Isalele bakenda mu buloba bûme munkatshi mua mai, mai akadi bu tshimanu kudibo ku tshianza tshia balume ne ku tshianza tshia bakashi.
30 Nunku Yehowa wakasungila Bena Isalele mu bianza bia Bena Ejipitu dituku adi Bena Isalele bakatangila Bena Ejipitu bafue kukala kua mai manene.
31 Bena Isalele bakatangila bualu bukole buakenzela Yehowa Bena Ejipitu, ne bantu bonso bakatshina Yehowa, bakeyemena Yehowa ne muntu wandi Mose.

Exodus 15
1 Mose ne Bena Isalele balambila Yehowa musambu eu, bakamba ne,
2 Yehowa udi bukole buanyi ne musambu wanyi,
3 Yehowa udi muena nvita,
4 Wakela makalu a Palô ne bisumbu biandi bionso mu mai manene,
5 Mai adi ha mutu habo,
6 Yehowa, tshianza tshiebe tshia balume tshidi ne butumbi ne bukole,
7 Mu bunene bua buakane buebe, wewe utu wahita bantu bukole badi badilesha ku mesu kuebe,
8 Muhuya webe wakabandisha mai, akimana bu tshimama,
9 Muena lukuna wakamba ne, Nembalonde, nembahete,
10 Wewe wakatuma luhehele luebe, ne mai manene akababuikila,
11 Nganyi udi bu wewe Yehowa, munkatshi mua nzambi mikuabo?
12 Wewe wakolola tshianza tshiebe
13 Mu luse luebe wewe wakalombela bantu bawakuhikula,
14 Bantu bakunvua lumu, bakutshina,
15 Bakelenge ba Edome bakukema,
16 Buowa ne ditshina biakubakuata,
17 Wewe neulue nʼabo, neubateke mu mukuna wa buhianyi buebe,
18 Yehowa neakokeshe bantu tshiendelele.
19 Bualu bua tubalu tua Palo ne makalu andi ne masalayi akadi enda ha nyima ha tubalu biakaya mu mai manene ne Yehowa wakahingasha mai ha mutu habo, kadi Bena Isalele bakenda ha buloba bûme munkatshi mua mai manene.
20 Mîyama, muprofete mukashi, muanʼabo mukashi ne Alona, wakangata tshisanji mu tshianza tshiandi, ne bakashi bonso bakamulonda ne bisanji, benda batuhika.
21 Mîyama wakabambila ne,
22 Mose wakalombela bantu bua kuyabo ku mpala bumuke ku Dishiba Dikunze, bakaya mu tshihela tshia Shû bakaya mu tshihela matuku asatu, kabakasangana mai.
23 Hakaluabo ku Mala kabakamanya mua kunua mai a Mala, mai akadi ne bululu, nunku balodiha muaba au ne, Mala.
24 Bantu baka tontolola Mose ne, Netunue tshinyi?
25 Yeye wakadila kudi Yehowa, ne Yehowa wakamulesha mu tshi, wakawela mu mai, mai akalua mimpe.
26 wakabambila ne, Binuatumikila di dia Yehowa Nzambi wenu, binuenza malu adi mimpe ku mesu kuandi, ne binuateleja matshu kudi mikenji yandi, binuenza mei andi onso, tshiena nnutumina nansha disama dimue dinakatumina Bena Ejipitu, bualu bua meme ndi Yehowa udi unuondaha.
27 Bakalua ku Elima kuakadi mpokolo ya mai dikumi ne ibidi, ne mabue makumi muanda mutekete bakashikamaku hehi ne mai.

Exodus 16
1 Bakabika mu Elima ne Bena Isalele bonso, bakalua mu tshihela, dina diatshi Sina, hankatshi ha Elima ne Sinai. Bakafika nunku dituku dia dikumi ne ditanu dia ngondo muibidi hakumukabo mu buloba bua Ejipitu.
2 Bena Isalele buonso buabo bakajiana Mose ne Alona mu tshihela.
3 Bena Isalele bakabambila ne, Biakadi bimpe bua tuetu kufua mu Ejipitu ku tshianza tshia Yehowa hatuakashikama hadi bivuadi bia munyinyi ne hatuakukutshika mafu ne bidia, kadi nuenu nuakuluila nʼetu mu tshihela etshi bualu bua kushiha tshisumbu etshi tshionso ne nzala.
4 Yehowa wakambila Mose ne, Mona, nennulokeshile bidia bia mu diulu, bantu nebaye, nebabisangishe ku dituku bikumbane ku dituku bikumbane. Nembatete ne badi benda mu di dianyi, anyi?
5 Mu dituku disambombo nebalongolole bidibo bangate, bikale tshituha ha mutu ha tshituha tshidibo basangisha ku dituku ku dituku.

25 udi muoshe mukuabo kahia nebamuoshe kabidi, udi utaha mukuabo mputa nebamutahe biandi, udi ukuma mukuabo mibundabunda nebamukume biandi.
26 Biatuta muntu muhika wandi mulume anyi muhika wandi mukashi ku disu, ne biafuadi, yeye neamumushe ku butaka buandi bualu bua disu diandi.
27 Biahuoleye dinu dia muhika wandi mulume anyi muhika wandi mukashi, yeye neamumushe ku buhika buandi bualu bua dinu diandi.
28 Biakuma ngombe mulume anyi mukashi ne nsengu yandi ne biafueye, nebakume ngombe mabue, kabadi munyinyi wandi nansha, kadi muena ngombe neabinge.
29 Kadi bikala ngombe muikale ne tshilele tshia kukuma bantu nsengu, ne bakamanyisha mukelenge wandi, ne yeye wakahidia kumukanda, biashiheye nansha mulume nansha mukashi, nebakume ngombe ne mabue, nebashihe mukelenge wandi kabidi.
30 Kadi biatekabo biuma bia kudihikula kudiye, yeye neafile biuma bionso bidibo balomba bua kuhikula muoyo wandi.
31 Bikaleye mushihe nansha muana wa balume nansha muana wa bakashi, nebamuenzele nunku bu mudi dilumbulula edi.
32 Biakuma ngombe muhika mulume anyi muhika mukashi ne nsengu, nebafute mukelenge wabo nshekele
33 Biumusha muntu tshibuikidi tshia ha mutu ha dina, anyi biumbuleye dina kayi uteka tshibuikidi ha mutu, ne biahonamu ngombe anyi kabalu,
34 muena dina neashikishe bualu bimpe, neahe muenʼabi biuma, ne nyama mufue neikale wandi.
35 Biakuma ngombe wa muntu ngombe wa muntu mukuabo, biamushiheye, bobo nebahane ngombe udi ne muoyo, nebabanyangane mushinga wandi, nebabanyangane ngombe mufue, kabidi.
36 Anyi bikala ngombe muikale ne tshilele tshia kukuma ne nsengu diambedi, ne bikala mukelenge wandi kayi mumulame, yeye neafute ngombe ku ngombe, ne ngombe mufue neikale wandi.

Exodus 22
1 Biba muntu ngombe anyi mukoko, biamushiheye anyi biamuhaneye, yeye neahingashe ngombe itanu ku ngombe umue, ne nukoko mai ku mukoko umue.
2 Biasanganabo muibi mubuele mu nsubu ne biamukumabo ne biafueye, kabena benze mushiku bualu.
3 Biamukuatabo mu munya, nebamusombuele, yeye neahingashe bintu, ne bikaleye kayi ne bintu, nebamuhane bua buibi buandi.
4 Biasanganabo bintu bidi ne muoyo mu tshianza tshiandi, nansha ngombe nansha mpunda nansha mukoko, yeye neahingashe bintu misangu ibidi.
5 Bialekela muntu ngombe wandi mu budimi bua muntu mukuabo, ne biadieye mitshi yandi ya tumuma tukese, yeye neamuhingashile bintu bidi bitamba buimpe bia mu budimi buandi ne bia mu budimi buandi bua nvinyo.
6 Biosha kahia bisumbu bia luosa anyi luosa lua mu budimi, muntu wakatemesha kahia neafute.
7 Biafila muntu argent anyi biuma mu bianza bia mukuandi bua kubilama, ne biabibabo mu nsubu muandi, biasanganabo muibi, yeye neabafute bintu misangu ibidi.
8 Kadi bikalabo kabayi basangana muibi, nunku muena nsubu nealue biandi kudi balumbuluishi, bua bobo bajingulule ne yeye wakalenga biuma bia mukuandi tshianza, anyi.
9 Biafuilanganabo bua tshintu tshionso, nansha ngombe nansha mpunda nansha mukoko nansha bilulu nansha bintu bikuabo bidi bantu bamba ne, Ebu mbualu bua bantu baye kudi balumbuluishi, muntu udi balumbuluishi bahishe yeye neafute mukuandi bintu misangu ibidi.
10 Biafila muntu nansha nyama wa mpunda nansha ngombe nansha mukoko nansha nyama mukuabo kudi mukuandi bua kumiamina, ne biafua nyama, anyi biamutahabo mputa, anyi biamuihatabo ne muntu kayi mumutangile,
11 ditshiha dia Yehowa nedikale hankatshi ha bubidi buabo bua kujingulula ne, Yeye kakalenga bintu bia mukuandi tshianza, ne bitabusha mukelenge wa nyama au nunku, mukuandi kena ne bualu bua kumufuta.
12 Kadi bibabo nyama mu tshianza tshiandi, yeye neafute mukelenge wandi.
13 Bikala nyama muhandibue bihese, yeye nealue ne munyinyi wandi bu tshimanymu tshia bualu, ne yeye kena ufuta bua nyama muhandibue.
14 Biasombesha muntu nansha nyama kai kudi mukuandi, ne biamutahabo mputa anyi biafueye ne muenʼandi kayiku, yeye nealamate bulelela.
15 Kadi bikalaku muenʼandi, yeye kena ne bualu bua kumufuta. Bikaleye mufutshile nyama au bua kuenza nʼandi mudimu, mushinga wakumufutshileye neukumbane.
16 Biateya mulume mukashi utshidi kamama, udi kayi muanze kutangila kudi mulume, ne bialaleye nʼandi, yeye neafile biuma bia dibaka bulelela bua yeye kuikaleye mukashi wandi.
17 Biahidia tatu wa mukashi kumufila kudiye, yeye neafute biuma bu bia dibaka dia bakashi batshidi kamama.
18 Kuitabushi bua muena milongo mukashi ashale ne muoyo.
19 Muntu yonso udi ulala ne nyama nebanshihe bulelela.
20 Muntu udi ushihela nzambi mikuabo nyama, kayi uyishihela Yehowa nkayandi, nebamushihe yeye.
21 Kuenzedi muenyi bualu bubi, kumukengeshi, bualu bua nuenu nuakadi benyi mu buloba bua Ejipitu.
22 Kanukengeshi bakashi ba mu lufuila anyi bana ba nshiya.
23 Biwabakengesha ne biadilabo kundi, meme nengunvuile muadi wabo,
24 ne tshiji tshianyi netshitambe bukole, nennushihe ne muele wa nvita, ne bakashi benu nebikale bakashi ba mu lufuila ne bana benu nebikale bana ba nshiya.
25 Biwasombesha mukuabo muhele wa munkatshi mua bantu banyi argent, kumubadi bu muena dibanza, kumulombi tshia kasombelu.
26 Biwangata tshilulu tshia mukuenu bu tshieya, wewe neutshihingashe kudiye diambedi diba kadiyi dianzekubuela,
27 bualu bua tshiotshi tshidi anu tshiandi tshia kudibuikila nʼatshi, yeye nealale tulu munyi? Hadileye kundi, nemmunvuile, bualu bua meme ndi ne luse.
28 Kutedi dina dia Nzambi tshitelatela, kuhendi mukokeshi wa bantu bebe.
29 Kunengi bua kufila bintu bia kunowa kudi Nzambi, ne nvinyo ûdi ukama.
30 Enzai nunku ne ngombe yenu ne mikoko yenu kabidi, muana neikale ne mamu wandi matuku muanda mutekete, kadi numufile kundi dituku dia muanda mukulu.
31 Nuenu nenuikale bu bantu bajidila kundi kanudi munyinyi wakahandabo kudi nyama ya mu mpata, nuwele kudi mbua.

Exodus 23
1 Kumanyishi bakuabo lumu ludi kaluyi lululela. Kubuelakanyi ne bantu babi bualu bua kuikala bu mumanyi wa malu adi kayi malelela.
2 Kulondi ku nyima kua tshisumbu tshia bantu tshinene bua kuenza malu mabi, kuikadi bu mu manyi wa bualu munkatshi mua bantu badi basangisha dî diabo bua kudibingisha mu tshilumbu tshidi ka tshiyi tshilelela.
3 Kueyemenyi buhele bua muntu muhele mu dikosa dia tshilumbu tshiandi.
4 Biwasangana nansha ngombe nansha mpunda bia muntu udi nʼebe lukuna biya ha bule, uhingashe nyama wandi kudiye kabidi.
5 Biwatangila mpunda wa muntu udi nʼebe lukuna muhone muinshi mua bujitu buandi, kushiya mpunda nunku, kadi umukuatshishe ne bujitu buandi.
6 Kuhidi kubingisha muntu muhele mu tshilumbu tshiakane tshidiye nʼatshi bualu bua buhele buandi.
7 Shila malu a mushimi kaba, kanushihi muntu muimpe udi kayi ne bualu meme tshiena mbingisha muntu mubi.
8 Kanuitabushi tshintu tshia mbueshilu, bualu bua tshintu tshia mbueshilu tshidi tshifofomesha muntu udi ne mesu matabale, tshidi tshikudimuna mei a muntu muakane.
9 Kanukengeshi muenyi wa munkatshi muenu, bualu bua nuenu nudi bamanye mutshima wa muenyi, nuenu nuakadi benyi mu buloba bua Ejipitu.
10 Mu bidimu bisambombo nukune bintu mu buloba, nuhuole bintu bidi bikumbana,
11 kadi tshidimu tshia muanda mutekete nushiye buloba bua kututuka ne bualu bua bantu bahele ba munkatshi muenu badie bintu, ne bintu bidibo bashiye, nyama ya mu mpata neyibidie. Nuenze mitshi yenu ya tumuma tukese nunku ne mitshi ya olive kabidi.
12 Nuenze mudimu wenu matuku asambombo kadi nuikishe mu dituku dia muanda mutekete, bua ngombe webe ne mpunda webe bikishe ne muana wa balume wa muhika webe mukashi, ne muenyi basangale.
13 Nudimuke mu malu onso anakanuambila, kanuambilanganyi dina dia nzambi mikuabo, bantu bakuabo kabunvu dina adi mukana muebe.
14 Nuenu nenudie bidia bia disanka ku mesu kuanyi misangu isatu ku tshidimu ku tshidimu.
15 Nenudie bidia bidi kabiyi ne luevene matuku muanda mutekete, mu tshikondo tshinakasungula mu ngondo wa Abiba (bualu bua nuakaluhuka mu Ejipitu mu ngondo au) muntu kalu ku mesu kuanyi ne bianza bituhu,
16 nenudie bidia bia disanka hanuahuola bintu bia kumudilu binuakakuna mu madimi, nudie bidia bia disanka kabidi kunshikidilu kua tshidimu hanuashikisha kusangisha bintu bionso bia mu madimi.
17 Balume bonso nebalue ku mesu kua Mukelenge Nzambi misangu isatu ku tshidimu ku tshidimu.
18 Kanuntendeledi ne mashi a nyama mishiha ne bidia bidi ne luevene hohamue kanushiyi manyi a nyama ya milambu yanyi to ne ku dinda
19 Bintu bia kumudilu binudi nu huola mu madimi, nulue nʼabi mu nsubu wa Yehowa, Nzambi wenu. Kulambi muana wa mukoko ne mabele a mamu wandi.
20 Mona, ndi nfuma muanjelo ku mpala kuebe, bua kukulama mu nshila ne bua kulua nʼebe mu muaba unakakulongoluela.
21 Udimuke ku mpala kuandi, unvue di diandi, kumufuishishi tshiji, yeye kena ukubuikidila bubi buebe bualu bua dina dianyi didi munda muandi.
22 Kadi biwunvua di diandi ne biwenza mei onso andi nkuambila, nunku meme nengikale bu muena lukuna kudi bantu badi nʼebe lukuna, ne bu muena nvita kudi bantu badi basue kusangana nʼebe.
23 Bualu bua muanjelo wanyi neaye ku mpala kuebe, nealue nʼebe kudi Bena Amole ne kudi Bena Pelizi ne kudi Bena Hiti ne kudi Bena Kanana ne kudi Bena Hiwi ne kudi Bena Yebuse, ne meme nembashihe.
24 Kuinaminyi nzambi yabo, kuyitendeledi, kuenji mudibo benza, kadi ubiele hanshi, ushihe bibumba biabo.
25 Nuenu nenutendelele Yehowa, Nzambi wenu, yeye neayudishe disanka ku bidia bienu ne ku mai enu, nennumushile disama munkatshi muenu.
26 Kakuena mukashi udi utula difu, kakuena mukashi udi wikala nkumba mu buloba buenu, nenkumbashe bungi bua matuku enu.
27 Nentume dishina dianyi ku mpala kuebe, nentshinyishe bantu bonso baudi ufika kudibo, nenkobamishe bantu bonso badi nʼebe lukuna nyima yabo.
28 Nentume tuishi ku mpala kuebe bualu bua kukuihatshila Bena Hiwi ne Bena Kanâna ne Bena Hiti.
29 Tshiena mbihata mu tshidimu tshimue bua buloba kabulu tshianana, ne nyama ya mu mpata kayikumbanyi ku mesu kuebe.
30 Nembihate bilulu bitulu tô ne hakakumbana ba bungi ne hanuahiana buloba.
31 Nenteke mikalu yenu ku Dishiba Dikunze tô ne ku mai manene a Bena Peleshete, ne ku tshihela tô ne ku musulu wa Pelata, bualu bua meme nenfile bena buloba mu bianza bienu, nuenu nenubihate ku mpala kuenu.
32 Kuhungi nʼabo tshihungidi anyi ne nzambi yabo.
33 Bobo kabena bikala mu buloba buebe bualu bua bobo nebakuenzeshe bua kungenzela malu mabi, biwatendelela nzambi yabo, bualu ebu nebuikale buteyi kûdi.

Exodus 24
1 Yehowa wakambila Mose ne, Ulue biebe kudi Yehowa ne wewe ne Alona ne Nadaba ne Abihu ne bakulu ba Bena Isalele makumi muanda mutekete, nuntendelele ha bule.
2 Mose nealue nkayandi hehi ne Yehowa, kadi bobo kabalu hehi nansha, bantu kabayi nʼandi.
3 Mose wakalua, wakamanyisha bantu mei onso akamba Yehowa, ne mikenji yandi yonso, ne bantu bonso bakitaba dî dimue ne, Mei onso akutuambila Yehowa netuênze.
4 Mose wakafunda mei onso a Yehowa mu mukanda, wakabika ne dinda dionso, wakasa tshioshelu kuinshi kua mukuna, ne mabue manene dikumi ne abidi bu bungi bua bisamba bia Bena Isalele.
5 Yeye wakatuma bansongalume ba Bena Isalele, bakoshila Yehowa nyama, bakamushihela ngombe ya milambu ya kuvuisha nʼai ditalala.
6 Mose wakangata tshituha tshibidi tshia mashi, wakateka mu malonga, wakamiamina tshituha tshikuabo tshibidi ha tshioshelu.
7 Wakangata mukanda wa dihungila, wakaubala ku mpala kua bantu, bobo bakamba ne, Mei onso akuamba Yehowa netuênze, netuatumikile.
8 Mose wakangata mashi, wakâmiamma hamʼbidi ha bantu, wakamba ne, Tangilai mashi a dihungila, diakuhunga nʼenu Yehowa bua mei au onso.
9 Hashishe, Mose wakabanda ne Alona ne Nadaba ne Abihu ne bakulu ba Bena Isalele makumi muanda mutekete.
10 Bakamona Nzambi wa Isalele, muinshi mua makasa andi muakadi mabue a saphir, akadi matoke tô bu mubidi wa diulu.
11 Yeye kakalenga tshianza tshiandi ha bantu banene ba Bena Isalele, bakamona Nzambi, bakadia bidia, bakanua.
12 Yehowa wakambila Mose ne, Ulue biebe kundi ha mukuna, wikaleku, nenkuhe mabue adi ne mikenji inakufunda, bua wewe kuyiyisha bantu.
13 Mose wakabika ne Yoshua mukuatshishi wandi, Mose wakabanda mu mukuna wa Nzambi.
14 Yeye wakambila bakulu ne, Nutuindile bienu kunoko tô ne ha bialua kunudi kabidi, ne monai, Alona ne Hû badi nʼenu, muntu udi ne tshilumbu aye biandi kudibo.
15 Mose wakabanda mu mukuna ne difutu diakabuikila mukuna.
16 Butumbi bua Yehowa buakikala ha mukuna wa Sinai, ne difutu diakaubuikila matuku asambombo Dituku dia muanda mutekete yeye wakabikila Mose munkatshi mua ditutu.
17 Dimueneka dia butumbi bua Yehowa diakadi bu kahia kakole ha mutu ha mukuna ku mesu kua Bena Isalele.
18 Mose wakabuela munkatshi mua difutu, wakabanda mu mukuna Mose wakashala mu mukuna munya ne butuku mafuku makumi anai.

Exodus 25
1 Yehowa wakambila Mose ne,
2 Wambile Bena Isalele bua kumpeshabo bintu bia kuntendelela nʼabi, nenuangate kudi muntu ne muntu bintu bidi mutshima wandi musue bua kuntendelela nʼabi.
3 Ebi mbintu binudi mua kuangata kudibo or ne argent ne tshiamu tshia mitaku,
4 ne bilulu bia bleu ne bilulu bifikuluke ne bilulu bikunze-kunzû ne bilulu bia lin ne miosa ya mbushi,
5 ne biseba bia bimpanga bina mu mai makunze, ne biseba bia nkonge ne mutshi wa acacia,
6 ne manyi a muinda, manananshi adibo bela mu manyi a kulaba hamʼbidi, ne manyi adi atua dihembu,
7 mabue a mushinga mukole adibo bateka mu efoda ne mu tshiamu tshidibo baluata ha tshiadi.
8 Bobo bangashile tshitendelelu, bua meme kuikala munkatshi muabo.
9 Nutshiase bu bintu binakakulesha ne bu tshidikishilu tshia tshitendelelu ne bu tshidikishilu tshia bintu bia kutekamu.
10 Bobo nebenze mushete wa mutshi wa acacia buludi bua mushete nebuikale coudées
11 Wewe neulamike tshiamu tshia or mutambe buimpe ha nyima honso ne munda mua mushete kabidi, neuteke tshiamu tshia or ku mibangu ya mushete.
12 Enza biebe nkanu ya or mai, witeka kû mikolo ya mushete, ibidi ku luseke lumue, ibidi ku luseke lukuabo.
13 Enza biebe mitshi ya acacia, lamika tshiamu tshia or ku nseke yai yonso.
14 Neubueshe mitshi ai mu nkanu idi ku nseke ya mushete, bua kutuala nʼai mushete.
15 Mitshi ai neyikale mu nkanu ya mushete, kayumuki nansha.
16 Wewe neuteke mu mushete mabue a dihungila andi nkuha.
17 Wewe newenze tshibuikilu ne tshiamu tshia or mutambe buimpe, buludi buatshi nebuikale coudées ibidi ne tshituha ne butshiama buatshi nebuikale coudée umue ne tshituha.
18 Enza biebe bakeluba babidi ne or mutudibue, ubateke ku matshika a tshibuikilu tshia mushete.
19 Enza mukeluba umue ku butshika bumue ne mukeluba mukuabo ku butshika bukuabo enza bakeluba ne tshibuikilu tshia mushete ne tshituha tshijima tshia or.
20 Bakeluba nebolole mahuahua abo kulu, nebabuikile tshibuikilu tshia mushete ne mahuahua abo, bobo batangilangana mpala, mpala yabo neyikale mitangile ha tshibuikilu tshia mushete.
21 Wewe neuteke tshibuikilu ha mutu ha mushete neubueshe mabue a dihungila andi nkuha mu mushete.
22 Meme ne nsangakene nʼebe aha, nensombe nʼebe ha mutu ha tshibuikilu tshia mushete, hankatshi ha bakeluba badi ha mutu ha mushete, nenkumanyishe malu onso andi nkuhesha bu mikenji kudi Bena Isalele.
23 Wewe newenze mesa a mutshi wa acacia buludi buau nebuikale coudées ibidi, ne butshiama buau nebuikale coudée umue, ne bule buau bua mulu nebuikale coudée umue ne tshituha.
24 Wewe neulamike tshiamu tshia or mutambe buimpe mu nseke yonso, ne ku mpenge kabidi.
25 Enza mushonyi wa or bunene bua butshiama bua tshianza ku matshika onso, enza tshintu tshia or ku matengu onso.
26 Enza nkanu ya or mai, ne teka nkanu ku mikolo mai ya mesa.
27 Teka nkanu hehi ne mpenge ya mesa bualu bua kutuala nʼai mesa
28 Enza biebe mitshi ya acacia, ulamike or ku nseke yai yonso, bua kutuala nʼai mesa.
29 Wewe newenze malonga ne nkuta ne mpanza ne bihanza bia or mutambe buimpe.
30 Teka bidia bilesha ha mesa au matuku onso ku mpala kuanyi.
31 Wewe newenze tshitekelu tshia minda tshia or mutambe buimpe, nebenze tshitekelu etshi ne or muhite, ne tshishikaminu tshiatshi ne mukolo watshi, tukoho tuatshi ne mahu atshi ne bilongo biatshi nebenze ne tshituha tshimue tshia or mene tshiakenzabo nʼatshi tshitekelu.
32 Enza mahu asambombo ha tshitekelu, mahu asatu ku luseke lumue, mahu asatu ku luseke lukuabo.
33 Uzole tukoho tusatu bu bilongo bia amande mu ditamba dimue, dihu ne tshilongo dihu ne tshilongo, uzole tukoho tusatu bu bilongo bia amande mu ditamba dikuabo, dihu ne tshilongo dihu ne tshilongo, uzole nunku ku matamba asambombo adi masa ku tshitekelu.
34 Enza tukoho binai tua ha tshitekelu bu bilongo bia amande ne mahu atshi ne bilongo bia tshi.
35 Enza dihu ne tshituha tshimue tshia or hankatshi ha mahu abidi, hankatshi ha mahu abidi ku mahu asambombo a ha tshitekelu tshia minda.
36 Bintu ebi bionso ubienze ne tshituha tshimue tshia or, tshintu tshijima tshienza ne tshituha tshimue tshia or muedibue mutambe buimpe.
37 Enza minda muanda mutekete, bualu bua kutemenayi muaba udi ku mpala kua tshitekelu.
38 Enza mpandu ne tulonga tukese bia tsiutekelu ne or mutambe buimpe.
39 Enza tshitekelu ne bintu ebi bionso ne ntalanta
40 Udimuke biebe bua kuenza bintu bionso bu tshidikishilu tshinakukulesha mu mukuna.

Exodus 26
1 Wewe newase tshitendelelu ne bilulu binene dikumi bia lin mutambe buimpe, bilulu bia bleu ne bilulu bikunzubile ne bilulu bikunze-kunzû, ubienze ne bakeluba benjibue ne lungenyi lule.
2 Bule bua tshilulu ne tshilulu nebuikale coudées
3 Bilulu bitanu nebikale bituangane ne bilulu bitanu bikuabo, binga bilulu bitanu nebikale bituangane ne bilulu bitanu bikuabo.
4 Enza makoha ne muonshi wa bleu ku mibangu ya bilulu idi ituangana, ku tshisumbu ne ku tshisumbu tshia bilulu.
5 Enza makoha makumi atanu ku mibangu ya tshilulu ne makoha makumi atanu ku mibangu ya tshilulu tshikuabo tshidi tshituangana nʼatshi, makoha ikale makanangane.
6 Enza mbote ya or makumi atanu ku mubangu wa tshilulu tshimue, ne mesu a mbote makumi atanu ku mubangu wa tshilulu tshikuabo, bua kutuangasha bilulu, tshitendelelu netshikale bu tshilulu tshimue.
7 Enza biebe bilulu bia miosa ya mbushi dikumi ne tshimue bualu bua kuteka ha mutu ha tshitendelelu.
8 Bule bua tshilulu tshimue nebuikale coudées makumi asatu, ne butshiama bua tshi coudées mai, idikisha bilulu dikumi ne tshimue ne luidi lumue.
9 Utuangashe bilulu bitanu ha buabi, ne utuangashe bilulu bisambombo ha buabi, tshilulu tshisambombo netshikale tshihitakane ku mushiku wa tshitendelelu.
10 Enza makoha makumi atanu ku mibangu ya tshilulu, ne makoha makumi atanu ku mibangu ya tshilulu tshikuabo tshidi tshituangane katshi.
11 Enza mbote makumi atanu ya tshiamu tshia mitaku, ne mesu a mbote ku mibangu ya tshilulu tshikuabo bualu bua kutuangasha bilulu, bua ntenta ikale bu tshilulu tshimue.
12 Tshituha tshia tshilulu tshidi tshibita tshisonga netshilembelele hanshi ku nyima kua tshitendelelu.
13 Tshituha tshidi bule bua coudée ku luseke lumue ne tshituha tshidi bule bua coudée tshiota tshisonga netshikale ku luseke lua tshitendelelu bua kutshibuikila.
14 Enza biebe tshintu tshia biseba bia bimpanga biaka tuabo mu mai makunze-kunzû ne tshia biseba bia mbushi tshia kuteka ha musonga wa tshitendelelu.
15 Uhande mabaya a mitshi ya acacia bua kuimanyika kulu mu tshitendelelu.
16 Bule bua dibaya dimue nebuikale coudées dikumi ne butshiama buadi nebuikale coudée umue ne tshituha.
17 Mabaya abidi neikale ne mpoko ibidi bua kutuângasha, enza mabaya onso a tshitendelelu nunku.
18 Enza mabaya makumi abidi bua kuteka ku luseke lua ku Sude lua tshitendelelu.
19 Enza biamu bidi ne mahoka ne tshiamu tshia argent makumi anai ku mabaya makumi abidi, mahoka abidi ku dibaya ne ku dibaya, bualu bua kuelamu tutshi tua tutuhu.
20 Enza mabaya makumi abidi a kutoka ku luseke lua tshitendelelu lua ku nôde,
21 ne biamu bia mahoka ne tshiamu tshia argent makumi anai, mahoka abidi ku dibaya dimue, bualu bua kuelamu tutshi tua bituhu.
22 Enza mabaya asambombo a kutoka ku wesete ku luseke lua ku nyima.
23 Enza mabaya abidi a kutoka ku matumba a tshitendelelu adi ku nyima.
24 Mabaya ikale mashilangane kuinshi ne matuangane kulu, ku dikoha dia kumudilu, bubidi buau neikale nunku, teka mabaya au ku matumba a tshitendelelu.
25 Enza mabaya muanda mukulu nunku, ne biamu bia mahoka bia argent, mabaya dikumi ne asambombo, biamu bibidi ku dibaya ne ku dibaya.
26 Enza biebe mitshi ya acacia ya kuela ku mabaya atanu adi ku luseke lumue lua tshitendelelu,
27 mitshi itanu ya kuela ku mabaya adi ku luseke lumue ne mitshi itanu ya kuela ku mabaya adi ku luseke lukuabo, ne mitshi itanu ya kuela ku mabaya adi ku nyima.
28 Mutshi udi hankatshi ha mabaya neuhite ku ditumba dimue tô ne ku ditumba dikuabo.
29 Lamika tshiamu tshia or ku nseke yonso ya mabaya ne enza nkanu ya or ya kuelamu mitshi, lamika or ku nseke yonso ya mitshi ei.
30 Wewe neushimike tshitendelelu bu mudi tshileshilu tshinakukulesha mu mukuna.
31 Enza biebe tshilulu tshinene tshia buanda bua bleu ne bukunzubile ne bukunze-kunzu ne bua lin udi mutambe buimpe utshienzeshe kudi bantu badi ne lungenyi lule badi bamanye mua kuenza tshifuanyikishi tshia bakeluba mu tshilulu atshi.
32 Utshilembeleshe ha makunshi a acacia anai adi ne or ku nseke yonso, teka nkanu ya or ku tshilulu, ku bitubuidi binai bia argent bia ha makunshi.
33 Ulembeleshe tshilulu ha bitubuidi, Ulue ne mushete wa Nzambi ku nyima kua tshilulu, tshilulu atshi netshikale hankatshi ha muaba wa tshijila ne muaba mutambe wa tshijila.
34 Wewe neuteke tshibuikilu ha mutu ha mushete wa Nzambi mu muaba mutambe wa tshijila.
35 Teka mesa ku mpala kua tshilulu, ne teka muinda ku luseke lua tshitendelelu ludi ku Sude, neuteke mesa ku luseke lua ku nôde.
36 Enza biebe tshilulu tshia kuteka ku mbelu kua tshitendelelu tshia buanda bua bleu ne bukunzubile ne bukunze-kunzû ne bua lin mutambe buimpe, utshienzeshe kudi muntu udi ne lungenyi lule lua kulengesha.
37 Enza biebe makunshi atanu a acacia adi ne or ku nseke yonso enza bitubuidi biau ne or, ne enza nkanu ya tshiamu tshia mitaku itanu ya kuelaku.

Exodus 27
1 Enza biebe tshioshelu tshia mabaya a acacia, buludi bua tshi nebuikale coudées
2 Enza bintu bu nsengu ya ngombe ku matumba anai, enza nsengu ne dibaya dimue, lamika tshiamu tshia mitaku ku nseke yonso.
3 Enza bivuadi bia tshiamu bia kumusha nʼabi butue bua tshioshelu, ne bikasu biatshi ne malonga atshi ne bitubuidi bia kukuata nʼabi munyinyi, ne bihanza biatshi, enza bintu ebi bionso ne tshiamu tshia mitaku.
4 Enza tshiamu tshilukibue bu munyingu ne nkanu mai ya tshiamu tshia mitaku ku matumba atshi anai.
5 Teka tshiamu etshi munda mua nseke mai ya tshioshelu ne hankatshi, mu bule buatshi.
6 Enza mitshi ya acacia ya kuela ku tshioshelu, lamika tshiamu tshia mitaku ku nseke yonso ya mitshi.
7 Buesha mitshi ei mu nkanu idi ku nseke ibidi ya tshioshelu bua kutshituala.
8 Utshienze ne mabaya, ushiye munda muatshi mutuhu, utshienze bu munakukulesha ku mukuna.
9 Wewe neulongolole luhangu lua tshitendelelu, ku luseke lua sude enza bilulu bidi bituangane tô ne ku coudées lukama,
10 makunshi makumi abidi ne nkanu ya tshiamu tshia mitaku makumi abidi, bitubuidi bia ku makunshi nebikale bia argent.
11 Ku luseke lua ku nôde kabidi, enza bilulu bidi bituangane to ne ku coudées lukama, ne makunshi makumi abidi ne nkanu ya tshiamu tshia mitaku makumi abidi; bitubuidi bia ku makunshi nebikale bia argent.
12 Ku butshiama bua luhangu ku luseke lua ku wesete, enza bilulu bidi bituangane tô ne ku coudées
13 Butshiama bua luhangu lua ku esete nebuikale coudées makumi atanu.
14 Bilulu bia ku luseke lua tshibi nebikale coudées dikumi ne itanu, ne makunshi abi asatu ne nkanu isatu.
15 Ku luseke lukuabo lua tshibi bilulu nebikale coudées dikumi ne itanu, ne makunshi asatu ne nkanu isatu.
16 Ku mbelu kua luhangu tshilulu netshikaleku tshia buanda bua bleu ne bukunzubile ne bukunze-kunzû ne bua lin mutambe buimpe, tshienjibue kudi mundi wa lungenyi lule, enza makunshi anai ne nkanu mai.
17 Lengesha makunshi onso ne tshiamu tshia argent, ne bitubuidi bia argent ne nkanu ya tshiamu tshia mitaku.
18 Buludi bua muaba wa luhangu nebuikale coudées lukama ne butshiama bualu nebuikale coudées makumi atanu, ne bule bualu bua mulu coudées itanu, ne bilulu bia lin ne nkanu yabi ya tshiamu tshia mitaku.
19 Bintu bionso bidibo bakuata nʼabi mudimu mu tshiendelele ne milonda yonso yatshi, ne milonda yonso ya luhangu luatshi, nebikale bia tshiamu tshia mitaku.
20 Wewe newele dî kudi Bena Isalele bua kuluabo ne manyi a olive a kuela mu muinda, muinda uteme matuku onso.
21 Mu tshitendelelu ku mpala kua tshilulu tshidi ku mpala kua mushete wa Nzambi Alona ne bana bandi balume nebalame muinda dilolo tô ne dinda ku mesu kua Yehowa, bualu ebu nebuikale bu mukenji ku bikondo bienu tshiendelele kudi bana ba Isalele.

Exodus 28
1 Ubikile Alona mukuluʼebe kûdi, ne bana bandi balume balue nʼandi ba munkatshi mua Bena Isalele, yeye ankuatshile mudimu wa buakuidi, Alona mene ne Nadaba ne Abihu, Eleazâ ne Itamâ, bana balume ba Alona.
2 Wewe neutelele Alona, mukuluʼebe, bilulu bia tshijila, bidi bitambe buimpe.
3 Wewe neubikile bantu bonso badi ne mitshima ya lungenyi, bantu banakûsha ne lungenyi tente, bua bobo batelele Alona bilulu bia kumujidila, bua yeye ankuatshile mudimu wa buakuidi.
4 Ebi mbilulu bidibo ne bua kutela tshintu tshia kuteka ha tshiadi, ne efoda, ne tshikowelu, ne kazaku ka bibaka bibaka, ne tshilulu ne mukaba, bobo nebatelele Alona ne bana bandi bilulu bia tshijila, bua yeye ankuatshile mudimu wa buakuidi.
5 Bobo bangate or ne bilulu bia bleu ne bikunzubile ne bikunze-kunzû ne bia lin mutambe buimpe.
6 Bobo nebatele efoda ne bintu bia or ne bilulu bia bleu ne bukunzubile ne bikunze-kunzû ne bia lin mutambe buimpe, bintu bidibo benzesha kudi bantu badi ne lungenyi lule.
7 Batele bilulu bikese ha maha bua kutuangasha bituha bia efoda bibidi.
8 Bobo nebenze mukaba wa efoda bu mudibo benza efoda, newikale tshituha tshimue tshia ku efoda, ne bintu bia or ne bilulu bia bleu ne bikunzubile ne bikunze-kunzû ne bia lin mutambe buimpe.
9 Wewe newangate mabue abidi a onyx, ufundehu mena a Bena Isalele,
10 mena asambombo ha dibue dimue ne mena asambombo adi ashala ha dibue dikuabo, balondangane bu mudi kuledibua kuabo.
11 Wenzeshe mabue â kudi muntu udi ne lungenyi lule lua kufunda mateta bimpe, yeye neafundehu mena a Bena Isalele, babueshe mabue mu masoso a mu bintu bia or.
12 Wewe neuteke mabue abidi ha bilulu bia ha maha ha efoda; mabue au neikale bu tshivulukidi tshia Bena Isalele, Alona neatuale mena abo ku mesu kua Yehowa ha maha andi abidi bu tshivulukidi.
13 Wewe newenze bitubuidi bia or,
14 ne tukanu tua or tubidi, tudi tuhindakasha bu muonshi, uteke tukanu ku bitubuidi.
15 Wewe newenzeshe tshilulu tshia dilumbulula tshia ha tshiadi kudi bantu badi ne lungenyi lule, bobo nebenze tshilulu etshi bu muakenzabo efoda, ne bintu bia or ne bilulu bia bleu ne bikunzubile ne bilulu bikunze-kunzû ne bia lin mutambe buimpe.
16 Utshidikishe nseke mai mikosolola hankatshi bua kuikala nseke ibidi ya bunene bumue, buludi buatshi nebuikale bu ntanta wa tshianza, ne butshiama buatshi bu ntanta wa tshianza.
17 Uteke milongo ya masoso mai a kubueshamu mabue, mulongo wa kumudilu wa mabue a sardoine ne topaze ne émeraude,
18 mulongo muibidi wa mabue a escarboucle ne saphir ne calcédoine,
19 mulongo muisatu wa mabue a opale ne agate ne améthyste,
20 ne mulongo muinai wa mabue a chrysolithe ne onyx ne jaspe ubueshe mabue a mu masoso.
21 Mabue neikale dikumi ne abidi bu bungi bua mena a Bena Isalele, nebafunde dina dia tshisamba ne tshisamba tshia Bena Isalele ha dibue ne ha dibue.
22 Newenze tukanu tua or mutambe buimpe tudi tuhindakasha bu mionshi.
23 Wewe newenze nkanu ya or ibidi, uteke nkanu ei ku nseke ibidi ya tshilulu tshia ha tshiadi.
24 Uteke tukanu tua or tuhindakasha ku nkanu idi ku nseke ya tshilulu.
25 Uteke nsonga ibidi ya tukanu tuhindakasha ku bilulu bia efoda bidi ha maha, ku mpala kuabi.
26 Enza biebe nkanu ya or ibidi, uyiteke ku luseke lua tshilulu tshia ha tshiadi tshituangane ne efoda.
27 Enza biebe nkanu ya or ibidi, uyiteke muinshi mua bilulu bia efoda bia ha maha, ku mpala kuabi ne ku mutu kua mukaba wa efoda.
28 Nebasuike tshilulu tshia ha tshiadi ku nkanu yatshi ku nkanu ya efoda ne muonshi wa bleu muhindakasha, tshilulu tshia ha tshiadi netshikale hamʼbidi ha mukaba muluka bilengele wa efoda, kabasulule tshilulu tshia ha tshiadi ku efoda.
29 Alona neatuale mena a Bena Isalele ha tshilulu tshia dilumbulula tshidi ha mutshima wandi, hayeye ku muaba wa tshijila, bu tshivulukidi tshia tshiendelele ku mesu kua Yehowa.
30 Ubueshe mabue a Ulima ne Tumima mu masoso a mu tshilulu tshia dilumbulula tshia ha tshiadi, a neikale ha mutshima wa Alona hayeye ku mpala kua Yehowa, Alona neatuale kulumbulula kua malu a Bena Isalele ku mpala kua Yehowa matuku onso.
31 Wewe neutele tshikowelu tshia efoda ne bilulu bia bleu.
32 Ushiye disoso ku mutu kua tshikowelu bua kubueshamu mutu, nyungulula disoso ne muonshi muhindakasha, bu mudibo benza tshikowelu tshia nvita tshikowelu katshihandiki nansha.
33 Neufunde bifuanyikishi bia tumuma tua grenade ne buanda bua bleu ne bukunzubile ne bukunzekunzû kuinshi kua tshikowelu tshionso, neuteke ndibu ya or hankatshi ha tumuma,
34 neuteke kadibu ka or ne kamuma, ne kadibu ka or ne kamuma to ne kuinshi kua tshikowelu tshionso.
35 Alona nealuate tshikowelu henzeye mudimu, nebunvue tshiona tshia ndibu hayeye mu muaba wa tshijila ku mesu kua Yehowa, ne haluhukeye kabidi, bua yeye kafu nansha.
36 Wewe newenze tshikololo tshia tshiamu tshia or, neufundehu ne, TSHIA TSHIHE A KUDI YEHOWA.
37 Neutshiteke ha muonshi wa bleu muhindakasha, utshisuike ku mpala kua tshilulu.
38 Netshikale ha mpala ha Alona, ne Alona neatuale mahambuka a Bena Isalele mu muaba wa tshijila hayeye bua kubajidila ne bintu bidibo bafila kudi Nzambi, netshikale ha mpala handi matuku onso bua Yehowa abitabushe.
39 Wewe newenze tshikowelu bikeshe bikeshe ne buanda bua lin muimpe, ne wewe newenze tshilulu tshia lin muimpe ne mukaba muimpe mutambe.
40 Wewe neutelele bana balume ba Alona bikowelu ne mikaba ne bifulu bia butumbi ne buimpe.
41 Wewe neubiluatshishe Alona muanʼenu ne bana bandi balume badi nʼandi, wewe neubele manyi ha mutu, ubalengeshe, ubajidile, bua bobo bankuatshile mudimu wa buakuidi.
42 Ubatelele mihanu ya lin ya kubuikila butaka buabo, mihanu neyikale ku tshimono mifike to ne ku bibelu.
43 Alona ne bana bandi balume nebaluate mihanu ei hayabo mu tshitendelelu anyi hayabo hehi ne tshioshelu bua kuenza mudimu wabo mu muaba wa tshijila, bobo kabatuadi mahambuka, kabafu. Mukenji eu newikale kudiye ne kudi bana bandi badi banulonda ku nyima tô ne tshiendelele.

Exodus 29
1 Wewe newenze bualu ebu bua kubajidila bankuatshile mudimu wa buakuidi. Angata muana mulume wa ngombe ne bimpanga bibidi bidi biakane,
2 ne mampa adi kâyi ne luevene, ne mikata idi kayiyi ne luevene misangisha ne manyi, ne tukata luhehele ludi katuyi ne luevene tudi ne manyi mela ha mutu, ubilambe ne bukula buakane bua blé.
3 Ubiteke mu tshisaka tshimue, ulue nʼabi mu tshisaka ne muana wa ngombe ne bimpanga bibidi.
4 Ulue ne Alona ne bana bandi balume ku tshibi tshia ntenta wa kusangakena, uboweshe mai.
5 Wewe newangate bilulu, uluatshishe Alona tshikowelu ne tshilulu tshia ha mutu ha efoda ne efoda ne tshilulu tshia ha tshiadi ne umuele mukaba muluka bilengele wa efoda ku tshimono.
6 Teka tshifulu ha mutu handi ne tshitotolo tshia tshijila ha tshifulu.
7 Hashishe angata manyi adibo bela ha mutu, ne ela manyi au ha mutu handi.
8 Wewe neulue ne bana bandi balume, ubaluatshishe bikowelu.
9 Ubele mikaba ku bimono, Alona ne bana bandi balume, teka bifulu ha mitu habo, bobo nebikale ne mudimu wa buakuidi tshiendelele, wewe neujidile Alona ne bana bandi balume.
10 Wewe neulue ne muana wa ngombe ku tshibi tshia ntenta wa kusambakena, Alona ne bana bandi balume nebatentekashe bianza biabo ha mutu ha muana wa ngombe.
11 Wewe neushihe muana wa ngombe ku mesu kua Yehowa ku tshibi tshia ntenta wa kusambakena.
12 Wewe newangate mashi a muana mulume wa ngombe, ulabe mashi ha nsengu ya ha tshioshelu ne munu webe, hongolola mashi onso ku tshitaku tshia tshioshelu.
13 Angata dianyi dionso didi ku mala ne lukutu lua ku mutshima ne tumuma tubidi ne, dianyi diabi, ubioshile ha tshioshelu.
14 Kadi wewe newoshe munyinyi wa muana mulume wa ngombe, ne tshiseba ne tunvi ne kahia ku butshika bua tshitudilu ebu mbualu bua kubuikidila bubi bua bantu.
15 Angata tshimpanga tshimue, ne Alona ne bana bandi balume nebatentekashe bianza biabo ha mutu hatshi.
16 Wewe neushihe tshimpanga, newangate mashi atshi wamiamine ha tshioshelu tshionso.
17 Wewe neusaye tshimpanga, uvuwa mala atshi ne mikolo, uteke bituha biatshi hamue ne mutu watshi.
18 Wewe newoshe tshimpanga tshijima ha tshioshelu, tshidi mulambu wa kuoshila Yehowa, udi utua dihembu, udibo boshila Yehowa ha kahia.
19 Wewe newangate tshimpanga tshikuabo, Alona ne bana bandi balume nebatentekashe bianza biabo ha mutu ha tshimpanga.
20 Wewe neashihe tshimpanga, angata mashi atshi, wateke ku lusongo lua ditshu didi ku tshianza tshia balume tshia Alona, ne ku lusongo lua ditshu ku tshianza tshia balume tshia bana bandi balume, ne ku tshiala tshia ku tshianza tshiabo tshia bukole, ne ku muanʼa dikasa munene dia ku tshianza tshia balume, umiamine mashi ha tshioshelu tshionso.
21 Wewe newangate mashi adi ha tshioshelu, ne manyi adibo bela ha mutu, ubimiamine Alona, ha bilulu biandi ne ha bana bandi balume ne ha bilulu biabo, yeye ne bilulu biandi ne bana bandi balume ne bilulu biabo nebikale bijidibue.
22 Wewe newangate dianyi dia tshimpanga, ne mukila udi ne dianyi ne dianyi didi ku mala ne lukutu lua ku mutshima, ne tumuma tubidi ne dianyi diabi ne tshibelu tshia ku tshianza tshia balume, bualu bua tshimpanga etshi ntshia kujidila nʼatshi bakuidi.
23 Angata mutanda wa mampa, ne mukala mulamba ne manyi, ne kakata kahehele bia mu tshisaka tshia bidia bidi kabiyi ne luevene bidi ku mpala kua Yehowa.
24 Uteke bintu ebi bionso mu bianza bia Alona ne mu bianza bia bana bandi balume, bobo nebabinyukule bu mulambu udibo banyukula ku mpala kua Yehowa.
25 Wewe newangate bintu ebi ku bianza biabo, ubioshile ha tshioshelu bu mulambu udi utua dihembu dimpe ku mesu kua Yehowa, udi mulambu udibo boshila Yehowa ha kahia.
26 Wewe newangate tshiadi tshia tshimpanga tshia kujidila kua Alona, utshinyukule bu mulambu udibo banyukula ku mpala kua Yehowa, netshikale tshituha tshiebe.
27 Wewe neujidile tshiadi tshidibo banyukula, ne tshihela tshidibo babisha mulu tshia tshimpanga tshia kujidila kua Alona ne tshia bana bandi balume.
28 Tshintu etshi netshikale tshituha tshia Alona ne tshia bana bandi balume kudi Bena Isalele tshiendelele, bualu bua tshidi tshituha tshia bakuidi tshia mu mulambu ya kuvuisha nʼai ditalala ya Bena Isalele, tshidi mulambu udibo babisha mulu kudi Yehowa.
29 Bilulu bia tshijila bia Alona nebikale kudi bana bandi balume badi banulonda ku nyima bua kubela manyi ne kubajidila.
30 Muana mulume udi biandi muakuidi mu muaba wandi neabiluate matuku muanda mutekete hayeye mu ntenta wa disangisha bua kuenza mudimu mu muaba wa tshijila.
31 Wewe newangate tshimpanga tshia tshijila, ulambe munyinyi watshi ne mai a kahia mu muaba wa tshijila.
32 Alona ne bana bandi balume nebadie munyinyi wa tshimpanga ne bidia bidi mu tshisaka ku tshibi tshia ntenta wa kusangakena.
33 Bobo nebadie bintu abi bidibo babuikidila nʼabi bantu mibi yabo, bua kubajidila ne kubalengesha kudi Yehowa, kadi muenyi kena mua kudia bintu ebi, bualu bua bidi bia tshijila.
34 Bikala tshintu tshikese tshia munyinyi wa tshijila anyi tshia bidia bishale tô ne ku dinda, wewe neutshioshe mu kahia, kabatshidi, bualu bua tshidi tshia tshijila.
35 Wewe newenzele Alona ne bana bandi balume bu mudi mei onso anakukuambila, ubajidile matuku muanda mutekete.
36 Wewe newoshe muana wa ngombe ku dituku ku dituku bua kubuikidila bantu mibi yabo, wewe neutokeshe tshioshelu hawoshila bintu bia kubuikidila nʼabi bantu mibi yabo, wele manyi ha tshioshelu bua kutshijidila.
37 Wewe neubuikile mibi idi ha tshioshelu matuku muanda mutekete, utshijidile, tshioshelu netshikale bu tshintu tshitambe tshia tshijila, muntu yonso udi ulenga tshioshelu neikale wa tshijila.
38 Ei mmilambu ludi mua kuoshila ha tshioshelu bana ba mikoko babidi ba tshidimu tshimue ku dituku ku dituku misangu yonso
39 Wewe newoshile umue wa mikoko ne dinda ne mukuabo dilolo
40 Woshe muana umue wa mukoko ne tshia dikumi tshia tshihiminu
41 Neufile muana wa mukoko mukuabo dilolo, neumufile ne mulambu wa bukula ne mulambu wa kunua, bu muwakenza dinda, bu mulambu wa dihembu dimpe, mulambu udibo boshila Yehowa ha kahia.
42 Neboshile bintu ebi ku dituku ku dituku ku bikondo bienu bionso ku tshibi tshia ntenta wa kusangakena ku mesu kua Yehowa, kundi nsangakena nʼenu bua kuyikila nʼebe.
43 Meme nensangakeneku ne Bena Isalele, ne tshitendelelu netshikale tshiedibue ku butumbi buanyi.
44 Meme nenjidile ntenta wa kusangakena ne tshioshelu, nenjidile Alona ne bana bandi balume kabidi bua kunkuatshilabo mudimu wa buakuidi.
45 Meme nengikale munkatshi mua Bena Isalele, nengikale Nzambi wabo.
46 Bobo nebamanye ne, Meme ndi Yehowa, Nzambi wabo, wakabahatula mu buloba bua Ejipitu bua meme kuikala munkatshi muabo, ndi bianyi Yehowa, Nzambi wabo.

Exodus 30
1 Wewe newenze tshioshelu tshia musenga wa manananshi ne mutshi wa acacia.
2 Buludi buatshi nebuikale coudée
3 Ulamike tshiamu tshia or mutambe buimpe ha mutu hatshi ne ku nseke yatshi ne ku nsengu yatshi, nyungulula mubangu watshi ne or ku nseke yonso.
4 Enza biebe nkanu ya or ibidi, uyiteke ku nseke ibidi kuinshi kua mutu watshi; neyikale bu miaba ya kubueshamu mitshi ya kutshituadila.
5 Enza mitshi ya acacia ibidi, ulamike or ku nseke yonso.
6 Utshiteke hehi ne tshilulu tshidi ku mushete wa Nzambi, ku mpala kua tshibuikilu tshidi ha mutu ha mushete, kundi nsangakena nʼebe.
7 Alona neoshileku musenga wa manananshi dinda ne dinda hayeye kulongolola minda.

11 Yehowa wakambila Mose ne,
12 Hawabala bungi bua Bena Isalele, muntu ne muntu afile bintu bia kuhikula nʼabi muoyo wandi kudi Yehowa; bua makenga makole kâbakuatshi hawababala.
13 Muntu ne muntu udibo babala neafile tshituha tshia nshekele
14 Muntu ne muntu udibo babala wakabita bidimu makumi abidi neafile kuha kua Yehowa.
15 Muntu mubanji kena ne bualu bua kufila tshintu tshidi tshilamba tshituha tshia nshekele bunene, ne muntu muhele kena ne bualu bua kufila tshintu tshidi tshilamba tshituha tshia nshekele bukese, hanuafila kuha kua Yehowa bua kuhikula mioyo yenu.
16 Wewe newangate argent ya dihikula dia Bena Isalele, uyiteke bua kukuatshisha mudimu wa mu ntenta wa kusangakena, neyikale bu tshiyulukidi tshia Bena Isalele ku mesu kua Yehowa bua kuhikula kua mioyo yenu.
17 Yehowa wakambila Mose ne,
18 Wewe newenze tshiowelu tshia tshiamu tshia mitaku ne tshitaku tshiatshi tshia tshiamu tshia mitaku kudibo bowa mai. Neutshiteke hankatshi ha tshitendelelu ne tshioshe lu, newelemu mai.
19 Alona ne bana bandi balume neboweshe bianza biabo ne makasa abo ne mai mu tshiowelu,
20 hayabo mu ntenta wa kusangakena, nebowe mai bua bobo kabafu nansha, anyi hayabo ku tshioshelu bua kuosha nyama ha kahia kudi Yehowa.
21 Nunku bobo neboweshe bianza biabo ne makasa abo ne mai, bua bobo kabafu, etshi ntshilele tshiabo tshiendelele kudiye ne bana bandi badi banulonda ku nyima ku bikondo biabo bionso.
22 Yehowa wakambila Mose kabidi ne,
23 Angata bintu bia manananshi bidi bitambe buimpe myrrhe bujitu bua nshekele nkama itanu, ne cinnamone bujitu bua nshekele nkama ibidi ne makumi atanu, ne canne aromatique bujitu bua nshekele nkama ibidi ne makumi atanu,
24 ne nkashia bujitu bua nshekele nkama itanu bu idiho bafila mu tshitendelelu ne tshihiminu
25 Wewe newenze manyi a tshijila adibo bela ha mutu ne bintu ebi, manyi adi atua dihembu bu mudibo benza kudi benyi ba manananshi, manyi au neikale manyi a tshijila adibo bela ha mutu.
26 Wewe newamiamine ntenta wa kusangakena ne mushete wa Nzambi,
27 ne mesa ne malonga onso a mesa, ne muinda ne malonga onso a muinda, ne tshioshelu tshia musenga wa manananshi,
28 ne tshioshelu tshia kahia ne malonga onso a tshioshelu, ne tshiowelu ne tshitaku tshia tshiowelu.
29 Wewe neubilengeshe, neubijidile, muntu yonso udi ubilenga tshianza neikale wa tshijila.
30 Wewe newele Alona ne bana bandi balume manyi ha mutu, neubajidile, bua bobo bankuatshile mudimu wa buakuidi.
31 Wewe newambile Bena Isalele ne, Manyi au neikale ku mesu kuanyi bu manyi a tshijila adibo bela ha mutu ku bikondo bienu bionso.
32 Kuedi manyi au hamʼbidi ha bantu, kabena manyi mafuanangane nʼau, wowo adi a tshijima, neikale a tshijila kunudi.
33 Muntu yonso udi usangisha manyi bu au, anyi udi waela muenyi, nebamumushe munkatshi mua bana babo.
34 Yehowa wakambila Mose ne, Angata bintu bia manananshi a mushinga mukole bia stacte ne onyx ne gabanum, bintu bia manananshi matambe buimpe, widikishe bintu bionso ne tshihiminu tshimue.
35 Ubisangishe bu manananshi adi atua dihembu kudi benyi ba manananshi, ubisangishe ne luehu lutambe buimpe ne lua tshijila.
36 Wewe neuboteshe tshituha, utshiteke ku mpala kua mushete wa Nzambi mu ntenta wa kusangakena kundi nsangakena nʼebe, netshikale tshilamba tshia tshima kunudi.
37 Kanudisangishidi manananshi adi atua dihembu nunku ne bintu ebi, manananshi au neikale a tshijila kudi Yehowa.
38 Muntu yonso udi usangisha bintu ebi bua kudienzela manananshi, nebamumushe munkatshi mua bana babo.

Exodus 31
1 Yehowa wakambila Mose ne,
2 Nakubikila Besalele, muana mulume wa Uli, muana mulume wa Hu wa mu tshisamba tshia Yuda.
3 Nakumûsha tente ne Nyuma wa Nzambi ne lungenyi ne dijingulula ne meji mu kuenza kua midimu yonso ya bianza,
4 bua kulongololeye bintu bia or ne bia argent ne bia tshiamu tshia mitaku,
5 ne bintu bidibo basonga nʼabi mabue, ne bidibo basonga nʼabi mitshi, ne midimu yonso ya bantu badi ne lungenyi lule.
6 Mona, meme nakusungula Oholiaba, muana mulume wa Ahisamaka, wa mu tshisamba tshia Dana bua kumumatshisha, nakabuesha lungenyi lule mu mitshima ya bantu bonso badi ne meji bua bobo benze bintu bionso binakukuambila.
7 Nebenze ntenta wa kusangakena, ne mushete wa tshihungidi tshia mei a Nzambi, ne tshibuikilu tshia ha mutu ha mushete, ne bintu bionso bidi mu ntenta wa kusambakena,
8 ne mesa ne malonga a mesa, ne muinda muimpe ne malonga a muinda, ne tshioshelu tshia musenga wa manananshi,
9 ne tshioshelu tshia kahia ne malonga a tshioshelu, ne tshiowelu ne tshitaku tshia tshiowelu.
10 Nebenze bilulu bitambe buimpe, ne bilulu bia tshijila bia Alona muakuidi, ne bilulu bia bana bandi balume, bua kunkuatshilabo mudimu wa buakuidi,
11 ne manyi adibo bela ha mutu, ne bintu bia manananshi bidi bidia dihembu bia mu muaba wa tshijila bobo nebenze malu onso anakukuambila.
12 Yehowa wakambila Mose ne,
13 Wambile Bena Isalele kabidi ne, Nuenu nuvuluke matuku anyi a Lumingu dituku edi didi bu tshivulukidi hankatshi hetu ku bikondo bienu bionso bualu bua kunumanyisha ne, Meme ndi Yehowa wakunujidila.
14 Nuvuluke dituku dia Lumingu, didi dituku dia tshijila kunudi, mundi yonso udi wona dituku adi nebamushihe, mundi yonso udi wenza mudimu dituku adi, nebamumushe munkatshi mua bana babo.
15 Enzai mudimu wenu matuku asambombo; kadi dituku dia muanda mutekete ndituku dia dikisha dia kuela meji, dituku dia kujidila Yehowa, mundi yonso udi wenza mudimu dituku dia Lumingu nebamushihe bulelela.
16 Nunku Bena Isalele nebajidile dituku dia Lumingu ku bikondo biabo bionso bu tshihungidi tshia tshiendelele.
17 Etshi ntshimanyinu tshia tshiendelele hankatshi hanyi ne Bena Isalele, bualu bua Yehowa wakafuka diulu ne buloba matuku asambombo, wakikisha dituku dia muanda mutekete, wakadikankamika.
18 Hakashikisha Yehowa kusomba ne Mose ha mukuna wa Sinai, yeye wakamuha mabue abidi akadi ne mikenji mifundahu ne munu wa Nzambi.

Exodus 32
1 Kakamanya bantu ne, Mose wakashala ku mukuna musangu mule, bakadisangisha kudi Alona, bakamuambila ne, Bika biebe, utuenzele nzambi idi mua kuya ku mpala kuetu, bua muntu au Mose, wakalua nʼetu mu Ejipitu, katuena bamanye bualu buakumukuata.
2 Alona wakabambila ne, Nukuohole nkanu ya or idi ku matshu a bakashi benu ne a bana benu ba balume ne a bana benu ba bakashi, nulue nʼai kundi.
3 Bantu bonso bakakuohola nkanu ya or yakadi ku matshu abo, bakalua nʼai kudi Alona.
4 Yeye wakayangata ku bianza biabo, wakayilamba mu kahia, wakayiteka ne mulolo bu kana ka ngombe, bakamba ne, Bena Isalele, ke nzambi wenu, wakanuluhula mu buloba bua Ejipitu.
5 Hakamona Alona bualu ebu, Wakenza tshioshelu ku mpala kua luhingu, Alona wakela dî, wakamba ne, Makelela disanka dia Yehowa nedikaleku.
6 Hakatshiabu bakabika ne dinda dionso, bakafua milambu ya kuosha, bakalua ne milambu ya kuvuisha nʼai ditalala, bantu bakashikama bua kudia ne bua kunua, bakabika bua kunaya.
7 Yehowa wakambila Mose ne, Hueka mu tshibanda, bualu bua bantu bebe bawakaluhula mu buloba bua Ejipitu bakudiona.
8 Bakusesuka lubilu mu nshila unakubambila, bakulamba nkanu ya or, bakutula kana ka ngombe, bakukatendelela, bakukashihela milambu, bakuamba ne, Bena Isalele, nzambi wenu nyeu wakanuluhula mu buloba bua Ejipitu.
9 Yehowa wakambila Mose ne, Nakutangila bantu aba, mona, badi ne buhidia bukole,
10 nunku kumpumbishi nansha, tshiji tshianyi tshikale tshikole kudibo, neashihe, kadi nenkuvuishe wewe tshisamba tshinene.
11 Mose wakatendekena Yehowa, Nzambi wandi, wakamba ne, Yehowa, bualu kai tshiji tshiebe tshidi tshitambe bukole kudi bantu bebe bawakaluhula mu buloba bu Ejipitu ne bukole bunene ne tshianza tshia bukole?
12 Munyi mudi Bena Ejipitu mua kuamba ne, Yeye wakabaluhula bua kubenzela bibi, ne bua kubashiha ku mikuna, ne bua kubajimisha ha buloba? Lekela tshiji tshiebe tshikole, ukudimune mutshima bua malu audi musue kuenzela bantu bebe.
13 Uvuluke Abalahama ne Isaka ne Isalele, bantu bebe, bawakaditshiha kudibo, wakabambila ne, Nenvudishe bana benu bu bungi bua mitoto ya mulu, ne buloba ebu buonso bunakanuambila nempe bana benu, bobo nebabuhiane tshiendelele.
14 Yehowa wakakudimuna mutshima wandi bua malu akeleye meji bua kuenzela bantu bandi.
15 Mose wakatshunguluka, wakahueka mu tshibanda ne mabue abidi akadi ne mikenji mifundahu mu bianza biandi, mabue akadi mafu nda nseke yau ibidi, ha luseke lumue ne ha luseke lukuabo.
16 Mabue au akadi menza kudi Nzambi, ne mei akadi mafunda kudi Nzambi, mafunda ha mabue au.
17 Hakunvua Yoshua diyoyo dia bantu hakadibo bela mbila, yeye wakambila Mose ne, Au mmutoyi wa bantu badi baluangana nvita mu tshitudilu.
18 Mose wakamba ne, Adi kadiena dî dia bantu badi bahita bakuabo bukole, ne kadiena dî dia bantu bakutambibua bukole, ndi munvue mutoyi wa bantu badi bimba misambu.
19 Hakalueye hehi ne tshitudilu, wakatangila kana ka ngombe ne bantu baja maja Mose wakadi ne tshiji tshikole, wakela mabue hanshi akadi mu bianza biandi, wakatshibula kuinshi kua mukuna.
20 Mose wakangata kana ka ngombe kakenzabo, wakakosha mu kahia, wakakabotesha, wakakela mu mai, wakanuisha Bena Isalele mai au.
21 Mose wakakonka Alona ne, Bantu bakakuenzela tshinyi bua wewe wakubavuishila bualu bubi?
22 Alona wakamba ne, Mukelenge wanyi, kuikadi ne tshiji tshikole, wewe udi mumanye bantu aba, badi ne mitshima yabo ku malu mabi.
23 Bualu bua bobo bakungambila ne, Utuenzele nzambi idi mua kuya ku mpala kuetu, bua muntu eu Mose wakatumikila mu buloba bua Ejipitu, katuena bamanye bualu buakumukuata.
24 Meme nakabambila ne, Muntu yonso udi ne or akuohole, ne bobo bakafua or kundi, nakuela mu kahia ne kana aka ka ngombe ka or kakahatuka mu kahia.
25 Hakatangila Mose ne bantu bakadi batomboka (Alona wakabanda bua kudifuishishabo bundu kudi bena lukuna babo),
26 Mose wakimana ku mbelu kua tshitudilu, wakamba ne, Udi ku luseke lua Yehowa alue biandi kundi Bana balume bonso ba Lewi bakadisangisha kudiye.
27 Yeye wakabambila ne, Yehowa, Nzambi wa Isalele, udi Wamba ne, Muntu yonso aluate muele wandi wa nvita, endakane ku tshibi ne ku tshibi mu tshitudilu tshionso, ashihe muanʼabo mulume ne mulunda wandi ne muena mutumba nʼandi.
28 Bana balume ba Lewi bakenza bu mu di dia Mose, bantu bakafua dituku adi bu binunu bisatu.
29 Mose wakamba ne, Muntu yonso wakudifila kudi Yehowa lelu ku mushinga wa muanʼandi mulume ne muanʼabo mulume, Yehowa anuvudishile disanka lelu.
30 Hakatshiabu Mose wakambila bantu ne, Nuenu nuakuenza bualu bubi bunene Katataka nembande kudi Yehowa, binamanya mua kunubuikidila bubi buenu.
31 Mose wakahingana kudi Yehowa, wakamba ne, Bantu aba bakuenza bualu bubi bunene, bakudienzela nzambi wa or.
32 Kadi katataka biwasua kubabuikidila bubi buabo, kadi biwikala kuyi musue, ndi nkusengelela ne, Unjimishe mu mukanda webe uwakufunda.
33 Yehowa wakambila Mose ne, Muntu yonso wakungenzela bibi, nemmujimishe yeye mu mukanda wanyi.
34 Katataka ya biebe, uye ne bantu ku muaba unakukuambila, tangila, muanjelo wanyi neaye ku mpala kuebe, kadi dituku dinalua kudibo, nembenzele kabidi bu mudi bubi buabo.
35 Yehowa wakakengesha bantu bualu bua bobo bakenza kana ka ngombe kakadi Alona muenze.

Exodus 33
1 Yehowa wakambila Mose ne, Bikai, yai bienu, ne wewe ne bantu bawakaluhula mu buloba bua Ejipitu, ku buloba bunakalaya Abalahama ne Isaka ne Yakoba ne, Nembuhe bana benu.
2 Nentume muanjelo ku mpala kuebe, nengihate Bena Kanana ne Bena Amole ne Bena Hiti ne Bena Pelizi ne Bena Hiwi ne Bena Yebuse,
3 ku bu loba budi ne mabele ne buitshi bia bungi; bualu bua meme tshiena nya munkatshi muenu bualu bua nudi bantu ba buhidia bukole, bua tshinushihi mu nshila.
4 Hakunvua bantu lumu alu lubi, bakadila nansha mundi umue wa kaluata bintu bia kudilengesha nʼabi.
5 Yehowa wakambila Mose ne, Wambile Bena Isalele ne, Nudi bantu ba buhidia bukole, binaya bianyi munkatshi muenu anu tshituha tshikese, nennushihe, nunku nuvule bintu bia kudilengesha nʼabi, bua meme mmanye tshindi mua kunuenzela.
6 Bena Isalele bakavula bintu bia kudilengesha nʼabi ku mukuna wa Holeba tô ne ku mpala kuakadibo baya.
7 Mose wakadi ushimika ntenta ku butshika bua tshitudilu, wakamubikila ne, Ntenta wa kusambakena Muntu yonso wakasua kukeba Yehowa wakaluhuka mu tshitudilu bua kuya ku ntenta wa kusambakena, wakadi ku butshika bua tshitudilu.
8 Hakaya Mose ku tshitendelelu, bantu bonso bakabika, bakimana muntu ne muntu ku ntenta wandi, bakatangila Mose tô ne hakabueleye mu Ntenta.
9 Hakabuela Mose mu tshitendelelu, difutu diakahueka, diakimana ku mbelu kua Ntenta ne Yehowa wakayikila ne Mose.
10 Hakatangila bantu difutu dimana ku mbelu kua Ntenta, bantu bonso bakabika, bakatendelela muntu ne mu ntu ku ntenta wandi.
11 Yehowa wakayikila ne Mose mpala ha mpala bu mudi muntu uyikila ne mulunda wandi. Mose wakahingana ku tshidi dilu kabidi, kadi mukuatshishi wandi Yoshua, nsongalume, muana wa balume wa Nuna, kakaluhuka mu Ntenta.
12 Mose wakambila Yehowa ne, Mona, wewe udi ungambila ne, Uye ne bantu aba, ne wewe kuena muanze kummanyisha muntu udi musue kutuma nʼanyi. Kadi wewe wakamba ne, Ndi mumanye dina diebe, ne wewe wakasangana luse ku mesu kuanyi.
13 Katataka, bingikala ne luse ku mesu kuebe, undeshe bienzedi biebe, bua meme kukumanya wewe, ne bua kusangana luse ku mesu kuebe, uvuluke ne tshisamba etshi tshidi bantu bebe.
14 Yehowa wakamba ne, Mpala wanyi neaye nʼebe, ne meme nenkuhe dikisha.
15 Mose wakamba ne, Bikala mpala webe kayi uya nʼanyi, kudimusha kunoko.
16 Nemmanye ne, Nakasangana luse ku mesu kuebe, ne meme ne bantu bebe, kudi kunyi? Kabuena nunku ne, Wewe uyaya nʼetu, ne tuetu, meme ne bantu bebe, netuikale bu bantu badi bahanduluka kudi bisamba bionso bia bantu badi hanshi ha buloba, anyi?
17 Yehowa wakambila Mose ne, Nengenze bualu ebu buwakuamba bualu bua wewe wakasangana luse ku mesu kuanyi, meme ndi mumanye Hina diebe.
18 Mose wakamba ne, Ndi nkusengelela ne, Undeshe butumbi buebe.
19 Yehowa wakamba ne, Nengendeshe buimpe buanyi buonso ku mesu kuebe, nemmanyishe dina dianyi dia Yehowa ku mpala kuebe; nengikale ne dinanga kudi bantu bandi musue kuikala ne dinanga, nendeshe luse kudi bantu bandi musue kulesha luse.
20 Yeye wakamba ne, Wewe kuena mua kumona mpala wanyi; bualu bua muntu kena mua kumona mpala wanyi ne kuikala ne muoyo.
21 Yehowa wakamba ne, Tangila, muaba udiku, udi hehi nʼanyi; wewe newimane ha dibue.
22 Hahita butumbi buanyi, nenkuteke mu dina didi mu dibue, nenkubuikile ne tshianza tshianyi to ne hanamana kuhita ku mesu kuebe.
23 Hashishe nengumushe tshianza tshianyi, ne wewe neumone nyima wanyi; kadi kuena mua kumona mpala wanyi.

Exodus 34
1 Yehowa wakambila Mose ne, Songa biebe mabue abidi bu mabue a diambedi: neufundehu mei onso anakafunda ha mabue a diambedi awakatshibula.
2 Udilongolole dinda, Ulue biebe ne dinda ku mukuna wa Sinai; Ulue biebe kundi ku mutu kua mukuna.
3 Kulu ne muntu mukuabo nansha: muntu kamueneki ku mukuna nansha: bisumbu bia mikoko ne bia ngombe kabidi bintu ku mukuna.
4 Nunku Mose wakasonga mabue abidi bu mabue a diambedi; wakabika ne dinda dionso, wakabanda ku mukuna wa Sinai, bu muakamuambila Yehowa; wakaya ne mabue abidi mu bianza biandi.
5 Yehowa wakahueka mu ditutu, wakimanahu nʼandi. Wakamumanyisha dina dia Yehowa.
6 Yehowa wakahita ku mpala kuandi, wakela dî ne, Yehowa, Yehowa, Nzambi wa luse ne dinanga, kena ukuata tshiji lubilu, udi mûle tente ne luse lujalame ne bushuwa,
7 udi ulesha binunu bia bantu luse lujalame, udi ubuikidila bantu malonga abo ne kusambuka kuabo kua mikenji ne malu mabi abo; kadi kena ubingisha muntu udi uhila; udi utumina bana ba tshikondo tshisatu anyi tshinai mibi ya batatuʼabo.
8 Mose wakinama mutu wandi lubilu, wakatendelela.
9 Yeye wakamba ne, Bingikala muhete dianyisha ku mesu kuebe, Yehowa, witabushe bua Yehowa aye biandi munkatshi muetu; nansha bantu aba bikale ne buhidia bukole; utubuikidile kuhanga kuetu ne mibi yetu, utuitabushe bu buhianyi buebe.
10 Yeye wakamba ne, Nempunge nʼenu tshihungidi; nengenze malu a kukema ku mesu kua bantu bebe bonso, malu adi kâyi manze kumueneka ha buloba buonso anyi kudi tshisamba tshikuabo; bantu bonso badi nʼebe nebamone bienzedi bia Yehowa; bualu bua meme nengenze nʼebe bualu bukole.
11 Enza bu mundi nkuambila dituku edi. Mona, nengihate Bena Amole ne Bena Kanâna ne Bena Hiti ne Bena Pelizi ne Bena Hiwi ne Bena Yebuse ku mpala kuebe.
12 Udimuke, kuhungi tshihungidi ne bantu ba mu buloba bûdi uya; bualu ebu kabulu buteyi munkatshi muenu.
13 Ushimbule bioshelu biabo, utshibule bibumba biabo, ukose mpingu yabo ya Bashela.
14 Kutendeledi nzambi mukuabo nansha; bualu bua Yehowa udi ne dina diandi dia Mukau, udi Nzambi wa mukau.
15 Kuhungi tshihungidi ne bena buloba; hendabo masandi ne mpingu yabo, ne hashihelabo nzambi yabo nyama, hakubikilabo bua kudia nʼabo munyinyi wa nyama mishiha;
16 bua nuenu kanubikidi bana babo ba bakashi kudi bana benu ba balume; ne bana babo ba bakashi badi benda masandi ku mpingu yabo ka-benzeshi bana benu ba balume masandi kudi mpingu yabo.
17 Kudisongidi mpingu.
18 Nuenu nenudie bidia bidi kabiyi ne luevene ku mesu kua Yehowa. Nenudie bidia bidi kabiyi ne luevene matuku muanda mutekete, bu munakanuambila mu ngondo wa Abiba unakasungula; bualu bua nuakahatuka mu Ejipitu mu ngondo wa Abiba.
19 Bana bonso banudi batela mbanyi; banʼa bute ba balume ba bimuna mbanyi, banʼa bute ba ngombe ne ba mikoko.
20 Nenuhikule banʼa bute benu ba mpunda ku muanʼa mukoko; binuikala kanuyi nubahikula, nutshibule nshingu yabo. Nenuhikule banʼa bute benu ba balume bonso. Kanulu ku mesu kuanyi ne bianza bituhu.
21 Nuenze mudimu wenu matuku asambombo, kadi nuikishe dituku dia muanda mutekete: nuikishe mu tshidimu tshia kukuna bintu ne mu tshidimu tshia kuhuola bintu.
22 Nenudie bidia bia disanka dia mbingu ku mesu kua Yehowa hanuahuola bintu, ne hanuasangisha bintu kunshikidilu kua tshidimu.
23 Bana benu ba balume bonso nebamueneke kudi Yehowa, Nzambi wa Isalele, misangu isatu ku tshidimu ku tshidimu.
24 Bualu bua meme nengihate bisamba bia bantu ku mesu kuenu, nendeheshe matshika enu: bantu bakuabo kabena bikale ne lukuka lua buloba buenu, hanuamueneka kudi Yehowa Nzambi wenu misangu isatu ku tshidimu ku tshidimu.
25 Kanulu ne mashi a nyama mishiha ne mampa adi ne luevene hohamue; kanushihi nansha tshintu tshikese tshia mulambu wa disanka dia kuhita kua Yehowa tô ne ku dinda.
26 Bintu bia kumudilu binudi nuhuola biai nʼabi mu nsubu wa Yehowa, Nzambi wenu. Kanulambi muana wa mukoko ne mabele a mamu wandi.
27 Yehowa wakambila Mose ne, Ufunde mei â, bualu bua nakuhunga nʼebe ne Bena Isalele tshihungidi bu mudi mei â.
28 Yeye wakashala ne Yehowa minya makumi anai ne mafuku makumi anai; wakajila bia kudia ne mai a kunua. Wakafunda mei a dihungila, mikenji dikumi, ha mabue.
29 Hakumuka Mose ku mukuna wa Sinai ne mabue abidi a mei a dihungila mu bianza biandi, hakahuekeye nunku, Mose kakamanya ne musunyi wa ha mpala handi wakakonka bualu bua yeye wakadi usomba ne Yehowa.
30 Hakamona Alona ne Bena Isalele bonso Mose, monai, musunyi wa ha mpala handi wakakonka, bobo bakatshina bua kulua nʼandi ha buihi.
31 Mose wakababikila; Alona ne bakokeshi bonso ba munkatshi muabo ba-kahingana kudiye; Mose wakasomba nʼabo.

15 Bakenza tukanu tua or tuakadi tuhindakana bu muonshi.
16 Bakenza masoso a or abidi ne nkanu ibidi, bakateka nkanu ei ibidi ku nseke ibidi ya tshilulu tshia ha tshiadi.
17 Bakabuesha tukanu tua or tuhindakana mu nkanu ibidi yakadi ku nseke ya tshilulu tshia ha tshiadi.
18 Baketeka nsonga ibidi mikuabo ya tukanu tuhindakana mu masoso abidi, nunku bakatuteka ha bilulu bia efoda bia ha maha ku mpala kua tshilulu.
19 Bakenza nkanu ya or ibidi bakayiteka ku luseke lua tshilulu tshia ha tshiadi luakadi lutuangane ne efoda.
20 Bakenza nkanu ya or ibidi, bakayiteka muinshi mua bilulu bia ha maha, ku mpala kuabi ne ku mutu kua mukaba muimpe wa efoda
21 Bakasanka tshilulu tshia ha tshiadi ne nkanu yatshi ku nkanu ya efoda ne muonshi wa bleu muhindakana tshilulu etshi tshiakashala hehi ne mukaba wa efoda, kabakatshisulula, bu muakambila Yehowa Mose.
22 Wakenza tshikowelu tshia efoda ne buanda bua bleu,
23 bakashiya disoso bua kubueshamu mutu, bakanyunguluka disoso ne muonshi muhindakana bu mudibo benza tshikowelu tshia nvita, bua tshikowelu katshihandiki nansha.
24 Bakafunda bifuanyikishi bia tumuma tukese tua grenade ne buanda bua bleu ne bukunzubile ne bukunze-kunzû kuinshi kua tshikowelu tshionso.
25 Bakenza ndibu ya or nkayandi, bakateka ndibu hankatshi ha tumuma tuakadi kuinshi kua tshikowelu,
26 kadibu ne kamuma kadibu ne kamuma kuinshi kua tshikowelu tshia kuenza nʼatshi mudimu, bu muakambila Yehowa Mose.
27 Bakenzela Alona ne bana bandi balume bikowelu bia lin,
28 ne bifulu bia lin, ne bilulu bikuabo bia ha mutu, ne mihanu ya lin,
29 ne mukaba wa lin ne bilulu bia bleu ne bikunzubile ne bikunze-kunzû, mudimu wa bantu ba lungenyi lule, bu muakambila Yehowa Mose.
30 Bakenza tshitotolo tshia tshijila tshia tshiamu tshia or, bakafundahu bu muakafundabo ha mabue ne, TSHINTU TSHIJIDILA KUDI YEHOWA.
31 Bakatekahu muonshi wa bleu muhindakana, bakatshisuika ha tshilulu, bu muakambila Yehowa Mose.
32 Nunku bakashikisha mudimu wonso wa tshitendelelu, ntenta wa kusangakena, Bena Isalele bakenza malu onso akambila Yehowa Mose.
33 Bakatuala nsubu wa tshitendelelu, Ntenta mene, kudi Mose, ne bintu bionso biakenzabo, ne nkanu ne mabaya, ne mitshi ne makunshi ne masoso a tshiamu,
34 ne tshintu tshia biseba bia bimpanga tshima mu mai makunze-kunzû tshia kuteka ha mutu ha nsubu, ne tshintu tshia biseba bia nkonge tshia kuteka ha mutu hatshi, ne tshilulu tshia hankatshi ha nsubu,
35 ne mushete wa tshihungidi tshia mei a Nzambi, ne mitshi ya mushete, ne tshibuikilu tshia mushete,
36 ne mesa, ne malonga onso a mesa, ne bidia bilesha,
37 ne bitekelu bia minda, ne minda ne malonga au onso, ne manyi a minda,
38 ne tshioshelu tshia tshiamu tshia or, ne manyi adibo bela ha mutu, ne musenga wa manananshi, ne tshilulu tshia kuteka ku mbelu kua nsubu,
39 ne tshioshelu tshia tshiamu tshia mitaku, ne biamu bitshiamakane, ne mitshi yatshi, ne malonga atshi onso, ne tshiowelu ne mesa atshi,
40 ne bilulu bia luhangu lua muaba wa kutendelela, ne makunshi a luhangu ne nkanu, ne tshilulu tshia tshibi tshia luhangu, ne mionshi yonso ne milonda ne bintu bionso bia kuenza nʼabi mudimu mu nsubu wa tshitendelelu, mbia ntenta wa kusangakena,
41 ne bilulu bitedibue bimpe bia kuenza nʼabi mudimu wa mu nsubu wa tshijila, ne bilulu bia tshijila bia Alona muakuidi, ne bilulu bia bana bandi balume bia kuenza nʼabi mudimu wa buakuidi.
42 Bena Isalele bakenza mudimu wonso wakambila Yehowa Mose.
43 Mose wakamona mudimu wonso wakenzabo, ne mona, baka wenza bu muakamba Yehowa Mose wakabandisha disanka.

Exodus 40
1 Yehowa wakambila Mose ne,
2 Halua dituku dia kumudilu dia ngondo wa kumudilu, ushimike ntenta wa kusambakena.
3 Ubueshemu mushete wa tshihungidi tshia mei a Nzambi, ubuikile mushete ne tshilulu tshia mu nsubu.
4 Uluemu ne mesa, ulongolole bintu bidihu, uluemu ne bitekelu bia minda, utekemu minda.
5 Uteke tshioshelu tshia or tshia musenga wa manananshi ku mpala kua mushete, ukudike tshilulu ku mbelu kua nsubu wa tshitendelelu.
6 Uteke tshioshelu tshia mulambu wa kuosha ku mbelu kua nsubu wa tshitendelelu.
7 Uteke tshiowelu hankatshi ha ntenta wa kusambakena ne tshioshelu tshia milambu, welemu mai.
8 Ushimike luhangu lua tshilulu lunyunguluke, ukudike tshilulu tshia ku mbelu kua luhangu.
9 Wangate manyi adibo bela ha mutu, wamiamine ha nsubu wa tshitendelelu ne ha bintu bionso bidimu, ujidile nsubu ne bintu biandi bionso, neikale mujidila.
10 Umuamine manyi ha tshioshelu tshia mulambu wa kuosha, ne ha bintu biatshi bionso, ujidile tshioshelu tshioshelu netshikale tshijidila.
11 Umiamine manyi ha tshiowelu ne ha mesa atshi, utshijidile.
12 Ulue ne Alona ne bana bandi balume ku mbelu kua ntenta wa kusambakena, uboweshe mai.
13 Uluatshishe Alona bilulu bijidila, umuele manyi, umujidike, bua angenzele biandi mudimu wa buakuidi.
14 Ulue ne bana bandi balume kabidi, ubaluatshishe bikowelu,
15 ubele manyi bu muwakela tatuʼabo bua kukuatshilabo mudimu wa buakuidibualu ebu nebubajidile mu mudimu wa buakuidi bua tshiendelele mu bikondo biabo bionso.
16 Mose wakenza nunku, wakenza malu onso akamuambila Yehowa.
17 Bakashimika nsubu wa tshitendelelu mu dituku dia kumudilu dia ngondo wa kumudilu mu tshidimu tshibidi.
18 Mose wakashimika nsubu, wakalongolola nkanu ne mabaya, wakabueshamu mitshi, wakajadika makunshi a nsubu.
19 Wakabikila nsubu ne tshilulu tshia ha mutu ha nsubu, wakateka tshilulu tshikuabo ha mutu hatshi, bu muakambila Yehowa Mose.
20 Wakabuesha mabue a mikenji mu mushete, wakateka mitshi ya kutuala nʼai mushete, wakateka tshibuikilu ha mutu ha mushete.
21 Wakalua ne mushete mu nsubu wa tshitendelelu, wakakudika tshilulu, wakajibikila mushete bu muakambila Yehowa Mose.
22 Wakateka mesa mu nsubu wa tshitendelelu ku luseke lua ku nôde, ku mpala kua tshilulu.
23 Wakalongolola mampa ha mesa ku mpala kua Yehowa, bu muakambila Yehowa Mose.
24 Wakateka bitekelu bia minda mu nsubu wa tshitendelelu ku mpala kua mesa ku luseke lua nsubu lua ku sude.
25 Wakatemesha minda ku mpala kua Yehowa bu muakambila Yehowa Mose.
26 Wakateka tshioshelu tshia or mu nsubu wa tshitendelelu ku mpala kua tshilulu,
27 wakoshahu musenga wa manananshi, bu muakambila Yehowa Mose.
28 Wakakudika tshilulu ku mbelu kua nsubu.
29 Wakateka tshioshelu tshia mulambu wa kuosha ku mbelu kua nsubu wa tshiendelele wakoshahu mulambu wa bukula bua manvua bu muakambila Yehowa Mose.
30 Wakateka tshiowelu hankatshi ha ntenta wa kusambakena ne tshioshelu tshia mulambu ya kuosha, wakelamu mai a kuowa,
31 akadi Mose ne Alona ne bana bandi balume bowesha nʼau bianza ne makasa.
32 Hakadibo babuela mu nsubu wa tshiendelele ne hakaluabo hehi ne tshioshelu, bakowa mai, bu muakambila Yehowa Mose.
33 Wakashimika luhangu ludi lunyunguluka nsubu wa tshitendelelu ne tshioshelu tshia milambu ya kuosha, wakakudika tshilulu ku mbelu kua luhangu. Mose wakashikisha mudimu wonso nunku.
34 Hashishe ditutu diakabuikila ntenta wa kusangakena, ne butumbi bua Yehowa buakûla nsubu tente.
35 Mose kakamanya mua kubuela mu ntenta bualu bua difutu diakimana ha mutu ha nsubu, ne butumbi bua Yehowa buakula nsubu tente.
36 Mu ngendu yabo yonso, hakabandishibua difutu ha mutu ha nsubu, Bena Isalele bakadi babanga luendu.
37 Kadi hakadi difutu kadiyi dibandishibua, kabakaya mu luendu tô ne ku dituku diakabandishibua difutu.
38 Bualu bua difutu dia Yehowa diakadi ha mutu ha nsubu mu munya, ne mu butuku kahia kakadihu, ku mesu kua Bena Isalele bonso mu ngendu yabo yonso.
