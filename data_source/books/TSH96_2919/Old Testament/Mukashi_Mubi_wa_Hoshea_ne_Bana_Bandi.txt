Hosea 1
1 Dî dia Yehowa diakalua kudi Hoshea, muana wa Beli, mu matuku a Uziya, Yotama, Ahaza ne Hezikiya, bakelenge ba Yuda, ne mu matuku a Yalobama, muana wa Yoasha, mukelenge wa Isalele.
2 Ku tshibangidilu hakayikila Yehowa ne Hoshea, Yehowa wakamuambila ne, Ya biebe, ubake mukashi wa masandi, ulele nʼandi bana ba mu masandi; bualu bua ba mu buloba ebu badi balonda malu a masandi, bahambuke kudi Yehowa.
3 Nunku wakaya biandi, wakabaka Gomê, muana wa bakashi wa Dibelayama; ne yeye wakimita difu, wakamulelela muana wa balume.
4 Yehowa wakamuambila ne, Umuidike dina ne, Yizelele
5 Mu dituku adi nentshibule buta bua Bena Isalele mu tshibanda tshia Yizelele.
6 Yeye wakimita difu kabidi, wakalela muana wa bakashi. Yehowa wakambila Hoshea ne, Umuidike dina ne, Udibo-kabayi-bafuila-luse
7 Kadi nenfuile Bena Yuda luse; nembahandishe ku bukole bua Yehowa Nzambi wabo, tshiena mbahandisha ku buta, anyi ku muele wa nvita, anyi ku nvita anyi ku tubalu anyi kudi bena tubalu.
8 Bakajila Udibo-kabayi-bafuila-luse mabele, Gomê wakimita difu dikuabo, wakalela muana wa balume.
9 Yehowa wakamba ne, Umuidike dina ne, Kambantu-banyi
10 Kadi bungi bua Bena Isalele nebuikale bu nsenga ya ku mai manene, idibo kabayi mua kuidikisha anyi kubala; ne mu muaba wakadibo bamba ne, Kanuena bantu banyi, nebabambile ne, Nudi bana balume ba Nzambi wa muoyo.
11 Nebasangishe Bena Yuda ne Bena Isalele hohamue, ne bobo bine nebadisunguile muntu umue bua kuikaleye ha mutu habo; nebumuke mu buloba ebu, bua dituku dia Yizelele nedikale dinene.

Hosea 2
1 Ambilai bana benu ba balume ne, Nudi bantu banyi; ambilai bana benu ba bakashi ne, Nuakuheta luse.
2 Sengelelai mamu wenu, numusengelele;
3 bua tshiadi bivualu biandi,
4 Bulelela, tshiena nfuila bana bandi luse,
5 Bualu bua mamu wabo wakulonda malu a masandi;
6 Nunku nenjike nshila wandi ne luhangu lua meba,
7 Yeye nealonde bamunangi, kadi kena ubaheta;
8 Yeye kakamanya ne mmeme mene nakamuha manvua,
9 Nunku nengangate kabidi manvua anyi mu tshidimu tshiau,
10 Mpindiu nensokolole bundu buandi kudi bamunangi,
11 Nendekeshe miloû yandi yonso,
12 Nenkose mionshi yandi ya tumuma tua nvinyo,
13 Nunku Yehowa udi wamba ne, Nendue kudiye ne makenga
14 Nunku, mona, nemmuitabushishe, nemmulombole mu tshihela,
15 Hashishe, nemmuhingashileku madimi andi a tumuma tua nvinyo,
# nemmuhe tshibanda tshia Akola
16 Yehowa udi wamba ne, Mu dituku adi yeye neambikile ne, Bayanyi; kena umbikila ne, Nfumu wanyi.
17 Bualu bua nengumushe mena a Ba-Bâla mukana muandi, kabena batela mena abo kabidi.
18 Mu dituku adi kabidi nembahungile tshihungidi ne nyama ya mu mpata, nyunyu ya mulu ne bintu bidi bidikoka hanshi; nentshibule buta ne muele wa nvita ne bielelu bia nvita, nemmumushe mu buloba, nembaladike hanshi kabayi ne ntatu.
19 Nenkubangile kabidi bua kukubumbakasha kundi tshiendelele; bulelela, nenkubangile bua kukutuangasha nʼanyi mu buakane ne mu kulumbulula kuakane ne mu dinanga ne mu kukufuila kua luse.
20 Nenkubangile ne kuditshiha kûdi mua kueyemena, ne wewe neumanye Yehowa.
21 Yehowa udi wamba ne, Mu dituku adi nengitabe,
22 ne buloba nebukudimune manvua, nvinyo ne manyi dî,
23 ne meme nemmumiamine mu buloba bua bualu buanyi;

Hosea 3
1 Yehowa wakangambila ne, Ya kabidi, nanga mukashi udi munangibue kudi mulunda wandi ne udi muena masandi, bu mudi Yehowa unanga Bena Isalele, nansha bobo bafila mitshima yabo kudi nzambi mikuabo ne bananga mikata ya tumuma tua nvinyo.
2 Nunku nakamusela ku bituha bia argent dikumi ne bitanu, ne ku homer
3 Nakamuambila ne, Bûdi nʼabu mbua kuikala muena kuanyi matuku a bungi; kulondi malu a masandi, kuikadi mukashi wa muntu mukuabo; ne meme nengikale muomumue kûdi.
4 Bualu bua Bena Isalele nebashale matuku a bungi kabayi ne mukelenge, kabayi ne mukokeshi, kabayi ne milambu mishihela Yehowa, kabayi ne dikunshi dia tshijila, kabayi ne tshilamba tshia efoda
5 Hashishe, Bena Isalele nebahingane ne nebakebe Yehowa Nzambi wabo ne Davidi mukelenge wabo; nebalue kudi Yehowa ne ditshina, ne ku buimpe buandi mu matuku adi alua.

Hosea 4
1 Unvuai dî dia Yehowa, nuenu Bena Isalele!
2 Anu kuditshiha ne mitshihu ya mashimi,
3 Bua mianda yaya buloba budi buasa muadi,
4 Kadi muntu kabambakashi mei ne mukuabo, kabandi mukuabo;
5 Ne wewe, muakuidi, neulenduke mu munya;
6 Bantu banyi badi babutudibua bualu bua kabena ne lungenyi;
7 Bu muakakumbanabo ba bungi,
8 Badi badia bukute bua malu mabi a bantu banyi,
9 Bualu ebu nebuikaleku ne, Bu mudi bantu, mbu mudi muakuidi;
10 Nebadie, kadi kabena bukuta;
11 Masandi ne maluvu ne nvinyo mihia-mihia
12 Bantu banyi badi balomba lungenyi ku tshibasa tshia mutshi,
13 Badi bashihela mpingu milambu ha mitu ha mikuna mile,
14 Tshiena nkengesha bana benu ba bakashi habuelabo mu bundumba,
15 Bena Isalele, nansha binualonda malu a masandi,
16 Bena Isalele bakulua bena tshitshu
17 Bena Efelayima bakudilamika kudi mpingu yabo,
18 Kunua kuabo kua maluvu kudi kuamba kushika,
19 Luhehele luakuya nʼabo mu mahuahua alu;

Hosea 5
1 Unvuai dî edi, nuenu bakuidi!
2 Bakumbula dijimba dile ku Shitima,
3 Ndi mumanye Bena Efelayima,
4 Bienzedi biabo kabiena bikumbana
5 Dihetu dia Bena Isalele didi dibahisha ku mesu kuabo,
6 Nebaye ne bisumbu biabo bia mikoko ne ngombe
7 Bobo bakutungila Yehowa tshitungu,
8 Elai lusengu mu Gibea,
9 Bena Efelayima nebabutudibue
10 Bakokeshi ba mu Yuda bakulua
11 Bena Efelayima badi bakengeshibue,
12 Nunku meme ndi bu tshibayibayi kudi Bena Efelayima,
13 Hakamona Bena Efelayima disama diabo,
# bakatuma dî kudi Mukelenge Yaleba.
14 Bualu bua nengikale bu nyama
15 Nenye, nempingane ku muaba wanyi,

Hosea 6
1 Luai bienu, tuhingane kudi Yehowa;
2 Hahita matuku abidi, yeye neahingashe;
3 Tumumanye bietu, tunanukile kukeba kumanya Yehowa.
4 Nuenu. Bena Efelayima, nennuenzele tshinyi?
5 Nunku nakubafila ku mei a baprofete,
6 Bualu bua ndi musue kudifila kuenu, kankufila kua milambu;
7 Kadi bobo bakashiha tshihungidi tshianyi bu muakenza Adama;
8 Gilada udi musoko wa benji ba malu mabi,
9 Bu mudi bisumbu bia banyengi balamina muntu,
10 Munkatshi mua Bena Isalele nakumona muanda muhite bubi;
11 Bua bualu buenu, Bena Yuda bakunusunguila tshikondo tshia makenga,

Hosea 7
1 Hanakusua kushikisha disama a dia Bena Isalele,
2 Kabena belangana meji mu mitshima yabo ne,
3 Badi basankisha mukelenge ne bubi buabo,
4 Buonso buabo badi bena masandi;
5 Mu dituku dia disanka dia mukelenge wetu
6 Bualu bua bakuvuisha mitshima yabo bu nfulu,
7 Buonso buabo badi batema bu nfulu,
8 Bena Efelayima badi babuelakana ne malu a bisamba bia bantu;
9 Bantu ba bende badi badia bukole buabo,
10 Dihetu dia Bena Isalele didi dibahisha,
11 Bena Efelayima badi bu nyunyu wa nkutshi muhote udi kayi ne lungenyi;
12 Hatshidibo baya, nenvungulule bukuondo buanyi ku mpala kuabo;
13 Mulau wikale kudibo, bualu bua bakuhambuka!
14 Kabakudia kundi ne mitshima yabo,
15 Nakubayisha ne nakukolesha maboko abo,
16 Badi bahingana, kadi kabena bahingana kudi Nzambi wa mu diulu,

Hosea 8
1 Teka mpungi ku mishiku yebe!
2 Nebadile kundi ne,
3 Bena Isalele bakubenga malu adi makane;
4 Bakadisunguila bakelenge, tshiyi mubambile;
5 Bena Samalea, nakûmusha muanʼa ngombe wenu!
6 Luhingu elu luakufuma ku buloba bua Isalele;
7 Bua bobo badi bakuna luhehele, nebahuole nvunde;
8 Bakumana kumina Bena Isalele.
9 Bualu bua bakuhanda ku Ashû,
10 Nansha biadiabo bulunda ne ba munkatshi mua bisamba bia bantu,
11 Bualu bua Bena Efelayima bakuvudisha bioshelu bua kuenzelahu malu mabi,
12 Nansha bina bakindila mayisha mangi a mu mikenji yanyi
13 Badi bafila milambu ya nyama minshihela,
14 Bua Bena Isalele bakuhua Mufuki wabo muoyo,

Hosea 9
1 Bena Isalele, kanusanki bua kutumba bu mudi ba mu bisamba bia bende hatumba!
2 Tshishihuidi ne tshikaminu kabiena bibadisha kabidi,
3 Kabena bashala mu buloba bua Yehowa,
4 Kabena bahongoluela Yehowa milambu ya nvinyo,
5 Nenuenze tshinyi mu disanka dia kudia bia kudia,
6 Monai, bakuya kukuabo bualu bua kubutuka kua buloba buabo,
7 Matuku a kubakengesha akufika,
8 Bena Efelayima badi balamina Nzambi wanyi;
9 Bakudinyanga ne tshinyangu tshikole,
10 Nakasangana Bena Isalele
11 Butumbi bua Bena Efelayima nebûmuke kudibo bu mudi nyunyu ubuka,
12 Nansha biakoleshabo bana,
13 Bu munakadianjila kumona, bana ba balume ba Bena Efelayima
14 Yehowa, ubahe;
15 Bubi buabo buonso budi mu Gilegala,
16 Ba mu Efelayima bakutahibua,
17 Nzambi wanyi neabihate,

Hosea 10
1 Bena Isalele badi bu muonshi udi ulamba bikole,
2 Mitshima yabo idi mitahuluke nseke ibidi;
3 Bulelela, nebambe biabo ne,
4 Badi bamba mei a hatuhu;
5 Bua muana wa ngombe wa ku Bete-awena
6 Nebaye nʼandi kabidi ku Ashû,
# bua kumufila bu mulambu kudi Mukelenge Yaleba.
7 Mukelenge wa mu Samalea neabutudibue
8 Bitendelelu bia ha mukuna wa Awena nebibutudibue,
9 Ku matuku a ku Gibea tô ne mpindiu, nuenu Bena Isalele nuakuenza bibi;
10 Hanasua kubakengesha, nembakengeshe;
11 Bena Efelayima bakadi bu lukungulu lua ngombe luakuyishabo,
12 Nudikunyine buakane,
13 Nunkukuna malu mabi,
14 Nunku diyoyo nedijuke munkatshi mua bana benu,
15 Nuenu Bena Isalele, nebanuenzele nunku

Hosea 11
1 Hakadi Bena Isalele bana, nakabananga,
2 Kadi hanakananukila kubabikila,
3 Kadi meme nguakayisha Bena Efelayima mua kuendabo;
4 Nakabakoka ne mionshi idibo bakoka nʼai muntu,
5 Kabena bahingana mu buloba bua Ejipitu;
6 Muele wa nvita neufike mu misoko yabo,
7 Bantu banyi badi basue anu kuntombokela;
8 Bena Efelayima, ndi mua kunulekela munyi?
9 Tshiena ngenza malu onso adi bukole bua tshiji tshianyi bingambila nansha,
10 Nebalonde Yehowa;
11 Nebafume ku Ejipitu bazakale bu nyunyu,
12 Bena Efelayima badi bannyunguluka ne mashimi,

Hosea 12
1 Bena Efelayima badi badidisha ne luhehele,
2 Yehowa udi ne tshilumbu tshia kulumbuluisha Bena Yuda,
3 Hakadiye mu difu dia mamuʼandi, wakakuata muanʼabo ku tshikankanya;
4 Eyo, wakaluangana ne muanjelo, wakamutamba bukole;
5 Yehowa udi Nzambi wa misumba,
6 Nunku hinganai kudi Nzambi wenu,
7 Bena Efelayima badi bangendʼa mushinga,
8 Bena Efelayima bakuamba ne, Tuakulua babanji,
9 Kadi meme ndi Yehowa Nzambi wenu,
10 Nakambila baprofete mei,
11 Bikala Gilada tshintu tshia hatuhu,
12 Yakoba wakanyemena mu madimi a mu Alama;
13 Yehowa wakaluhula Bena Isalele mu Ejipitu ku bulombodi bua muprofete,
14 Bena Efelayima bakamukuatshisha tshiji tshikole;

Hosea 13
1 Hakamba Bena Efelayima dî, buowa buakakuata bantu;
2 Mpindiu badi bananukila kulonda malu mabi,
3 Bua muanda eu nebikale bu ditutu dia lunkelu,
4 Kadi meme ndi Yehowa Nzambi wenu, kutuadisha ku buloba bua Ejipitu tô ne lelu,
5 Nakanumanya mu tshihela tshia lusenga,
6 Hakadibo mu buloba bua mashinde, bakukuta;
7 Nunku nengikale bu nyama wa ntambue kudibo,
8 Nembabunde bu nkashama udibo banyenga bana bandi,
9 Bena Isalele, eku akubutuka kuenu,
10 Mukelenge wenu udi kudi kunyi bua kunuhandisheye mu misoko yenu yonso?
11 Nakunuha mukelenge mu tshifikʼa munda tshianyi,
12 Dihila dia Bena Efelayima didi disuikibue mu dibuki,
13 Didinyenga didi dikuata mukashi udi wamba kulela nedibakuate mene,
14 Nembahikule ku bukole bua Muaba wa Bafue, anyi?
15 Nansha biavudishabo bana munkatshi mua bana babo,
16 Bena Samalea nebahishibue

Hosea 14
1 Nuenu, Bena Isalele, hinganai kudi Yehowa Nzambi wenu,
2 Nuye ne mei makane,
3 Bena Ashû kabena batusungila;
4 Meme nengondahe, nenshikishe kutomboka kuabo;
5 Nengikale bu mume kudi Bena Isalele,
6 matamba abo neabaluluke;
7 Nebahingane, nebashikame mu dileshi dianyi,
8 Bena Efelayima, mpingu yenu idi ne mushinga kai kundi kabidi?
9 Udi ne meji ajingulule malu â;
