Song of Solomon 1
1 Musambu wa mu misambu, udi wa Solomo.
2 Utuangane nʼanyi mishiku,
3 manyi ebe adi ne dihembu dimpe,
4 Unkoke; netukulonde lukasa.
5 Nuenu bana bakashi ba mu Yelushalema,
6 Kanuntangidi bualu bua dikoba dianyi didi difike,
7 Wewe, udi mutshima wanyi munange,
8 Wewe mukashi udi uhita bakuabo bulengele,
9 Wanyi munanga, nakakuelekesha
10 Matama ebe adi malengele bua mabue adi malembelelahu,
11 Netukulongoluele bilenga bia or bilamika argent.
12 Hakadi mukelenge mushikame ku mesa andi,
13 Wanyi munanga udi kundi bu kabombi ka myrrhe
14 Wanyi munanga udi kundi bu tshisumbu tshia bilongo
15 Mona, wanyi munanga, wewe udi mulengele,
16 Mona, wanyi munanga, wewe udi mulengele,
17 Mikamba ya mu nsubu muetu idi ya mitshi ya cèdre

Song of Solomon 2
1 Meme ndi bu tshilongo tshia mu Shalona
2 Bu mudi tshilongo tshia nsungubidi munkatshi mua meba,
3 Bu mudi mutshi wa mamuma a pommes munkatshi mua mitshi ya ditu,
4 Yeye wakalua nʼanyi mu nsubu wa dibanji,
5 Unkoleshe ne tumuma tukese tûme,
6 Tshianza tshiandi tshia bakashi tshidi bu musamu ku mutu wanyi;
7 Nuenu, bana ba bakashi ba mu Yelushalema, ndi nnusengelela ne,
8 Dî dia wanyi munanga!
9 Wanyi munanga udi bu ngulungu anyi bu lukungulu lua ntole.
10 Wanyi munanga wakungambila ne,
11 Bualu bua tshikondo tshia mashika tshiakuhita;
12 Bilongo bidi bimena mu buloba;
13 Mamuma a mitshi ya nfigi akuboba;
14 Wewe, nyunyu wanyi wa nkutshi,
15 Utukuatshile tunyama,
16 Wanyi munanga nguanyi, meme ndi wandi kabidi;
17 tô ne hatalala munya

Song of Solomon 4
1 Mona, wanyi munanga, wewe udi mulengele,
2 Menu ebe adi bu tshisumbu tshia mikoko yakakosabo miosa,
3 Mishiku yebe idi bu buanda bukunze-kunzû;
4 Nshingu webe udi bu tshibunda tshia Davidi,
5 Mabele ebe abidi adi bu bana ba ngulungu,
6 Hatalala munya ne haleha mi ndidimbi,
7 Wanyi munanga, wewe udi mulengele be;
8 Mukashi wanyi muhungile, umuke nʼanyi ku Lebanona;
9 Wewe, muanʼetu mukashi, mukashi wanyi muhungile,
10 Muanʼetu mukashi, mukashi wanyi mubangile, dinanga diebe didi dimpe be!
11 Mukashi wanyi mubangile, mishiku yebe idi bu dikatshi dia buitshi;
12 Muanʼetu mukashi, mukashi wanyi mubangile,
13 Wewe udi bu mitoloko mimene mu tshibunda tshia mamuma a grenade
14 nard ne safran, cannelle ne cinnamone,
15 Wewe udi bu mushimi wa mai mu tshibunda tshia bilongo,
16 Luhehele lua ku nôde, tabala,

Song of Solomon 5
1 Muanʼetu mukashi, mukashi wanyi mubangile,
# Nakasangisha myrrhe
2 Nakadi mulale tulu, kadi mutshima wanyi wakadi mutabale;
3 Nakavula bilulu bianyi,
4 Wanyi munanga wakabuesha tshianza tshiandi ku dididishi,
5 Nakajuka bua kunzulula wanyi munanga tshibi;
6 Nakunzuluila wanyi munanga tshibi,
7 Bansentedi badi bendakana ku musoko,
8 Nuenu bana ba bakashi ba mu Yelushalema, ndi nnusengelela ne,
9 Webe munanga udi uhita bakuabo munyi,
10 Wanyi munanga udi mutoke ne mpala wandi udi lunkunjilakunjila;
11 Mutu wandi udi bu or wa mushinga mukole be;
12 Mesu andi adi bu a nyunyu ya nkutshi ibidi mishikame kukala kua mai,
13 Matama andi adi bu bibunda bia bintu bia dihembu dimpe
# manananshi a myrrhe
14 Bianza biandi bidi bu tukanu tua or
15 Mikolo yandi idi bu makunshi a mabue masonga
16 Mei a mukana muandi adi mashema;

Song of Solomon 6
1 Webe munanga wakuya kudi kunyi,
2 Wanyi munanga wakuya ku tshibunda tshiandi,
3 Meme ndi wa wanyi munanga; wanyi munanga nguanyi kabidi;
4 Wanyi munanga, wewe udi mulengele bu mukuna wa Tisa:
5 Umusha mesu ebe kundi,
6 Menu ebe adi bu tshisumbu tshia mikoko
7 Matama ebe adi bu bihela bia dimuma dia grenade
8 Bakelenge bakashi makumi asambombo badiku, ne bakashi bahika babakibue makumi muanda mukulu badiku,
9 Wanyi nyunyu wa nkutshi udi muakane be, udi anu umue umuehele;
10 Nganyi udi ufuanangana ne lunkelu,
11 Nakaya bianyi mu tshibunda tshia mamuma,
12 Diambedi tshiyi muanji kumanya bualu,
13 Hingana, hingana, wewe Muena Shulama;

Song of Solomon 7
1 Wewe muana wa mukelenge,
2 Mubidi webe udi luhanza luenza ne dibumba,
3 Mabele ebe abidi adi bu bana ba mahasa ba ngulungu.
4 Nshingu webe udi bu tshibumba tshia mubanga.
5 Mutu webe udi ufuanangana ne mukuna wa Kâmele;
6 Wewe udi mulengele be, udi unsankisha,
7 Mubidi webe udi bu dibue dia ngashi,
8 Nakamba ne, Nembande ku dibue dia ngashi,
9 Mukana muebe muikale bu nvinyo ya mushinga mukole;
10 Ndi wa wanyi munanga,
11 Lua biebe, wanyi munanga,
12 Tujuke lunkelu bua kuya mu madimi a tumuma tukese;
13 Tumuma tua mandragores tudi tutuma muhuya muimpe;

Song of Solomon 8
1 Bu wewe muikale muanʼetu wa balume,
2 Nakadi kukulombola,
3 Tshianza tshiandi tshia bakashi
4 Ndi nnutshihisha, nuenu bana bakashi ba mu Yelushalema, ne,
5 Udi ufuma ku tshihela
6 Unteke mu mutshima webe bu tshimanyinu
7 Misulu ya mai ya bungi kayena mimanye mua kujima dinanga,
8 Tudi ne muakunyʼetu wa bakashi
9 Bikaleye bu tshimanu tshia nsubu,
10 Meme ndi bu tshimanu tshia nsubu,
11 Solomo wakadi ne budimi bua tumuma tukese mu Bâla-hamona,
12 Budimi buanyi, budi buanyi nkayanyi, budi ku mpala kuanyi;
13 Wewe udi mushikame mu bibunda,
14 Wanyi munanga, ulue lukasa;
