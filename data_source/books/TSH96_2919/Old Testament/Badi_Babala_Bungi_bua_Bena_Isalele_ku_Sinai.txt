Numbers 1
1 Yehowa wakambila Mose mu tshihela tshia Sinai, mu ntenta wa kusambakena, ku dituku dia kumudilu dia ngondo muibidi mu tshidimu tshibidi tshia kuluhuka kuabo mu buloba bua Ejipitu, ne,
2 Ubale bungi bua Bena Isalele bonso, ku meku abo ne ku nsubu ya batatuʼabo, bungi bua mena a balume, muntu ne mundi,
3 wewe ne Alona nubange kudi balume bonso ba mu Isalele bakahita bidimu makumi abidi to ne ha mutu, nubale buonso buabo ku tshisumbu ne ku tshisumbu badi bamanye mua kuya mu nvita.
4 Nuye bienu ne muntu wa mu tshisamba muntu wa mu tshisamba, muntu yonso udi ku mutu kua nsubu wa batatuʼabo.
5 Au mmena a balume badi mua kuimana nʼenu. Wa mu tshisamba tshia Lubena, Elisu muana mulume wa Shedeu.
6 Wa mu tshisamba tshia Shimeona, Shelumiele muana mulume wa Sulishadai.
7 Wa mu tshisamba tshia Yuda, Nashona muana mulume wa Aminadaba.
8 Wa mu tshisamba tshia Yisakâ, Netanaele muana mulume wa Sua.
9 Wa mu tshisamba tshia Zebuluna, Ehaba muana mulume wa Helona.
10 Wa munkatshi mua bana ba Yosefe, wa mu tshisamba tshia Efelayima, Elishama muana mulume wa Amihuda. Wa mu tshisamba tshia Manashe, Gamaliele muana mulume wa Pedasû.
11 Wa mu tshisamba tshia Benyamina, Abidana muana mulume wa Gideom.
12 Wa mu tshisamba tshia Dana, Ahiezê muana mulume wa Amishadai.
13 Wa mu tshisamba tshia Ashe, Pagiele muana mulume wa Okelana.
14 Wa mu tshisamba tshia Gada, Eleyasafa muana mulume wa Deuele.
15 Wa mu tshisamba tshia Nafatale, Ahila muana mulume wa Enana.
16 Aba mbantu bakabikilabo ba mu tshisumbu tshia Bena Isalele, bakelenge ba bisamba bia batatuʼabo, bakadi ku mutu kua binunu bia Bena Isalele.
17 Mose ne Alona bakangata balume aba bakabikilabo ku mena abo
18 bakasangisha tshisumbu tshionso tshia Bena Isalele ku dituku dia kumudilu dia ngondo muibidi, bakababadila milongo ya bankambua babo ku meku abo, ku nsubu ya batatuʼabo, bungi bua mena abo ku bidimu makumi abidi tô ne ha mutu, muntu ne muntu.
19 Bu muakambila Yehowa Mose, wakababadila mu tshihela tshia Sinai.
20 Bana ba Lubena, muanʼa bute wa Isalele, ku bikondo biabo, ku meku abo, ku nsubu ya batatuʼabo, bungi bua mena abo muntu ne muntu, ku bidimu makumi abidi tô ne ha mutu balume bonso bakamanya mua kuya mu nvita,
21 bakabala ba mu tshisamba tshia Lubena binunu makami anai ne bisambombo ne nkama itanu.
22 Bana ba Shimeona, ku bikondo biabo, ku meku abo, ku nsubu ya batatuʼabo, bungi bua mena abo muntu ne muntu, balume bonso ku bidimu makumi abidi to ne ha mutu, bakamanya mua kuya mu nvita,
23 bakabala ba mu tshisamba tshia Shimeona binunu makumi atanu ne tshitema ne nkama isatu.
24 Bana ba Gada ku bikondo biabo, ku meku abo, ku nsubu ya batatuʼabo, bungi bua mena abo, ku bidimu makumi abidi tô ne ha mutu, bakamanya mua kuya mu nvita;
25 bakabala ba mu tshisamba tshia Gada binunu makumi anai ne bitanu ne nkama isambombo ne makumi atanu.
26 Bana ba Yuda ku bikondo biabo, ku meku abo, ku nsubu ya batatuʼabo, bungi bua mena abo ku bidimu makumi abidi tô ne ha mutu, bonso bakamanya mua kuya mu nvita;
27 bakabala ba mu tshisamba tshia Yuda binunu makumi muanda mutekete ne binai ne nkama isambombo.
28 Bana ba Yisakâ ku bikondo biabo, ku meku abo, ku nsubu ya batatuʼabo, bungi bua mena abo ku bidimu makumi abidi tô ne ha mutu, bonso bakamanya mua kuya mu nvita,
29 bakabala ba tshisamba tshia Yisakâ binunu makumi atanu ne binai ne nkama mai.
30 Bana ba Zebuluna ku bikondo biabo, ku meku abo, ku nsubu ya batatuʼabo, bungi bua mena abo ku bidimu makumi abidi tô ne ha mutu, bonso bakamanya mua kuya mu nvita;
31 bakabala ba mu tshisamba tshia Zebuluna binunu makumi atanu ne muanda mutekete ne nkama mai.
32 Bana ba Yosefe ba mu tshisamba tshia Efelayima ku bikondo biabo, ku meku abo, ku nsubu ya batatuʼabo, bungi bua mena abo ku bidimu makumi abidi tô ne ha mutu, bonso bakamanya mua kuya mu nvita,
33 bakabala ba mu tshisamba tshia Efelayima binunu makumi anai ne nkama itanu.
34 Bana ba Manashe ku bikondo biabo, ku meku abo, ku nsubu ya batatuʼabo, bungi bua mena abo ku bidimu makumi abidi tô ne ha mutu, bonso bakamanya mua kuya mu nvita,
35 bakabala ba mu tshisamba tshia Manashe binunu makumi asatu ne bibidi ne nkama ibidi.
36 Bana ba Benyamina ku bikondo biabo, ku meku abo, ku nsubu ya batatuʼabo, bungi bua mena abo ku bidimu makumi abidi tô ne ha mutu, bonso bakamanya mua kuya mu nvita;
37 bakabala ba mu tshisamba tshia Benyamina binunu makumi asatu ne bitanu ne nkama mai.
38 Bana ba Dana ku bikondo biabo, ku meku abo, ku nsubu ya batatuʼabo, bungi bua mena abo ku bidimu makumi abidi to ne ha mutu, bonso bakamanya mua kuya mu nvita,
39 bakabala ba mu tshisamba tshia Dana binunu makumi asambombo ne bibidi ne nkama muanda mutekete.
40 Bana ba Ashê ku bikondo biabo, ku meku abo, ku nsubu ya batatuʼabo, bungi bua mena abo ku bidimu makumi abidi tô ne ha mutu, bonso bakamanya mua kuya mu nvita,
41 bakabala ba mu tshisamba tshia Ashê binunu makumi anai ne tshimue ne nkama itanu.
42 Bana ba Nafatale ku bikondo biabo, ku meku abo, ku nsubu ya batatuʼabo, bungi bua mena abo ku bidimu makumi abidi tô ne ha mutu, bonso bakamanya mua kuya mu nvita,
43 bakabala ba mu tshisamba tshia Nafatale binunu makumi atanu ne bisatu ne nkama mai.
44 Aba mbantu bakabalabo kudi Mose ne Alona, ne kudi bakelenge ba Bena Isalele dikumi ne babidi, muntu ku nsubu wa batatuʼabo muntu ku nsubu wa batatuʼabo.
45 Nunku bungi bua Bena Isalele buakabalabo ku nsubu ya batatuʼabo, ku bidimu makumi abidi tô ne ha mutu, bonso bakamanya mua kuya mu nvita mu Isalele
46 bungi bua buonso buabo buakabalabo buakadi binunu nkama isambombo ne bisatu ne nkama itanu ne makumi atanu.
47 Kadi kabakabala Bena Lewi munkatshi muabo ku tshisamba tshia batatuʼabo.
48 Bualu bua Yehowa wakambila Mose ne,
49 Kubadi tshisamba tshia Lewi nansha, kubadi bungi buabo munkatshi mua Bena Isalele,
50 kadi usungule Bena Lewi bua kuenzabo mudimu mu nsubu wa tshitendelelu, ne wa bintu bionso bidimu bobo nebatuale nsubu ne bintu bionso bidimu, bobo nebabibale, nebashikame ha buihi ne nsubu wa tshitendelelu.
51 Hayabo ne nsubu wa tshitendelelu mu nshila, Bena Lewi nebatule nsubu, ne halongololabo tshitudilu, Bena Lewi nebamushimike, bialua muenyi ha buihi nebamushihe.
52 Bena Isalele nebadishimikile ntenta yabo, mundi ne muntu ku tshitudilu tshiandi, hehi ne dibendele diandi ku bisumbu biabo.
53 Kadi Bena Lewi nebashimike ntenta yabo minyunguluke nsubu wa tshitendelelu, bua mulau kaukuatshi Bena Isalele Bena Lewi nebabale nsubu wa tshitendelelu.
54 Bena Isalele bakenza nunku, bakenza malu onso akambila Yehowa Mose.

Numbers 2
1 Yehowa wakambila Mose ne Alona ne,
2 Muntu ne muntu wa munkatshi mua Bena Isalele nenshimike ntenta wandi hehi ne dibendele diandi, didi ne bimanyinu bia nsubu ya batatuʼabo bifundahu, nebashimike ntenta yabo minyunguluke tshitendelelu mitangile ku tshitendelelu.
3 Bantu badi ne bimanyinu bia dibendele dia tshisamba tshia Yuda ku tshisumbu ne ku tshisumbu nebashikame ku luseke lua ku esete kudi diba dihatuka. Nashona muana mulume wa Aminadaba neikale mukelenge wa bana ba Yuda.
4 Bantu bandi bakamubadilabo bakadi binunu makumi muanda mutekete ne binai ne nkama isambombo.
5 Bantu ba mu tshisamba tshia Yisakâ nebashikame hehi nʼandi. Netanele muana mulume wa Sua neikale mukelenge wa bana ba Yisakâ.
6 Bantu bandi bakamubadilabo bakadi binunu makumi atanu ne binai ne nkama mai.
7 Tshisamba tshia Zebuluna, Eliaba muana mulume wa Helona neikale mukelenge wa bana ba Zebuluna.
8 Bantu bandi bakamubadilabo bakadi binunu makumi atanu ne muanda mutekete ne nkama mai.
9 Bantu bonso bakabalabo ba mu tshitudilu tshia Yuda bakadi binunu lukama ne makumi muanda mukulu ne bisambombo ne nkama mai ku bisumbu biabo Bobo nebaye ku mpala.
10 Nebateke dibendele dia tshitudilu tshia Lubena ku luseke lua ku sude. Elisu muana mulume wa Shedeu neikale mukelenge wa bana ba Lubena.
11 Bantu bandi bakamubadilabo bakadi binunu makumi anai ne bisambombo ne nkama itanu.
12 Bantu ba mu tshisamba tshia Shimeona nebashikame hehi nʼandi Shelumiele muana mulume wa Sulishadai neikale mukelenge wa bana ba Shimeona.
13 Bantu bandi bakamubadilabo bakadi binunu makumi atanu ne tshitema ne nkama isatu.
14 Tshisamba tshia Gada, Eleyasafa muana mulume wa Luele neikale mukelenge wa bana ba Gada.
15 Bantu bandi bakamubadilabo bakadi binunu makumi anai ne bitanu ne nkama isambombo ne makumi atanu.
16 Bantu bonso bakabalabo ba mu tshitudilu tshia Lubena bakadi binunu lukama ne makumi atanu ne tshimue ne nkama mai ne makumi atanu Bobo nebatonde ku nyima, tshisumbu tshibidi.
17 Nunku nebaye ne ntenta wa kusambakena ne tshisumbu tshia Bena Lewi tshikala munkatshi mua bisumbu bikuabo bu mudibo bashikama mu bitudilu, nebaye ku mpala nunku, muntu mu muaba wandi mundi mu muaba wandi ku mabendele abo.
18 Nebateke dibendele dia tshitudilu tshia Efelayima ku luseke lua ku wesete ku bisumbu biabo Elishama muana mulume wa Amihuda neikale mukelenge wa bana ba Efelayima
19 Bantu bandi bakamubadilabo bakadi binunu makumi anai ne nkama itanu.
20 Bantu ba mu tshisamba tshia Manashe nebashikame hehi nʼandi. Gamaliele muana mulume wa Pedasu neikale mukelenge wa bana ba Manashe.
21 Bantu bakamubadilabo bakadi binunu makumi asatu ne bibidi ne nkama ibidi.
22 Tshisamba tshia Benyamina Abidana muana mulume wa Gideoni neikale mukelenge wa bana ba Benyamina.
23 Bantu bandi bakamubadilabo bakadi binunu makumi asatu ne bitanu ne nkama mai.
24 Bantu bonso bakabalabo ba mu tshitudilu tshia Efelayima bakadi binunu lukama ne muanda mukulu ne lukama lumue ku bisumbu biabo. Bobo nebabalonde ku nyima, tshisumbu tshisatu.
25 Nebateke dibendele dia tshitudilu tshia Dana ku luseke lua ku nôde. Ahiezê muana mulume wa Amishadai neikale mukelenge wa bana ba Dana.
26 Bantu bandi bakamubadilabo bakadi binunu makumi asambombo ne bibidi ne nkama muanda mutekete.
27 Bantu ba mu tshisamba tshia Ashe nebashikame hehi nʼandi Pagiele muana mulume wa Okelana neikale mukelenge wa bana ba Ashe.
28 Bantu bandi bakamubadilabo bakadi binunu makumi anai ne tshimue ne nkama itanu.
29 Tshisamba tshia Nafatale Ahila muana mulume wa Enana neikale mukelenge wa bana ba Nafatale.
30 Bantu bandi bakamubadilabo bakadi binunu makumi atanu ne bisatu ne nkama mai.
31 Bantu bonso bakabalabo ba mu tshitudilu tshia Dana bakadi binunu lukama ne makumi atanu ne muanda mutekete ne nkama isambombo. Bobo nebalonde ku nyima kua bonso ku mabendele abo.
32 Aba mbantu bakabalabo ba Bena Isalele ku nsubu ya batatu abo, bungi bua bantu bakabalabo ba mu bitudilu biabo ku bisumbu biabo buakadi binunu nkama isambombo ne bisatu ne nkama itanu ne makumi atanu.
33 Kadi kabakabala Bena Lewi munkatshi mua Bena Isalele, bu muakambila Yehowa Mose.
34 Bena Isalele bakenza nunku, mu malu onso akambila Yehowa Mose, nunku bakashikama ku mabendele abo, ne bakaya ku mpala ku meku abo ne ku nsubu ya batatuʼabo.

Numbers 3
1 Ebi mbikondo bia Alona ne Mose mu dituku diakasomba Yehowa ne Mose mu mukuna wa Sinai.
2 Au mmena a bana ba balume ba Alona Nadaba muanʼa bute ne Abihu ne Eleazâ ne Itamâ.
3 Au mmena a bana ba balume ba Alona bakuidi bakadi ne manyi mela ha mitu, bakajidilabo bua kuenza mudimu wa buakuidi.
4 Nadaba ne Abihu bakafua ku mpala kua Yehowa, hakaluabo ne kahia kakadi kakayi kajidila ku mpala kua Yehowa, mu tshihela tshia Sinai, bobo kabakadi ne bana, ne Eleazâ ne Itamâ bakenza mudimu wa buakuidi ku mesu kua Alona tatuʼabo.
5 Yehowa wakambila Mose ne,
6 Ulue biebe ne tshisamba tshia Lewi, ubateke ku mpala kua Alona muakuidi, bua bobo bamuenzele mudimu.
7 Bobo nebalame bintu biandi ne bintu bia bantu bonso ku mpala kua ntenta wa kusambakena, bua kuenzelabo mudimu wa mu nsubu wa tshitendelelu.
8 Bobo nebalame bintu bia mu nsubu wa tshitendelelu, ne bintu bia Bena Isalele, bua kuenzelabo mudimu wa mu ntenta wa kusambakena.
9 Wewe neufue Bena Lewi kudi Alona ne kudi bana bandi balume bobo badi bafidibue kudi Alona, bua kukuatshishabo Bena Isalele.
10 Wewe neusungule Alona ne bana bandi balume, ne bobo nebashale ne mudimu wa buakuidi, bialua muenyi ha buihi nebamushihe.
11 Yehowa wakambila Mose ne,
12 Mona, meme nakudiangatshila Bena Lewi munkatshi mua Bena Isalele mu miaba ya banʼa bute bonso bakalelabo munkatshi mua Bena Isalele, Bena Lewi nebikale banyi:
13 bualu bua banʼa bute bonso mbanyi, mu dituku dinakashiha banʼa bute bonso mu buloba bua Ejipitu, nakadijidila banʼa bute bonso ba mu Isalele, ne ba bantu ne ba nyama, bobo mbanyi, meme ndi Yehowa.
14 Yehowa wakambila Mose mu tshihela tshia Sinai ne,
15 Ubale bana ba Lewi ku nsubu ya batatuʼabo ne ku meku abo ubale bana ba balume bonso ba mu ngondo umue tô ne ha mutu.
16 Mose wakababala bu mu dî dia Yehowa.
17 Aba mbana ba balume ba Lewi ku mena abo Gêshona ne Kohata ne Melali.
18 Au mmena a bana ba balume ba Gêshona ku meku abo Libini ne Shimei.
19 Bana ba balume ba Kohata ku meku abo Amalama ne Yisehâ ne Hebelone ne Uziele
20 Bana ba balume ba Melali ku meku abo Mali ne Mushi. Au mmeku a Bena Lewi ku nsubu ya batatuʼabo.
21 Mu Gêshona muakadi diku dia Bena Libini ne diku dia Bena Shimei, au mmeku a bena Gêshona.
22 Bantu bakabalabo, bana balume ba mu ngondo umue tô ne ha mutu, bakadi binunu muanda mutekete ne nkama itanu.
23 Meku a Bena Gêshona nebashikame ku nyima kua nsubu wa tshitendelelu ku wesete.
24 Eleyasafa muana mulume wa Laele neikale mukelenge wa nsubu wa batatu ba Bena Gêshona.
25 Bintu bia mu nsubu wa tshitendelelu bidi bana balume ba Gêshona balama nebikale ntenta, ne tshilulu tshidibo bateka ha mutu, ne bilulu bia ku bibi bia ntenta wa kusambakena,
26 ne bilulu bia luhangu ne bilulu bidibo bateka ku mbelu kua luhangu, hehi ne nsubu wa tshitendelelu, ne hehi ne tshioshelu, ne nshinga ya ku luhangu bua mudimu wa tshitendelelu.
27 Mu Kohata muakadi diku dia Bena Amalama ne dia Bena Yisehâ ne dia Bena Hebelone ne dia Bena Uziele au mmeku a Bena Kohata.
28 Bungi bua bana ba balume ba mu ngondo umue tô ne ha mutu buakadi binunu muanda mukulu ne nkama isambombo, bakadi balama bintu bia nsubu wa tshitendelelu.
29 Bantu ba mu meku a bana balume ba Kohata nebashikame ku luseke lua ku sude.
30 Elisafana muana wa balume wa Uziele neikale mukelenge wa nsubu wa batatu ba Bena Kohata.
31 Bobo nebalame mushete wa Nzambi ne mesa ne minda ne bioshelu ne malonga adibo benza nʼau mudimu mu nsubu wa tshitendelelu, ne tshilulu tshidibo bateka ku mpala kua muaba wa tshijila.
32 Eleazâ muana mulume wa Alona, muakuidi, neikale ku mutu kua bakelenge ba Bena Lewi, ne ku mutu kua bantu badi balama bintu bia mu nsubu wa tshitendelelu.
33 Mu Melali muakadi diku dia Bena Mali ne dia Bena Mushi au mmeku a Melali.
34 Bungi bua balume ba mu ngondo umue tô ne ha mutu buakadi binunu bisambombo ne nkama ibidi.
35 Suliele, muana mulume wa Abihayila, neikale mukelenge wa nsubu wa batatu ba meku a Melali bobo nebashikame ku luseke lua nsubu wa tshitendelelu lua ku nôde.
36 Bintu bidi bana balume ba Melali balama bidi mabaya a nsubu wa tshitendelelu, ne mitshi ne makunshi ne nkanu ne bintu biabi bionso;
37 ne makunshi a ku luhangu ne nkanu yai ne mitanda yai ne nshinga yai.
38 Mose ne Alona ne bana bandi balume nebashikame ku mpala kua ntenta wa kusambakena ku luseke lua ku esete kudi diba dihatuka, bua kulama nsubu wa tshitendelelu ne bintu bia Bena Isalele; bialua muenyi ha buihi nebamushihe.
39 Bungi bua Bena Lewi bakabalabo kudi Mose ne Alona bu muakambila Yehowa, ku meku abo, balume bonso ba mu ngondo umue tô ne ha mutu, buakadi binunu makumi abidi ne bibidi.
40 Yehowa wakambila Mose ne, Ubale banʼa bute balume bonso ba Bena Isalele ba mu ngondo umue to ne ha mutu, funda bungi bua mena abo.
41 Wewe newangatshile Bena Lewi (meme ndi Yehowa) mu miaba ya banʼa bute ba munkatshi mua Bena Isalele, ne bimuna bia Bena Lewi mu miaba ya banʼa bute ba bimuna bia Bena Isalele.
42 Mose wakabala banʼa bute bonso ba munkatshi mua Bena Isalele, bu muakamuambila Yehowa.
43 Bungi bua banʼa bute balume ba mu ngondo umue tô ne ha mutu buakabalabo buakadi binunu makumi abidi ne bibidi ne nkama ibidi ne makumi muanda mutekete ne basatu.
44 Yehowa wakambila Mose ne,
45 Angata Bena Lewi mu miaba ya banʼa bute ba munkatshi mua Bena Isalele, ne bimuna bia Bena Lewi mu miaba ya bimuna biabo, Bena Lewi nebikale banyi meme ndi Yehowa
46 Bua kuhikula banʼa bute ba Bena Isalele, nkama ibidi ne makumi muanda mutekete ne basatu, badi bahita balume ba Bena Lewi bungi,
47 newangate nshekele
48 wewe neufue nshekele ya bungi buabo buakahikulabo, kudi Alona ne kudi bana bandi balume.
49 Mose wakangata argent ya buhikudi bua bantu bakahita Bena Lewi bungi;
50 yeye wakangata nshekele kudi banʼa bute ba Bena Isalele bu tshinunu tshimue ne nkama isatu ne makumi asambombo ne itanu, bu nshekele udibo bafile mu nsubu wa tshitendelelu.
51 Mose wakafua argent ya buhikudi buabo kudi Alona ne bana bandi balume bu muakadi dî dia Yehowa, bu muakambila Yehowa Mose.

Numbers 4
1 Yehowa wakambila Mose ne Alona ne,
2 Balai bungi bua bana balume ba Kohata ba munkatshi mua bana balume ba Lewi ku meku abo ne ku nsubu ya batatuʼabo,
3 mu bidimu makumi asatu to ne ku bidimu makumi atanu buonso buabo badi bakuata mudimu wa mu ntenta wa kusambakena.
4 Eu udi mudimu wa bana balume ba Kohata udibo benza ne bintu bia tshijila mu ntenta wa kusambakena,
5 hayabo ku luendu Alona neabuelemu ne bana bandi balume nebatuhe tshilulu tshikudika, nebabituale hatshi mushete wa Nzambi,
6 nebâteke tshibuikilu tshia biseba bia nkonge ha mutu hatshi, nebâteke tshilulu tshifike ha mutu hatshi kabidi, nebabueshemu mitshi ya mushete.
7 Nebateke tshilulu tshia bleu ha mutu ha mesa a mampa, nebâteke ha mutu hatshi malonga ne nkutu ne mpanza ne makoho adibo balongolola nʼau, ne mampa neikalehu,
8 nebatekehu tshilulu tshikunze, nebâteke tshibuikidi tshia biseba bia nkonge ha mutu hatshi, nebabueshemu mitshi ya mesa.
9 Nebangate tshilulu tshia bleu, nebabuikile hatshi bitekelu bia minda ne minda, ne bintu bidibo bajima habi minda, ne tuhanza tua mishila ya kahia, ne bihanza bia manyi adibo bakuatshisha nʼau minda
10 nebateke minda ne malonga ne bintu ebi bionso mu tshibuikidi tshia biseba bia nkonge, nebabiteke ha mitshi ibidi idi mikuatakasha.
11 Nebateke tshilulu tshia bleu ha mutu ha tshioshelu tshia or, nebâteke ha mutu hatshi tshibuikidi tshia biseba bia nkonge, nebabueshemu mitshi ya tshioshelu
12 nebangate malonga onso adibo benza nʼau mudimu wa mu nsubu wa tshitendelelu nebâteke mu tshilulu tshia bleu, nebâbuikde ne tshibuikidi tshia biseba bia nkonge, nebâteke ha mitshi ibidi idi mikuatakasha
13 Nebumushe butue ku tshioshelu, nebatekehu tshilulu tshikunzubile
14 nebâteke ha mutu hatshi bintu bionso bidibo benza nʼabi mudimu watshi ne malonga a kahia ne nkobo ya munyinyi, ne nkasu, ne bihanza ne malonga onso a tshioshelu, nebâteke tshibuikilu tshia biseba bia nkonge, nebabueshemu mitshi yatshi.
15 Kushikisha Alona ne bana bandi balume kubuikila nsubu wa tshitendelelu ne bintu bionso bia mu nsubu, hayabo mu nshila, hashishe bana balume ba Kohata nebalue bua kubituala, kadi bobo kabalengi bintu bia tshijila bianza, bua kabafu. Bintu ebi mmajitu a bana balume ba Kohata mu nsubu wa tshitendelelu.
16 Bintu bidi nʼabi Eleazâ, muana mulume wa Alona, muakuidi, nebikale manyi a minda ne musenga wa manananshi, ne mulambu wa bukula, ne manyi adibo bela ha mutuyeye udi mulami wa nsubu wa tshitendelelu ne bintu bionso bidimu, ne wa muaba wa tshijila ne bintu bionso bidimu.
17 Yehowa wakambila Mose ne Alona ne,
18 Kanumushi tshisamba tshia meku a Bena Kohata munkatshi mua Bena Lewi,
19 nubenzele nunku, bua bikale ne muoyo, kabafu, haluabo ha buihi ne bintu bia tshijila Alona ne bana bandi balume nebabuelemu, nebaleshe muntu ne muntu mudimu wandi ne bujitu buandi,
20 kababuedi bua kutangila bintu bia tshijila nansha kakese, bua kabafu.
21 Yehowa wakambila Mose ne,
22 Ubale bungi bua bana balume ba Gêshona kabidi ku nsubu ya batatuʼabo ne ku meku abo;

41 Meme nda Yehowa, Nzambi wenu, wakanuhatula mu buloba bua Ejipitu, bua kuikala Nzambi wenumeme ndi Yehowa, Nzambi wenu.

Numbers 16
1 Kola, muana mulume wa Yiseha, muana mulume wa Kohata, muana mulume wa Lewi, ne Datana ne Abilama, bana balume ba Eliaba, ne Ono, muana mulume wa Pelete, bana balume ba Lubena,
2 bakabika ne Bena Isalele bakuabo ne bakelenge ba mu tshisumbu bu nkama ibidi ne makumi atanu, bantu bakadi ne lumu lunene,
3 bakadisangisha bua kufuilangana ne Mose ne Alona, bakabambila ne, Nudi nutamba kudienzela malu, bualu bua bantu bonso mbantu ba tshijila ne Yehowa udi munkatshi muabo nuenu nudi nudibandisha ku mutu kua bantu ba Yehowa munyi?
4 Hakunvua Mose dî edi, yeye wa kakuma mpala hanshi,
5 wakambila Kola ne bakadiye habo ne, Dinda Yehowa neadileshe bantu bandi, ne nganyi udi wa tshijila, neamubikile ku mpala kuandi, neabifile wasunguleye ku mpala kuandi.
6 Nuenze bienu nunku Kola ne bantu hadiye habo bangate malonga a kahia,
7 batekemu kahia, batekehu musenga wa manananshi ku mpala kua Yehowa makelela, ne muntu udi musungudibue kudi Yehowa neikale muntu wa tshijila, nuenu bana balume ba Lewi nudi nutamba kudienzela malu.
8 Mose wakambila Kola ne, Unvuai, bana balume ba Lewi:
9 nudi nuelangana meji ne bualu ebu budi bukese bua Nzambi wa Isalele wakamujidila munkatshi mua Bena Isalele, bua nuenu nulue ha buihi nʼandi kumukuatshia mudimu mu nsubu wa Yehowa, ne bua kukuatshila tshisumbu tshonso mudimu kabidi,
10 yeye wakanuhatula ne bana benu bonso ba Lewi ha buihi nʼandi, nuenu nudi nudikebela mudimu wa buakuidi kabidi, anyi?
11 Wewe ne bantu baudi nʼabo nuakudisangisha bua kuelangana ne Yehowa! Alona udi nganyi bua nuenu nudi numutontolola?
12 Mose wakabikila Datana ne Abilama, bana balume ba Eliaba, ne bobo bakamba ne, Katuena tulua,
13 bualu budi bukese bua wewe wakumuka nʼetu mu buloba budi bûle tente ne mabele ne buitshi bua kubishiha mu tshihela, udi musue kudibandisha bu mukelenge wetu kabidi, anyi?
14 Bualu bukuabo, wewe kuena muanze kulua nʼetu mu buloba budi bûle tente ne mabele ne buitshi, kuena mutuhe buhianyi bua madimi ne madimi a tumuma, udi musue kuvuisha bantu aba bafofo, anyi? Katuena tulua.
15 Mose wakadi ne tshiji tshikole ne yeye wakambila Yehowa ne, Kuitabusha milambu yabo nansha, meme tshiakunyenga umue wabo nyama wandi wa mpunda, tshiakuenzela umue wabo bibi.
16 Mose wakambila Kola ne, Wewe ne bantu bonso baudi habo nuikale ku mpala kua Yehowa ne kua Alona makelela:
17 mundi yonso angate dilonga diandi dia kahia, atekehu musenga wa manananshi, bantu bonso balue ne malonga nkama ibidi ne makumi atanu ku mpala kua Yehowa, ne wewe ne Alona, mundi ne dilonga diandi mundi ne dilonga diandi.
18 Muntu yonso wakangata dilonga diandi, wakatekamu kahia, wakatekahu musenga wa manananshi, bakimana ne Mose ne Alona ku mbelu kua nsubu wa tshitendelelu.
19 Kola wakabikila tshisumbu tshonso tshia batomboki ku mbelu kua nsubu wa tshitendelelu, ne butumbi bua Yehowa buakamueneka kudi tshisumbu tshonso.
20 Yehowa wakambila Mose ne Alona ne,
21 Umukai bienu munkatshi mua tshisumbu etshi, nembashihe mu tshituha tshikese.
22 Bobo bakadikuma hanshi, bakamba ne, Nzambi, Nzambi wa mioyo ya bantu, wewe newikale ne tshisumbu tshonso tshiji bua bubi bua mundi umue, anyi?
23 Yehowa wakambila Mose ne,
24 Wambile tshisumbu tshonso ne, Umukai ku nsubu wa tshitendelelu wa Kola ne Datana ne Abilama.
25 Mose wakabika, wakaya kudi Datana ne Abilama, ne bakulu ba mu Isalele bakamulonda ku nyima.
26 Yeye wakambila tshisumbu tshia bantu ne, Umukai, ndi nnusengelela, ku ntenta ya bantu aba babi, kanulengi tshintu tshiabo tshianza, kabanushihi bua mibi yabo.
27 Nunku bakumuka ku nsubu wa tshitendelelu wa Kola ne Datana ne Abalama ku nseke yonso: ne Datana ne Abilama bakaluhuka, bakimana ku mbelu kua ntenta yabo ne bakashi babo ne bana babo balume ne bana babo bakese.
28 Mose wakamba ne, Nenumanye ne Yehowa wakatuma bua kuenza malu au onso, tshiakadienzela bualu bua mutshima wanyi.
29 Biafila balume aba bu mudi bantu bonso bafue, anyi biabakuata bualu bu mudibu bukuata bantu bonso, nunku Yehowa kena muntume.
30 Kadi bikala Yehowa wenza bualu buhia-buhia, ne buloba bubulula mukana muabu bubamina ne bantu bonso hadibo nʼabo, ne bobo baya ku Muaba wa Bafue hatshidibo ne muoyo, nunku nenumanye ne bantu aba bakalengulula Yehowa.
31 Hakashikisheye kubambila mei a onso, buloba buakahandika muinshi mua makasa abo,
32 buloba buakubulula mukana muabu, buakabamina ne bantu bonso ba mu nsubu yabo ne bantu bonso bakalamata Kola ne bintu biabo bionso.
33 Nunku bobo ne bantu bonso bakalamata kudibo bakaya mu Muaba wa Bafue hakadibo ne muoyo, ne buloba buakabuikila mukana muabu ku mutu kuabo, ne bobo biakajimina munkatshi mua tshisumbu.
34 Bena Isalele bonso bakadi ha buihi habo bakanyema hakunvuabo muadi wabo, bobo bakamba ne, Buloba kabutuminyi.
35 Kahia bakahatuka kudi Yehowa, kakosha balume nkama ibidi ne makumi atanu bakadi bafila musenga wa manananshi.
36 Yehowa wakambila Mose ne,
37 Wambile Eleazâ, muana mulume wa Alona muakuidi, bua yeye angate malonga munkatshi mua kahia, ne wewe utangalashe kahia kuaka ebi mbintu bia tshijila,
38 malonga a bantu aba babi bakadijinushila mioyo yabo, wenze nʼau tshibuikilu tshia tshioshelu bobo bakâfila ku mpala kua Yehowa, nunku adi bintu bia tshijila, neikale bu tshivulukidi kudi Bena Isalele.
39 Eleazâ muakuidi wakangata malonga a tshiamu tshia mitaku akatuabo, bakâtula bu tshibuikilu tshia tshioshelu,
40 bua kuikalau tshivulukidi kudi Bena Isalele ne kubamanyisha ne, Muntu udi kayi muana wa Alona, kalu ha buihi bua kuosheye musenga wa manananshi ku mpala kua Yehowa, kenji bu Kola ne bantu bandi, bu muakambila Yehowa Mose.
41 Kadi hakatshiabu Bena Isalele bonso bakatontolola Mose ne Alona ne, Nuenu nuakushiha bantu ba Yehowa.
42 Hashishe bakadisangisha tshisumbu tshia bantu bua kuelangana ne Mose ne Alona, baka tangila ku ntenta wa kusambakena, ne monai, difutu diakamubuikila ne butumbi bua Yehowa buakamueneka,
43 Mose ne Alona bakatua ku mbelu kua ntenta wa kusambakena.
44 Yehowa wakambila Mose ne,
45 Umuka biebe munkatshi mua tshisumbu etshi tshia bantu, nenubashihe mu tshituha tshihi. Bobo bakahaula mpala yabo hanshi.
46 Mose wakambila Alona ne, Angata dilonga dia kahia, tekahu kahia ka ku tshioshelu, tekahu musenga wa manananshi, uye biebe lubilu kudi tshisumbu tshia bantu, bua kubabuikidila bubi buabo, bualu bua tshiji tshiakumana kuhatuka kudi Yehowa, disama dikole diakumana kubanga.
47 Alona wakangata dilonga diandi bu muakamba Mose, wakaya lubilu munkatshi mua tshisumbu tshia bantu, ne monai, disama dikole diakamana kubanga munkatshi mua bantu, yeye wakatekahu musenga wa manananshi bua kubuikidila bantu bubi buabo.
48 Wakimana biandi hankatshi ha bantu bafue ne bantu bakatshidi ne muoyo, ne disama diakashika.
49 Bungi bua bantu bakafua ku disama edi bakadi binunu dikumi ne binai ne nkama muanda mutekete, ha mutu ha bantu bakafua bua bualu bua Kola.
50 Alona wakahingana kudi Mose ku mbelu kua ntenta wa kusambakena, disama diakashika.

Numbers 17
1 Yehowa wakambila Mose ne,
2 Ela dî kudi bana ba Isalele, usungule bantu dikumi ne babidi badi bakelenge ba nsubu ya batatuʼabo, wewe wangata milangala kudibo, muntu ne mulangala wandi muntu ne mulangala wandi, wewe ufunde dina dia muntu ne muntu ha mulangala wandi.
3 Wewe neufunde dina dia Alona ha mulangala wa tshisamba tshia Lewi, bualu bua wewe neusungule mulangala umue kudi muntu ne muntu udi ku mutu kua nsubu ya batatuʼabo.
4 Wewe neuyiteke mu ntenta wa kusambakena ku mpala kua mushete kundi ndimuenesha, muaba utudi tusangakana nʼebe.
5 Hashishe mulangala wa muntu unakusungula newele ntonga, meme nenshikishe kutontolola kua Bena Isalele kudibo bakutontolola.
6 Mose wakamanyisha Bena Isalele, bakelenge babo bakamuha milangala dikumi ne ibidi, mukelenge mulangala wandi mukelenge mulangala wandi, bu mu nsubu ya batatuʼabo, ne mulangala wa Alona wakadi munkatshi mua milangala yabo.
7 Mose wakateka milangala ku mpala kua Yehowa mu ntenta wa kusambakena.
8 Hakatshiabu Mose wakabuela mu ntenta wa kusambakena, ne monai, mulangala wa Alona wa mu nsubu wa Lewi wakadi muele ntonga ne bilongo, wakakuama tumuma tukese tua amandes.
9 Mose wakaluhuka ku mpala kua Yehowa ne milangala yonso kudi Bena Isalele bobo bakatangila ne muntu yonso wakangata mulangala wandi.
10 Yehowa wakambila Mose ne, Hingasha mulangala wa Alona ku mpala kua mushete, bu tshivulukidi kudi bantu badi basue kutomboka, neumanye mua kushikisha kutontolola kudibo bantontolola nʼaku bua bobo kabafu nansha.
11 Mose wakenza nunku bu muakamuambila Yehowa, mbu muakenzeye.
12 Bena Isalele bakambila Mose ne, Mona! tudi bafue, ditu tuahanga, ditu tuahanga bonso.
13 Muntu yonso udi ulua ha buihi ne nsubu wa Yehowa utu wadia: buonso buetu netulue, anyi?

Numbers 18
1 Yehowa wakambila Alona ne, Wewe ne bana bebe balume ne ba mu nsubu wa ba tatuʼebe nenutuale mahambuka a ku buakane a bantu mu nsubu wa tshitendelelu, wewe ne bana bebe balume nenutuale mahambuka a mudimu wenu wa buakuidi.
2 Bana benu kabidi, tshisamba tshia Lewi, tshisamba tshia tatuʼebe, ulue nʼabo bua bobo bakukuatshile mudimu kadi wewe ne bana bebe balume badi nʼebe nenuikale ku mpala kua ntenta wa kusambakena.
3 Bobo nebalame bintu biebe ne bintu bia mu Ntenta: kadi bobo kabalu ha buihi ne malonga a mu nsubu anyi tshioshelu, bua bobo kabafu ne nuenu kanufu kabidi.
4 Bobo nebakukuatshishe bua bintu bia mu ntenta wa kusambakena, ne mu mudimu wonso wa Ntenta muenyi kalu ha buihi nʼenu.
5 Nuenu nenulame tshitendelelu ne tshioshelu, nunku mulau mukuabo kaukuatshi Bena Isalele.
6 Mona! Meme nakadiangatshila bana benu, Bena Lewi, munkatshi mua Bena Isalele badi bu kuha kuebe kuwakuha Yehowa bua kuenza mudimu wa mu ntenta wa kusambakena.
7 Wewe ne bana bebe balume badi nʼebe nenuikale ne mudimu wa buakuidi bua bintu bionso bia tshioshelu ne bintu bidi ku nyima kua tshilulu tshikudika, nuenu nenuenze mudimu eu, nakunuha mudimu wa buakuidi bu mudimu wa kuha kuenu, muenyi udi ulua ha buihi, nebamushihe.
8 Yehowa wakambila Alona ne, Mona, meme nakakuteka ku mutu kua milambu yanyi idibo banyukula ne bintu bionso bidi Bena Isalele bajila, nakakuha bintu ebi bu tshituha tshia bintu biebe bualu bua kujidila kuebe ne kua bana bebe balume tshiendelele.
9 Nebikale bintu biebe munkatshi mua bintu biakajidilabo ku kahia milambu yabo yonso, ne ya bukula, ne ya kubuikila nʼai bubi buabo, ne ya kubuikila nʼai kusambuka kuabo kua mikenji, milambu idibo bafila kundi neyikale bu tshintu tshia tshijila kûdi ne kudi bana bebe balume.
10 Wewe neudie bintu ebi bia tshijila, muntu mulume yonso neadie bintu ebi ebi mbintu biebe bia tshijila.
11 Ebi mbintu biebe kabidi: bintu bidi Bena Isalele banyukula, ne milambu yonso idibo bakuha, nakunuha ne bana bebe balume ne bana bebe bakashi badi nʼebe, bu tshintu tshienu tshiendelele muntu yonso udi muakane mu nsubu webe neabidie.
12 Bintu bimpe bia manyi ne bia tumuma tukese ne bia manvua
13 Mamuma mimpe a mu buloba buabo buonso adibo bahuola diambedi, adibo halua nʼau kudi Yehowa, neikale mamuma ebe, muntu yonso wa mu nsubu webe udi muakane neadie.
14 Tshintu tshionso tshidibo bajila mu Isalele netshikale tshiebe.
15 Bintu bionso bidibo balela, ne bia bantu ne bia nyama idibo bafila kudi Yehowa, nebikale biebe, kadi wewe neuhikule banʼa bute ba bantu ne banʼa bute bonso ba nyama idi kayiyi miakane.
16 Bintu bidi ne bualu bua kuhikudibua bia ngondo umue, wewe neubihikule bu mudi mushinga wabi, nshekele
17 Kadi banʼa bute ba ngombe ne ba mikoko ne ba mbushi, wewe kuena ubihikula, ebi mbintu bia tshijila wewe neumiamine mashi abi ha tshioshelu, newoshe dianyi diabi bu mulambu wa kuosha ha kahia, bu tshintu tshidi tshitua dihembu kudi Yehowa.
18 Munyinyi wabi newikale webe bu mudi tshiadi tshidibo banyukula ne tshibelu tshia ku tshianza tshia bukole.
19 Milambu yonso ya bintu bia tshijila bidibo banyukula bidi Bena Isalele bafila kudi Yehowa, nakunuhabi ne bana bebe balume ne bana bebe bakashi, bu tshintu tshienu tshiendelele: etshi ntshihungidi tshia luehu tshia tshiendelele ku mesu kua Yehowa kûdi wewe ne kudi bana bebe badi nʼebe.
20 Yehowa wakambila Alona ne, Wewe kuena ne buhianyi mu buloba buabo, wewe kuena ne tshituha munkatshi muabo meme ndi tshituha tshiebe ne buhianyi buebe munkatshi mua Bena Isalele.
21 Mona, nakuha Bena Lewi tshintu tshia dikumi tshionso tshia mu Isalele bu buhianyi buabo bua mudimu udibo benza mu ntenta wa kusambakena.
22 Bena Isalele kabalu ha buihi ne ntenta wa kusambakena bua kabadituadidi bubi buabo, kabafu.
23 Kadi Bena Lewi nebenze mudimu wa mu ntenta wa kusambakena, bobo nebadituadile bubi buabo eu udi mukenji wa tshiendelele ku bikondo bienu bionso, bobo kabena ne buhianyi munkatshi mua Bena Isalele.
24 Bualu bua nakuha Bena Lewi tshintu tshia dikumi tshia Bena Isalele tshidibo banyukula kudi Yehowa, bu buhianyi buabo, bua bualu ebu nakubambila ne, Kabena ne buhianyi munkatshi mua Bena Isalele.
25 Yehowa wakambila Mose ne,
26 Wambile Bena Lewi ne, Hanuangata tshintu tshia dikumi tshia bintu bia Bena Isalele tshinakunuha bu buhianyi buenu, nenutshinyukule bu mulambu udibo banyukula kudi Yehowa, tshintu tshia dikumi tshia tshintu tshia dikumi tshiakanuhabo.
27 Nebanubadile mulambu udibo banyukula bu bintu biakushihulabo ku tshishihuidi ne bu bintu biakukamabo ha tshikaminu.
28 Nunku nuenu nenufile mulambu wa bia dikumi bionso binudi nuangata kudi Bena Isalele bu mulambu udibo banyukula kudi Yehowa, wewe neuhe Alona muakuidi mulambu eu wa bintu bidibo banyukula kudi Yehowa.
29 Nenufile tshituha tshia bintu bienu bionso bu mulambu udibo banyukula kudi Yehowa nenufile bintu bidi bitamba buimpe, bintu bia tshijila mene.
30 Nunku wewe mubambile ne, Hanuanyukula bintu bidi bitamba buimpe, bintu ebi nebikale bibadibue kudi Bena Lewi bu bintu biakuvulangana ku tshishihuidi ne ha tshikaminu.
31 Nenudie bintu ebi mu miaba yonso, ne nuenu ne ba mu nsubu yenu edi ndifutu dienu diakanufutabo bua mudimu wenu mu ntenta wa kusambakena.
32 Bua bualu ebu kanuena nudituadila bubi, hanuanyukula bintu bidi bitamba buimpe kanuena nuona bintu bia tshijila bia Bena Isalele, bua nuenu kanufu.

Numbers 19
1 Yehowa wakambila Mose ne Alona ne,
2 Eu udi mukenji wakuela Yehowa ne, Nuambile Bena Isalele bua bobo balue kûdi ne muana wa ngombe mukunze, udi kayi ne ditoba anyi tshilema, udi kayi muanze kuya muinshi mua mutshi wa tshikokedi.
3 Nenumuhe Eleazâ muakuidi, yeye nealue nʼandi ku butshika bua tshitudilu, nebamushihe ku mesu kuandi,
4 ne Eleazâ muakuidi neangate mashi andi ku munu wandi, neamiamine mashi ku luseke lua tshibuelelu tshia ntenta wa kusambakena misangu muanda mutekete.
5 Umue wabo neoshe muana wa ngombe ku mesu kuandi, ne tshiseba tshiandi ne munyinyi wandi ne mashi andi ne tunvi tuandi:
6 ne muakuidi neangate mitshi ya cèdre, ne mabeshi a hysope, ne bintu bikunzubile, neabiele munkatshi mua kahia kadi kosha muana wa ngombe.
7 Hashishe muakuidi neuvuwe bilulu ne mubidi wandi ne mai, ne ku nyima nealue biandi mu tshitudilu, neikale bu muntu udi kayi muakane tô ne ku dilolo.
8 Muntu wakanuisha ne kahia neuvuwe bilulu biandi ne mubidi wandi ne mai, ne yeye neikale bu muntu udi kayi muakane tô ne ku dilolo.
9 Muntu udi muakane neasangishe butue buonso bua muana wa ngombe, neabuteke ku butshika bua tshitudilu ha muaba mutoke, nebabulamine tshisumbu tshia Bena Isalele bu mai a kulengesha hau bubi eu udi bu mulambu wa kubuikila nʼau bubi.
10 Muntu udi usangisha butue bua muana wa ngombe neuvuwe bilulu biandi, neikale bu muntu udi kayi muakane to ne ku dilolo eu udi mukenji wa Bena Isalele ne wa muenyi udi munkatshi muabo tô ne tshiendelele.
11 Muntu udi ulenga bianza ha mubidi wa muntu mufue neikale bu muntu udi kayi muakane tô matuku muanda mutekete,
12 yeye neadilengeshe ne mai au dituku disatu, ne halua dituku dia muanda mutekete yeye neikale muakane, kadi biahidieye kudilengesha dituku disatu, halua dituku dia muanda mutekete yeye kena wikala muakane.
13 Muntu yonso udi ulenga muntu mufue bianza, mubidi wa muntu mumane kufua, ne kena udilengesha, yeye udi wona nsubu wa Yehowa, nebamumushe munkatshi mua Bena Isalele, bualu bua kabakamumiamina mai a kumulengesha nʼau, yeye kena wikala muakane, ne bubi buandi butshidi hamʼbidi handi.
14 Eu udi mukenji wa hafuila mundi mu ntenta mundi yonso udi ubuela mu ntenta au, ne mundi yonso wakadi mu ntenta au neikale bu mundi udi kayi muakane tô matuku muanda mutekete.
15 Malonga onso a mu nsubu, adi kayi ne bibuikidi, neikale bu tshintu tshidi katshiyi tshiakane.
16 Muntu yonso wa mu budimi udi ulenga mundi wakafua ku muele wa nvita bianza, anyi mubidi wa mundi mufue, anyi mufuba wa mundi, anyi lukita, neikale bu mundi udi kayi muakane tô matuku muanda mutekete.
17 Muntu udi kayi muakane neangate butue bua mulambu wakoshabo bua kubuikila nʼau bubi, neangate mai adi ahueka mu dilonga:
18 mundi utshidi muakane neangate mabeshi a hysope, neâtue mu mai, neamiamine ha ntenta, ne ha malonga onso, ne ha bantu bonso bakadimu, ne mundi wakalenga mifuba tshianza, anyi mundi wakafua ku muele wa nvita, anyi mundi mufue, anyi lukita,
19 mundi utshidi muakane neamiamine mai ha mundi udi kayi muakane dî diku disatu ne dituku dia muanda mutekete, ne halua dituku dia muanda mutekete neamulengeshe, yeye neuvuwe bilulu biandi ne mubidi wandi ne mai, neikale muakane ku dilolo.
20 Kadi mundi udi kayi muakane ne kena udilengesha, nebamumushe munkatshi mua tshisumbu, bualu bua yeye wakuona nsubu wa tshitendelelu, kabakamumiamina mai a kumulengesha nʼau, yeye kena muakane.
21 Eu newikale mukenji wa tshiendelele kudibo muntu udi umiamine mai a kubalengesha nʼau, neuvuwe bilulu biandi, muntu udi ulenga mai a kubalengesha nʼau tshianza neikale bu muntu udi kayi muakane tô ne ku dilolo.
22 Bintu bionso bidi muntu udi kayi muakane ulenga bianza nebikale bu bintu bidi kabiyi biakane, ne muntu udi ubilenga tshianza neikale bu muntu udi kayi muakane tô ne ku dilolo.

Numbers 20
1 Bena Isalele bonso bakalua mu tshihela tshia Sina mu ngondo wa kumudilu, bakashikama mu Kadesha, Mîyama wakafua, bakamujikamu.
2 Bantu kabakadi ne mai a kunua, bakadisangisha kudi Mose ne kudi Alona.
3 Bantu bakatandangana ne Mose, bakamba ne, Biakadi bimpe bua tuetu kufua bu muakafua bana betu ku mesu kua Yehowa!
4 Nuakulua ne bantu ba Yehowa mu tshihela etshi bualu bua kubishiha ne nyama yetu munyi?
5 Bualu kai nuakatuhatula mu Ejipitu bua kulua nʼetu mu muaba eu mubi? Kimoko kakuena bintu bia kukuna nansha mamuma a nfigi nansha tumuma tukese nansha mamuma a grenade, kakuena mai a kunua.
6 Mose ne Alona bakumuka ku mesu kua bantu, bakaya ku mbelu kua ntenta wa kusambakena, bakatua mpala yabo hanshi. Butumbi bua Yehowa buakamueneka kudibo.
7 Yehowa wakambila Mose ne,
8 Angata mulangala, usangishe bantu bonso ne wewe ne Alona mukuluʼebe, wewe wambile dibue didi ku mesu kuabo, dihatule mai, wewe neubahatuile mai ku dibue, wewe neubanuishe mai ne bimuna biabo.
9 Mose wakangata mulangala ku mpala kua Yehowa bu muakamuambileye.

Numbers 35
1 Yehowa wakambila Mose mu mpata ya Moaba hehi ne Yadene mitangilangane ne Yeleko ne,
2 Wele mukenji kudi Bena Isalele, bua bobo bahe Bena Lewi misoko ya kushikamamu bu buhianyi buabo, bobo bahe Bena Lewi kabidi tshituha tshia buloba budi bunyunguluka misoko ei.
3 Bobo nebashikame mu misoko ei, ne bimuna ne bintu biabo ne nyama yabo mikuabo nebikale mu buloba budi bunyunguluka misoko.
4 Tshituha tshia buloba bunudi nuha Bena Lewi netshikale ku lumbu lua musoko tô ne ku coudées
5 Ku butshika bua musoko nenuidikishe ku luseke lua ku esete coudées binunu bibidi, ne ku luseke lua ku Sude coudées binunu bibidi, ne ku luseke lua ku wesete coudées binunu bibidi, ne ku luseke lua ku nôde coudées binunu bibidi Buloba ebu bua ku matshika a misoko nebuikale buhianyi buabo.
6 Nenuhe Bena Lewi misoko ei isambombo ya kunyemena, biashala muntu muntu mukuabo, yeye neanyemenemu; ha mutu ha misoko ei nenubahe misoko mikuabo makumi anai ne ibidi.
7 Bungi bua misoko mudi nuha Bena Lewi neyikale makumi anai ne muanda mukulu, nenubahe misoko ei ne buloba budi buyinyunguluka.
8 Bualu bua misoko mudi nubaha munkatshi mua Isalele, bantu badi ne misoko ya bungi, nebabahe misoko ya bungi, ne badi ne misoko mikese nebabahe misoko mikese, nebahe Bena Lewi misoko bu bungi bua buhianyi buabo.
9 Yehowa wakambila Mose ne,
10 Wambile Bena Isalele ne, Hanuasabuka Yadene mu buloba bua Kanâna,
11 nenusungule misoko ya kunyemena, ne hashiha muntu muntu mukuabo ku mpukahuka, yeye neanyemenemu.
12 Misoko neyikale bu binyemenu kudi musombuedi, mushihi wa mundi kafu biandi tô ne himaneye ku mpala kua tshisumbu tshia bantu bua kumulumbuluishabo.
13 Nenusungule misoko ya kunyemena isambombo.
14 Nenusungule misoko isatu dishiya dia Yadene, ne isatu mu buloba bua Kanâna, neyikale misoko ya kunyemena.
15 Neyikale misoko ya kunyemenamu kudi Bena Isalele, ne kudi muenyi udi munkatshi muabo, hashiha muntu muntu mukuabo ku mpukahuka, neanyemenemu.
16 Bikaleye mumukume ne tshintu tshia tshiamu tshifike, ne biafileye, yeye udi mushihi wa muntu, nebashihe mushihi wa muntu bulelela.
17 Bikaleye mumukume ne dibue mu tshianza tshiandi, ne biafileye, yeye udi mushihi wa muntu, nebashihe mushihi wa muntu.
18 Anyi bikaleye mumukume ne mutshi mu tshianza, ne biafileye, yeye udi mushihi wa muntu, nebashihe mushihi wa muntu.
19 Musombuedi nkayandi neashihe mushihi wa muntu.
20 Bikaleye mumukume ne tshiji, anyi bikaleye mumuele tshintu, hakadiye muale mu nshila, wakamushiha,
21 anyi bikaleye mumukume ne tshianza tshiandi ne tshiji, mumushihe, nebashihe muntu wakamukuma, yeye udi mushihi wa muntu musombuedi neashihe mushihi wa muntu, hasangakeneye nʼandi.
22 Kadi bikaleye mumutahe kayi ne tshiji, anyi bikaleye muele tshintu, tshimukume, kayi mumualamine mu nshila,
23 anyi bikaleye mumukume ne dibue kayi mumutangile, ne mumushihe, kayi muikale nʼandi lukuna, kayi musue kunuenzela bibi,
24 nebalumbulule tshilumbu tshia mukuna wa muntu ne musombuedi, bu mudi mikenji ei yamba,
25 nebasungile mushihi mu tshianza tshia musombuedi, nebamuhingishe ku musoko wa kunyemena, kuakunyemeneye, ne yeye neashikamemu to ne hafua muakuidi munene, udi ne manyi a tshijila mela ha mutu.
26 Kadi bikala mushihi mubi tshile butshika bua musoko wakanyemeneye,
27 ne bikala musombuedi musangakene nʼandi ku butshika bua musoko wandi wa kunyemena, ne bikala musombuedi mushihe mushihi wa muntu, yeye kena ne bualu bubi,
28 bualu bua yeye wakadi ne bualu bua kuikala mu musoko wa kunyemena to ne bakafua muakuidi munene, kadi ku nyima kua lufu lua muakuidi munene mushihi udi mumanye mua kuhingana ku buloba bua buhianyi buandi.
29 Ei idi mikenji ya kuhumbulula hai malu ku bikondo bienu bionso, mu miaba yonso mudi nushikama,
30 muntu udi ushiha mukuabo, nebashihe mushiku bu mu dî dia bamonyi; kadi kabena bamanye mua kumushiha anu ku dî dia mumonyi umue.
31 Kanuangatshi bintu bia buhikudi kudi mushihi wa muntu udi muhile bulelela, bualu bua nebamushihe bulelela.
32 Kanuangatshi bintu bia buhikudi kudi muntu wakasemena ku musoko wa kunyemena, bualu bua kumuhingisha ku buloba buandi to ne hafua muakuidi munene.
33 Nunku kanuena nuona buloba bunudi nushikamamu, bualu bua mashi adi ona buloba, kabena bamanye mua kuhikula buloba hakamata mashi, anu ku mashi a muntu wakamatshisha mashi.
34 Kanuonyi buloba bunudi nushikamamu, kundi mushikame kabidi, bualu bua meme Yehowa ndi mushikame munkatshi mua Bena Isalele.

Numbers 36
1 Bakadi ku mutu kua nsubu ya batatuʼabo ba diku dia Bena Gilada, muana mulume wa Makî, muana mulume wa Manashe, wa meku a bana balume ba Yosefe, bakalua kudi Mose ne kudi bakelenge ba ku mutu kua nsubu ya batatuʼabo ba mu Isalele, bakamba ne,
2 Yehowa wakela dî kudi mukelenge wetu bua kutahulula buloba bua buhianyi ku nshobo, Yehowa wakela dî kudi mukelenge wetu kabidi bua kuha buhianyi bua Selofehada muanʼetu mulume kudi bana bandi bakashi.
3 Bikalabo babakibue kudi balume ba mu tshisamba tshikuabo tshia Bena Isalele, nunku nebanyeme buhianyi buabo ku buhianyi bua batatuʼetu, nebabusangishe ku buhianyi bua tshisamba tshiakayabo.
4 Nunku halua tshidimu tshia disanka dia Bena Isalele, buhianyi buabo nebusangishibue ku buhianyi bua tshisamba tshiakayabo, nunku buhianyi buabo nebumuke ku buhianyi bua batatuʼetu.
5 Mose wakambila Bena Isalele bu mu dî dia Yehowa ne, Ba mu tshisamba tshia bana balume ba Yosefe badi bamba dî dilelela.
6 Edi ndî diakamba Yehowa bualu bua bana bakashi ba Selofehada ne, Bobo babakibue kudi balume hadibo basue, kadi babakibue kudi balume ba mu meku a tshisamba tshia batatuʼabo.
7 Nunku buhianyi bua Bena Isalele kabuena bumuka ku tshisamba ne ku tshisamba, muntu ne muntu wa munkatshi mua Bena Isalele neashale ne buhianyi bua tshisamba tshia batatuʼabo.
8 Muana mukashi yonso udi ne buhianyi munkatshi mua Bena Isalele, neikale mukashi wa umue wa mu tshisamba tshia tatuʼandi, nunku muntu ne muntu wa Bena Isalele neikale ne buhianyi bua batatuʼabo.
9 Nunku buhianyi bumue kabuena bumuka ku tshisamba ne ku tshisamba, tshisamba ne tshisamba tshia Bena Isalele netshikale ne buhianyi buatshi.
10 Bana bakashi ba Selofehada bakenza muakambila Yehowa Mose,
11 bualu bua Mahala ne Tîza ne Hogela ne Mileka ne Noa, bana bakashi ba Selofehada, bakadi babakibua kudi bana balume bakadi bana babo ne tatuʼabo.
12 Bakadi babakibua kudi ba mu meku a bana balume ba Manashe, muana mulume wa Yosefe, buhianyi buabo buakashala mu tshisamba tshia meku a tatuʼabo.
13 Au mmei ne mikenji bakambila Yehowa Bena Isalele mukana mua Mose mu mpata ya Moaba hehi ne Yadene mitangilangane ne Yeleko.
